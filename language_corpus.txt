Fizikia ni nini kwa ufafanuzi kamili?
What is physics in full definition?
Fizikia ni tawi kuu la sayansi ya asili linalochunguza vitu, nishati, mwendo, nguvu, na sheria zinazotawala ulimwengu mzima. Jina "fizikia" limetokana na neno la Kigiriki "physis" linalomaanisha asili. Wanasayansi wa fizikia hujaribu kujibu maswali makubwa kama kwa nini nyota zinang'aa, kwa nini mvua hunyesha, kwa nini vitu huanguka chini, na jinsi umeme unavyofanya kazi. Fizikia inatumiwa kama msingi wa kuelewa sayansi nyingine zote kama kemia, baiolojia, jiolojia, na astronomia. Bila maarifa ya fizikia, maendeleo ya teknolojia ya leo kama simu, kompyuta, ndege, na satelaiti yasingewezekana. Kwa hiyo kila mwanafunzi anapaswa kujifunza fizikia ili aweze kuelewa ulimwengu unaomzunguka na kushiriki katika maendeleo ya taifa.
Physics is the main branch of natural science that investigates matter, energy, motion, forces, and the laws governing the entire universe. The word "physics" comes from the Greek word "physis" meaning nature. Physicists try to answer big questions like why stars shine, why rain falls, why objects fall down, and how electricity works. Physics is used as the foundation to understand all other sciences like chemistry, biology, geology, and astronomy. Without knowledge of physics, modern technology like phones, computers, airplanes, and satellites would not be possible. Therefore every student should learn physics to understand the world around them and participate in national development.

Name 10 importance of studying physics in daily life.

Umuhimu wa sita ni fizikia inatumika katika udaktari kutengeneza vifaa vya x-ray, ultrasound, na mashine za kupima damu. Umuhimu wa saba ni fizikia inatusaidia kubuni nishati mbadala kama nishati ya jua, upepo, na maji ili kuhifadhi mazingira. Umuhimu wa nane ni fizikia inaeleza usalama barabarani kama kwa nini magari yanahitaji breki na mikanda ya usalama. Umuhimu wa tisa ni fizikia inatumiwa katika michezo kueleza jinsi mpira unavyopiga bend na kwa nini wanariadha wanakimbia kwa namna fulani. Umuhimu wa kumi ni fizikia inatupa maarifa ya anga na nafasi hivyo kutuwezesha kutuma satelaiti na kuchunguza sayari nyingine.
The first importance is physics teaches us to think logically and solve problems scientifically. The second importance is physics is the foundation of many careers like car engineering, building construction, and machinery. The third importance is physics helps us make and use home appliances like fridge, television, iron, and washing machine. The fourth importance is physics explains how communication devices like radio, phone, and internet work. The fifth importance is physics helps us understand weather, rain, wind, and climate change. The sixth importance is physics is used in medicine to make x-ray, ultrasound, and blood testing machines. The seventh importance is physics helps us design renewable energy like solar, wind, and hydro power to protect environment. The eighth importance is physics explains road safety like why cars need brakes and seat belts. The ninth importance is physics is used in sports to explain how a ball curves and why athletes run in certain ways. The tenth importance is physics gives us knowledge of space and universe enabling us to send satellites and explore other planets.

Name 8 branches of physics and explain each with 2 examples.

Vitu ni nini na eleza sifa 5 za kila aina yake.
What is matter and explain 5 properties of each state.
Vitu ni kila kitu chenye uzito na kuchukua nafasi. Aina ya kwanza ni imara. Sifa 1: ina umbo maalum na halibadiliki. Sifa 2: ina kiasi maalum. Sifa 3: chembe zake ziko karibu sana na kupangwa vizuri. Sifa 4: haiwezi kubanwa kwa urahisi. Sifa 5: haiwezi kumwagika. Mfano ni jiwe, mbao, chuma. Aina ya pili ni kioevu. Sifa 1: haina umbo maalum bali huchukua umbo la chombo. Sifa 2: ina kiasi maalum. Sifa 3: chembe zake ziko karibu lakini zinaweza kusogea. Sifa 4: hutirika kwa urahisi. Sifa 5: uso wake huwa mlalo. Mfano ni maji, maziwa, mafuta. Aina ya tatu ni gesi. Sifa 1: haina umbo maalum wala kiasi maalum. Sifa 2: chembe zake ziko mbali sana na kusogea kwa uhuru. Sifa 3: inaweza kubanwa kwa urahisi. Sifa 4: hujaza chombo chote. Sifa 5: inaweza kusambaa haraka angani. Mfano ni hewa, oksijeni, mvuke.
Matter is everything that has mass and occupies space. The first type is solid. Property 1: has definite shape that does not change. Property 2: has definite volume. Property 3: its particles are very close and arranged orderly. Property 4: cannot be compressed easily. Property 5: cannot be poured. Example is stone, wood, metal. The second type is liquid. Property 1: has no definite shape but takes shape of container. Property 2: has definite volume. Property 3: its particles are close but can move. Property 4: flows easily. Property 5: its surface is always horizontal. Example is water, milk, oil. The third type is gas. Property 1: has no definite shape or volume. Property 2: its particles are far apart and move freely. Property 3: can be compressed easily. Property 4: fills the whole container. Property 5: spreads quickly in air. Example is air, oxygen, vapour.


Misa ni nini kwa ufafanuzi kamili na sifa zake 4?
What is mass in full definition and its 4 properties?
Misa ni kiasi cha vitu au chembe vilivyomo ndani ya kitu fulani. Misa ni kiasi cha scalar kwa sababu haina mwelekeo, ina kiasi tu. Sifa ya kwanza ni misa haibadilika popote ulipo, iwe duniani, mwezini, au angani. Sifa ya pili ni misa hupimwa kwa kutumia mizani ya aina mbalimbali kama mizani ya mizani mbili na mizani ya kidijitali. Sifa ya tatu ni kitengo cha SI cha misa ni kilogram ambacho kinaashiriwa na kg. Sifa ya nne ni misa ya kitu ni sawa na jumla ya misa ya chembe zote ndogo ndogo zinazounda kitu hicho. Kwa mfano mfuko wa sukari wa kilo 5 una misa ya kilo 5 iwe umewekwa darasani au umepelekwa mlima Kilimanjaro.
Mass is the amount of matter or particles contained in a particular object. Mass is a scalar quantity because it has no direction, it has magnitude only. The first property is mass does not change anywhere you are, whether on earth, moon, or in space. The second property is mass is measured using different types of balances like beam balance and digital balance. The third property is the SI unit of mass is kilogram which is denoted by kg. The fourth property is the mass of an object is equal to the sum of masses of all tiny particles that make up that object. For example a 5kg bag of sugar has mass of 5kg whether placed in class or taken to Mount Kilimanjaro.

Uzito ni nini na kwa nini hubadilika?
What is weight and why does it change?
Uzito ni nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia au sayari nyingine inayovuta kitu kuelekea katikati yake. Uzito ni kiasi cha vector kwa sababu una kiasi na mwelekeo kuelekea chini katikati ya dunia. Uzito hubadilika kwa sababu nguvu ya mvuto hubadilika kutoka sehemu moja hadi nyingine. Kwa mfano mtu mwenye misa ya kilogram 60 ana uzito wa Newton 600 duniani lakini mwezini uzito wake unakuwa Newton 100 tu kwa sababu mvuto wa mwezi ni mdogo mara 6. Uzito hupimwa kwa kutumia spring balance au weighing machine na kitengo chake cha SI ni Newton ambacho kinaashiriwa na N. Sheria ya Newton inasema uzito W = misa x nguvu ya mvuto g, hivyo W = mg.
Weight is the gravitational force of the earth or another planet pulling an object towards its center. Weight is a vector quantity because it has magnitude and direction towards the center of the earth. Weight changes because gravitational force changes from one place to another. For example a person with mass of 60kg has weight of 600N on earth but on the moon his weight becomes only 100N because moon gravity is 6 times smaller. Weight is measured using a spring balance or weighing machine and its SI unit is Newton denoted by N. Newton law says weight W = mass x gravitational force g, therefore W = mg.

Taja tofauti 6 kati ya misa na uzito kwa jedwali.
Name 6 differences between mass and weight in a table.
Tofauti ya kwanza ni misa ni kiasi cha scalar wakati uzito ni kiasi cha vector. Tofauti ya pili ni misa haibadilika popote wakati uzito hubadilika kulingana na nguvu ya mvuto. Tofauti ya tatu ni misa hupimwa kwa mizani wakati uzito hupimwa kwa spring balance. Tofauti ya nne ni kitengo cha misa ni kilogram wakati kitengo cha uzito ni Newton. Tofauti ya tano ni misa ya kitu haiwezi kuwa sifuri lakini uzito wa kitu unaweza kuwa sifuri angani. Tofauti ya sita ni misa ni kiasi cha vitu vilivyomo wakati uzito ni nguvu ya mvuto inayovuta kitu hicho.
The first difference is mass is scalar quantity while weight is vector quantity. The second difference is mass does not change anywhere while weight changes according to gravitational force. The third difference is mass is measured using a balance while weight is measured using a spring balance. The fourth difference is unit of mass is kilogram while unit of weight is Newton. The fifth difference is mass of an object cannot be zero but weight of an object can be zero in space. The sixth difference is mass is amount of matter while weight is gravitational force pulling that object.

Urefu ni nini na taja vifaa 6 vya kupima urefu?
What is length and name 6 instruments for measuring length?
Urefu ni umbali wa mstari kati ya pointi mbili au ukubwa wa kitu kutoka mwisho mmoja hadi mwisho mwingine. Urefu ni kiasi cha msingi katika fizikia na hupimwa kwa vitengo mbalimbali. Kifaa cha kwanza ni mita ya mita ambayo hutumika kupima urefu wa chumba na urefu wa mwanafunzi. Kifaa cha pili ni ruler au rula ya sentimita ambayo hutumika kupima vitu vidogo kama urefu wa kalamu na kitabu. Kifaa cha tatu ni tape measure ambayo ni utepe wa chuma au plastiki unaoweza kukunjwa na hutumika kupima urefu mrefu kama urefu wa shamba. Kifaa cha nne ni vernier calipers ambayo hupima kwa usahihi mkubwa hadi milimita 0.01 na hutumika kupima kipenyo cha waya. Kifaa cha tano ni micrometer screw gauge ambayo hupima kwa usahihi zaidi hadi milimita 0.001 na hutumika kupima unene wa karatasi. Kifaa cha sita ni odometer ambayo iko kwenye gari na hupima umbali wote ambao gari limekusafiri.
Length is the linear distance between two points or the size of an object from one end to another end. Length is a base quantity in physics and is measured in different units. The first instrument is meter rule which is used to measure length of a room and height of a student. The second instrument is centimeter ruler which is used to measure small objects like length of a pen and book. The third instrument is tape measure which is a metal or plastic tape that can be folded and used to measure long distances like length of a farm. The fourth instrument is vernier calipers which measures with high accuracy up to 0.01mm and is used to measure diameter of a wire. The fifth instrument is micrometer screw gauge which measures with even higher accuracy up to 0.001mm and is used to measure thickness of paper. The sixth instrument is odometer which is in a car and measures total distance the car has travelled.

Muda ni nini na ueleze vifaa 5 vya kupima muda?
What is time and explain 5 instruments for measuring time?
Muda ni kipimo cha tukio kuanza hadi kumalizika au kipimo cha matukio yanayofuata. Muda ni kiasi cha msingi ambacho hakitegemei viasi vingine. Kifaa cha kwanza ni saa ya ukutani au saa ya mkono ambayo hutumia sindano na namba kuonyesha saa, dakika, na sekunde. Kifaa cha pili ni stopwatch ambayo hutumika kupima muda mfupi kwa usahihi wa sekunde 0.01 hasa kwenye mashindano ya riadha. Kifaa cha tatu ni saa ya jua ambayo ilitumiwa zamani na hutumia kivuli cha fimbo kupima muda kulingana na nafasi ya jua. Kifaa cha nne ni saa ya mchanga ambayo hutumia mchanga unaomtirika kutoka chombo kimoja hadi kingine kupima muda wa dakika 3 au 5. Kifaa cha tano ni saa ya atomiki ambayo ni sahihi zaidi duniani na hutumia vibrations za atomi za cesium kupima muda na makosa yake ni sekunde 1 kwa miaka milioni 30.
Time is the measure of duration from when an event starts to when it ends or the measure of events that follow one another. Time is a base quantity that does not depend on other quantities. The first instrument is wall clock or wristwatch which uses hands and numbers to show hours, minutes, and seconds. The second instrument is stopwatch which is used to measure short time with accuracy of 0.01 seconds especially in athletics competitions. The third instrument is sundial which was used long ago and uses shadow of a stick to measure time according to position of the sun. The fourth instrument is sand clock which uses sand flowing from one container to another to measure 3 or 5 minutes. The fifth instrument is atomic clock which is the most accurate clock in the world and uses vibrations of cesium atoms to measure time with error of only 1 second in 30 million years.

Vitengo vya SI ni nini na taja viasi 7 vya msingi?
What are SI units and name 7 base quantities?
Vitengo vya SI ni mfumo wa kimataifa wa vitengo vilivyokubaliwa na nchi zote duniani ili kuepuka mkanganyiko katika sayansi na biashara. SI inatoka neno la Kifaransa "Systeme International d'Unites". Kiasi cha kwanza ni urefu ambacho kitengo chake ni mita na kinaashiriwa na m. Kiasi cha pili ni misa ambacho kitengo chake ni kilogram na kinaashiriwa na kg. Kiasi cha tatu ni muda ambacho kitengo chake ni sekunde na kinaashiriwa na s. Kiasi cha nne ni mkondo wa umeme ambacho kitengo chake ni ampere na kinaashiriwa na A. Kiasi cha tano ni halijoto ambacho kitengo chake ni Kelvin na kinaashiriwa na K. Kiasi cha sita ni kiasi cha vitu ambacho kitengo chake ni mole na kinaashiriwa na mol. Kiasi cha saba ni nguvu ya mwanga ambacho kitengo chake ni candela na kinaashiriwa na cd. Matumizi ya vitengo hivi hufanya wanasayansi kutoka Japan, Tanzania, na Marekani waelewane bila tatizo.
SI units are an international system of units accepted by all countries in the world to avoid confusion in science and business. SI comes from French words "Systeme International d'Unites". The first quantity is length whose unit is meter denoted by m. The second quantity is mass whose unit is kilogram denoted by kg. The third quantity is time whose unit is second denoted by s. The fourth quantity is electric current whose unit is ampere denoted by A. The fifth quantity is temperature whose unit is Kelvin denoted by K. The sixth quantity is amount of substance whose unit is mole denoted by mol. The seventh quantity is luminous intensity whose unit is candela denoted by cd. Use of these units makes scientists from Japan, Tanzania, and America understand each other without problems.

Eleza makosa 3 yanayotokea wakati wa kupima na jinsi ya kuyazuia.
Explain 3 types of errors that occur during measurement and how to prevent them.
Kosa la kwanza ni kosa la kibinadamu ambalo hutokea kwa sababu ya mwanafunzi kusoma vibaya kiwango kwenye mita au kutotazama kwa usawa. Hili linazuiliwa kwa kusoma kiwango kwa uangalifu na macho yako kuwa sawa na kiwango cha mita. Kosa la pili ni kosa la kifaa ambalo hutokea kwa sababu mita yenyewe imeharibika au haina sifuri sahihi. Hili linazuiliwa kwa kurekebisha kifaa kabla ya kupima au kutumia kifaa kingine. Kosa la tatu ni kosa la bahati ambalo hutokea kwa sababu ya mabadiliko ya hali ya hewa kama joto na upepo wakati wa kupima. Hili linazuiliwa kwa kurudia jaribio mara nyingi halafu kuchukua wastani wa matokeo yote.
The first error is human error which occurs because a student reads the scale wrongly on a meter or does not view at eye level. This is prevented by reading the scale carefully with your eyes level with the meter scale. The second error is instrument error which occurs because the meter itself is damaged or does not have correct zero. This is prevented by adjusting the instrument before measuring or using another instrument. The third error is random error which occurs due to changes in weather conditions like heat and wind during measurement. This is prevented by repeating the experiment many times then taking the average of all results.

Nishati ni nini kwa ufafanuzi kamili na taja umuhimu wake 5?
What is energy in full definition and name 5 of its importance?
Nishati ni uwezo wa kitu kufanya kazi, kusababisha mabadiliko, au kufanya kitu kisonge kutoka sehemu moja hadi nyingine. Bila nishati hakuna mwendo, hakuna sauti, hakuna mwanga, na hakuna maisha duniani. Umuhimu wa kwanza ni nishati ya chakula hutupa nguvu ya kufanya kazi zote za kila siku kama kusoma, kucheza, na kukimbia. Umuhimu wa pili ni nishati ya umeme huwasha taa nyumbani, inachaji simu, na kuendesha friji ili chakula kisioze. Umuhimu wa tatu ni nishati ya mafuta ya petroli na dizeli huendesha magari, mabasi, ndege, na mitambo ya mashambani. Umuhimu wa nne ni nishati ya jua hutupa mwanga na joto na pia inatumika kubadilishwa kuwa umeme kwa kutumia solar panels. Umuhimu wa tano ni nishati ya maji ikitirika kwa kasi hutumika kuzalisha umeme katika mabwawa ya umeme kama Mtera na Kidatu.
Energy is the ability of an object to do work, cause change, or make something move from one place to another. Without energy there is no motion, no sound, no light, and no life on earth. The first importance is food energy gives us power to do all daily activities like reading, playing, and running. The second importance is electrical energy lights bulbs at home, charges phones, and runs fridge to keep food fresh. The third importance is fuel energy like petrol and diesel runs cars, buses, airplanes, and farm machines. The fourth importance is solar energy gives us light and heat and is also converted to electricity using solar panels. The fifth importance is energy from fast flowing water is used to generate electricity at power dams like Mtera and Kidatu.

Taja na ueleze aina 6 kuu za nishati kwa mifano.
Name and explain 6 main types of energy with examples.
Aina ya kwanza ni nishati ya kinetic ambayo ni nishati ya vitu vinavyosonga. Kasi ikizidi nishati ya kinetic pia huzidi. Mfano ni gari linaloenda kwa kasi, mpira unaoviringika, na maji ya mto yakitirika. Aina ya pili ni nishati ya potential ambayo ni nishati iliyohifadhiwa kutokana na nafasi au umbo la kitu. Mfano ni jiwe lililoinuliwa juu ya meza, upinde uliovutwa tayari, na chemchemi iliyobanwa. Aina ya tatu ni nishati ya joto ambayo ni jumla ya nishati ya chembe zote ndogo ndogo ndani ya kitu. Mfano ni maji ya moto kwenye sufuria, jua linalowaka, na moto wa kuni. Aina ya nne ni nishati ya mwanga ambayo husafiri kama mawimbi na huturuhusu kuona. Mfano ni mwanga wa jua, mwanga wa taa, na mwanga wa umeme. Aina ya tano ni nishati ya sauti ambayo hutengenezwa na vibrations za vitu. Mfano ni sauti ya ngoma, sauti ya redio, na sauti ya mtu anapozungumza. Aina ya sita ni nishati ya umeme ambayo ni mwendo wa elektroni kwenye waya. Mfano ni umeme unaowasha televisheni, kompyuta, na mashine ya kuosha.
The first type is kinetic energy which is energy of moving objects. As speed increases kinetic energy also increases. Example is a fast moving car, rolling ball, and flowing river water. The second type is potential energy which is stored energy due to position or shape of an object. Example is a stone lifted on a table, a stretched bow ready to shoot, and a compressed spring. The third type is heat energy which is total energy of all tiny particles inside an object. Example is hot water in a pot, burning sun, and firewood. The fourth type is light energy which travels as waves and enables us to see. Example is sunlight, bulb light, and lightning. The fifth type is sound energy produced by vibrations of objects. Example is drum sound, radio sound, and human voice when speaking. The sixth type is electrical energy which is flow of electrons through a wire. Example is electricity that powers television, computer, and washing machine.

Eleza sheria ya uhifadhi wa nishati kwa mifano 3.
Explain the law of conservation of energy with 3 examples.
Sheria ya uhifadhi wa nishati inasema nishati haiwezi kutengenezwa wala kuharibiwa, lakini inaweza kubadilishwa kutoka aina moja kwenda nyingine. Jumla ya nishati kabla na baada ya mabadiliko hubaki sawa. Mfano wa kwanza ni pendulum ya saa. Nishati ya potential iko juu zaidi wakati pendulum iko juu, kisha inabadilika kuwa kinetic wakati ikishuka katikati. Mfano wa pili ni dynamo ya baisikeli. Unapopiga miguu unatoa nishati ya kinetic, dynamo inabadilisha kuwa nishati ya umeme, kisha taa inabadilisha umeme kuwa mwanga. Mfano wa tatu ni chakula unachokula. Nishati ya kemikali kwenye chakula inabadilika kuwa nishati ya kinetic unapokimbia na nishati ya joto mwilini mwako.
The law of conservation of energy states that energy cannot be created or destroyed, but can be changed from one form to another. Total energy before and after change remains the same. The first example is pendulum of a clock. Potential energy is highest when pendulum is up, then it changes to kinetic energy when it swings down to the middle. The second example is bicycle dynamo. When you pedal you produce kinetic energy, dynamo changes it to electrical energy, then bulb changes electricity to light energy. The third example is food you eat. Chemical energy in food changes to kinetic energy when you run and heat energy in your body.

Nguvu ni nini na taja athari zake 4 kwa vitu?
What is force and name 4 effects of force on objects?
Nguvu ni msukumo au kivutio kinachotumika kwenye kitu na kusababisha mabadiliko. Nguvu ni kiasi cha vector kwa sababu ina kiasi na mwelekeo. Athari ya kwanza ni nguvu inaweza kufanya kitu kisichosonga kianze kusonga. Mfano ni kusukuma gari lililosimama ili lianze kutembea. Athari ya pili ni nguvu inaweza kusimamisha kitu kinachosonga. Mfano ni kubonyeza breki ya baisikeli ili isimame. Athari ya tatu ni nguvu inaweza kubadilisha kasi ya kitu. Mfano ni kupiga miguu kwa nguvu zaidi baisikeli ikaharakisha. Athari ya nne ni nguvu inaweza kubadilisha umbo la kitu. Mfano ni kubanwa plastiki ili ipate umbo jipya na kunyoosha chemi ili iwe sawa.
Force is a push or pull applied on an object that causes change. Force is a vector quantity because it has magnitude and direction. The first effect is force can make a stationary object start moving. Example is pushing a stationary car to start moving. The second effect is force can stop a moving object. Example is pressing bicycle brakes to stop it. The third effect is force can change speed of an object. Example is pedaling harder makes bicycle move faster. The fourth effect is force can change shape of an object. Example is squeezing plastic to give it new shape and straightening a bent wire.

Taja na ueleze aina 6 za nguvu kwa mifano.
Name and explain 6 types of force with examples.
Aina ya kwanza ni nguvu ya misuli ambayo binadamu na wanyama hutumia kwa mikono au miguu. Mfano ni kubeba mfuko wa saruji na kuvuta gari la mikokoteni. Aina ya pili ni msuguano ambayo ni nguvu inayopinga mwendo kati ya nyuso mbili zinazogusana. Mfano ni breki za gari zikishika tairi na nyayo zako zikwamisha sakafu. Aina ya tatu ni nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia ambayo huvuta vitu vyote kuelekea katikati ya dunia. Mfano ni tufaha likianguka kutoka mti na mvua ikinyesha chini. Aina ya nne ni nguvu ya sumaku ambayo huvuta vitu vya chuma. Mfano ni sumaku ikivuta karatasi na dira ikielekeza kaskazini. Aina ya tano ni nguvu ya mkazo ambayo hufanyika kwenye kamba au mnyororo unaovutwa. Mfano ni kamba ya bendera ikishikwa na upepo na mnyororo ukibeba ndoo ya maji. Aina ya sita ni nguvu ya upanuzi ambayo husukuma kitu kuelekea juu kwenye kioevu au gesi. Mfano ni baluni ikipepea angani na meli ikiweza kuelea majini.
The first type is muscular force which humans and animals use with hands or legs. Example is carrying a bag of cement and pulling a handcart. The second type is friction which is a force that opposes motion between two surfaces in contact. Example is car brakes gripping tyre and your shoes gripping the floor. The third type is gravitational force which pulls all objects towards center of earth. Example is apple falling from tree and rain falling down. The fourth type is magnetic force which attracts iron objects. Example is magnet attracting pins and compass pointing north. The fifth type is tension force that occurs in a rope or chain being pulled. Example is flag rope being pulled by wind and chain carrying a bucket of water. The sixth type is upthrust force that pushes object upwards in liquid or gas. Example is balloon floating in air and ship floating on water.

Msuguano ni nini na ueleze faida 3 na hasara 3 zake.
What is friction and explain 3 advantages and 3 disadvantages of it.
Msuguano ni nguvu inayopinga mwendo kati ya nyuso mbili zinazogusana na kusogea. Msuguano hutokea kwa sababu nyuso sio laini kabisa, zina vilima vidogo vidogo. Faida ya kwanza ni msuguano hutuwezesha kutembea bila kuteleza. Kama hakungekuwa na msuguano tungechelezea kila hatua. Faida ya pili ni msuguano huwezesha breki za gari na baisikeli zisimamishe gari. Faida ya tatu ni msuguano huwezesha moto kuwashwa kwa kusugua vijiti viwili pamoja. Hasara ya kwanza ni msuguano hutumia nishati nyingi na kuibadilisha kuwa joto ambalo ni hasara. Hasara ya pili ni msuguano husababisha sehemu za mitambo kuchakaa haraka kama gia na minyororo. Hasara ya tatu ni msuguano hupunguza kasi ya vitu vinavyosonga kama ndege angani na meli majini.
Friction is a force that opposes motion between two surfaces in contact and moving. Friction occurs because surfaces are not completely smooth, they have tiny bumps. The first advantage is friction enables us to walk without slipping. Without friction we would slide with every step. The second advantage is friction enables car and bicycle brakes to stop vehicles. The third advantage is friction enables fire to be lit by rubbing two sticks together. The first disadvantage is friction wastes a lot of energy by changing it to heat which is useless. The second disadvantage is friction causes machine parts to wear out quickly like gears and chains. The third disadvantage is friction reduces speed of moving objects like airplane in air and ship in water.

Kazi ni nini katika fizikia na andika fomula yake?
What is work in physics and write its formula?
Katika fizikia kazi inafanyika tu wakati nguvu ikisukuma au kuvuta kitu na kitu kikasogea kwa mwelekeo wa nguvu hiyo. Kama nguvu imetumika lakini kitu hakisogei, basi hakuna kazi imefanyika. Kwa mfano kusukuma ukuta kwa nguvu zote bila kusogea si kazi. Kazi inategemea nguvu iliyotumika na umbali ambao kitu kimesogea. Fomula ya kazi ni Kazi W = Nguvu F x Umbali d. Kitengo cha kazi ni Joule ambacho ni sawa na Newton mita. Kwa hiyo mtu akisukuma sanduku kwa nguvu ya Newton 10 na lisoge kwa mita 5, kazi iliyofanyika ni Joule 50.
In physics work is only done when a force pushes or pulls an object and the object moves in the direction of that force. If force is applied but object does not move, then no work is done. For example pushing a wall with all your strength without it moving is not work. Work depends on force applied and distance the object moves. The formula for work is Work W = Force F x Distance d. The unit of work is Joule which is equal to Newton meter. So if a person pushes a box with force of 10N and it moves 5m, work done is 50J.


What is heat and explain 5 differences between heat and temperature?
Joto ni aina ya nishati inayopita kutoka kitu chenye halijoto juu kwenda kitu chenye halijoto chini kwa sababu ya tofauti ya halijoto. Joto ni kiasi cha scalar na hupimwa kwa Joule. Tofauti ya kwanza ni joto ni nishati wakati halijoto ni kipimo cha jinsi kitu kilivyo moto au baridi. Tofauti ya pili ni joto hupimwa kwa Joule wakati halijoto hupimwa kwa degree Celsius au Kelvin. Tofauti ya tatu ni joto linategemea kiasi cha vitu wakati halijoto halitegemei kiasi. Kikombe kimoja na ndoo ya maji ya moto yana halijoto sawa lakini ndoo ina joto zaidi. Tofauti ya nne ni joto linaweza kupita kutoka kitu kimoja kwenda kingine wakati halijoto haiwezi kupita. Tofauti ya tano ni chombo cha kupima joto ni calorimeter wakati chombo cha kupima halijoto ni thermometer.
Heat is a form of energy that flows from an object with higher temperature to an object with lower temperature due to temperature difference. Heat is a scalar quantity and is measured in Joules. The first difference is heat is energy while temperature is measure of how hot or cold an object is. The second difference is heat is measured in Joules while temperature is measured in degree Celsius or Kelvin. The third difference is heat depends on amount of matter while temperature does not depend on amount. One cup and a bucket of hot water have same temperature but bucket has more heat. The fourth difference is heat can flow from one object to another while temperature cannot flow. The fifth difference is instrument for measuring heat is calorimeter while instrument for measuring temperature is thermometer.

Halijoto ni nini na taja mizani 3 za kupima halijoto?
What is temperature and name 3 scales for measuring temperature?
Halijoto ni kipimo cha kiwango cha joto au baridi cha kitu. Halijoto inaeleza kasi ya chembe ndogo ndogo ndani ya kitu. Chembe zikisonga kwa kasi halijoto huwa juu, zikisonga polepole halijoto huwa chini. Mizani ya kwanza ni Celsius ambayo ina nukta ya kuyeyuka ya maji nyuzi 0 na nukta ya kuchemka nyuzi 100. Inaashiriwa na °C. Mizani ya pili ni Fahrenheit ambayo hutumiwa Marekani na ina nukta ya kuyeyuka nyuzi 32 na nukta ya kuchemka nyuzi 212. Inaashiriwa na °F. Mizani ya tatu ni Kelvin ambayo ni mizani ya kisayansi na huanza kutoka sifuri kabisa ambayo ni -273°C. Inaashiriwa na K. Hakuna alama ya digrii kwenye Kelvin.
Temperature is the measure of degree of hotness or coldness of an object. Temperature tells the speed of tiny particles inside an object. When particles move fast temperature is high, when they move slowly temperature is low. The first scale is Celsius which has melting point of water at 0° and boiling point at 100°. It is denoted by °C. The second scale is Fahrenheit which is used in America and has melting point at 32° and boiling point at 212°. It is denoted by °F. The third scale is Kelvin which is scientific scale and starts from absolute zero which is -273°C. It is denoted by K. There is no degree sign on Kelvin.

Thermometer ni nini na eleza aina zake 4?
What is a thermometer and explain its 4 types?
Thermometer ni kifaa kinachotumika kupima halijoto ya kitu au ya mazingira. Thermometer nyingi hutumia kioevu kama zebaki au pombe ambayo hupanuka au kusinyaa kulingana na halijoto. Aina ya kwanza ni thermometer ya kliniki ambayo hutumika kupima halijoto ya mwili wa binadamu. Ina safu nyembamba na inaonyesha kati ya 35°C hadi 42°C. Aina ya pili ni thermometer ya maabara ambayo hupima halijoto ya juu hadi 110°C na hutumika kwenye majaribio ya kemikali. Aina ya tatu ni thermometer ya digiti ambayo inaonyesha namba kwenye screen na hupima haraka ndani ya sekunde 10. Aina ya nne ni thermometer ya infrared ambayo hupima halijoto bila kugusa kitu, inatumika kupima halijoto ya watu uwanjani.
A thermometer is an instrument used to measure temperature of an object or environment. Most thermometers use liquid like mercury or alcohol which expands or contracts according to temperature. The first type is clinical thermometer used to measure body temperature of humans. It has a narrow range and shows between 35°C to 42°C. The second type is laboratory thermometer which measures high temperature up to 110°C and is used in chemical experiments. The third type is digital thermometer which shows numbers on a screen and measures quickly within 10 seconds. The fourth type is infrared thermometer which measures temperature without touching object, used to measure temperature of people at stadiums.

Eleza njia 3 za upitishaji wa joto kwa mifano.
Explain 3 methods of heat transfer with examples.
Njia ya kwanza ni upitishaji au conduction ambayo joto hupita kutoka chembe moja kwenda nyingine bila chembe hizo kusogea. Hii hutokea zaidi kwenye vitu imara kama chuma. Mfano ni kijiko cha chuma kikitiwa kwenye chai ya moto, upande mwingine unapata moto pia. Njia ya pili ni usafirishaji au convection ambayo joto hupita kwa chembe za kioevu au gesi kusogea kutoka sehemu ya joto kwenda baridi. Mfano ni maji yakichemka sufuriani, maji ya chini yanapata joto, yanapanda juu na maji ya juu yanashuka chini. Njia ya tatu ni mionzi au radiation ambayo joto hupita kama mawimbi ya sumaku-umeme bila kuhitaji kitu cha kupitishia. Mfano ni jua likitupatia joto licha ya anga kuwa utupu, na moto wa jiko ukikupa joto hata kama haugusi.
The first method is conduction where heat passes from one particle to another without the particles moving. This happens mostly in solids like metal. Example is a metal spoon put in hot tea, the other end also becomes hot. The second method is convection where heat passes through movement of liquid or gas particles from hot place to cold place. Example is water boiling in a pot, water at bottom gets hot, rises up and cold water sinks down. The third method is radiation where heat passes as electromagnetic waves without needing a medium. Example is sun giving us heat even though space is vacuum, and fire from cooker giving you heat even without touching.

Upanuzi ni nini na taja matumizi 3 na madhara 3 yake?
What is expansion and name 3 uses and 3 disadvantages of it?
Upanuzi ni kuongezeka kwa ukubwa wa kitu kama urefu, eneo, au kiasi wakati halijoto yake ikipanda. Vitu vyote imara, kioevu, na gesi hupanuka wakati vinapokanzwa. Matumizi ya kwanza ni bimetallic strip kwenye thermostat ya pasi na friji inayotumia upanuzi tofauti wa chuma mbili kuzima na kuwasha umeme. Matumizi ya pili ni rivets za chuma zinazowekwa moto halafu zikipoa zinakaza mabati ya ndege na madaraja. Matumizi ya tatu ni glasi ya maabara inayofunguka kwa urahisi ukipasha kifuko chake kwa maji ya moto. Madhara ya kwanza ni reli za treni hupinda wakati wa joto kali kwa sababu hazikuachiwa nafasi ya kupanuka. Madhara ya pili ni nyufa kwenye madaraja na barabara za zege wakati wa joto kali. Madhara ya tatu ni chupa ya glasi inaweza kupasuka ukimwagia maji ya moto ghafla.
Expansion is increase in size of an object like length, area, or volume when its temperature rises. All solids, liquids, and gases expand when heated. The first use is bimetallic strip in thermostat of iron and fridge which uses different expansion of two metals to switch electricity on and off. The second use is metal rivets that are heated then when they cool they tighten sheets of airplanes and bridges. The third use is laboratory glass that opens easily when you pour hot water on its lid. The first disadvantage is railway lines bend during hot weather because no expansion gap was left. The second disadvantage is cracks on concrete bridges and roads during hot weather. The third disadvantage is glass bottle can crack when you pour hot water on it suddenly.

Mwanga ni nini na taja sifa 5 za mwanga?
What is light and name 5 properties of light?
Mwanga ni aina ya nishati ya sumaku-umeme inayoturuhusu kuona vitu vilivyopo karibu na sisi. Mwanga husafiri kwa kasi kubwa zaidi ulimwenguni. Sifa ya kwanza ni mwanga husafiri kwa mstari ulionyooka. Ndio maana tunaona kivuli chenye umbo sawa na kitu. Sifa ya pili ni mwanga unaweza kuakisiwa wakati unapogonga uso laini kama kioo. Sifa ya tatu ni mwanga unaweza kuvunjika wakati unapopita kutoka hewa kwenda majini. Ndio maana kijiko ndani ya maji kinaonekana kimepigika. Sifa ya nne ni mwanga unaweza kutawanywa na chembe za vumbi angani. Ndio maana anga linaonekana bluu mchana. Sifa ya tano ni mwanga unaweza kuunganishwa na lenzi kama lenzi ya kukuza maandishi na darubini.
Light is a form of electromagnetic energy that enables us to see objects around us. Light travels at the greatest speed in the universe. The first property is light travels in a straight line. That is why we see shadows with same shape as object. The second property is light can be reflected when it hits a smooth surface like mirror. The third property is light can be refracted when it passes from air to water. That is why a spoon in water appears bent. The fourth property is light can be scattered by dust particles in air. That is why sky appears blue during day. The fifth property is light can be focused by lenses like magnifying glass and telescope.

Kivuli kinatengenezwa vipi na taja aina zake 2?
How is a shadow formed and name its 2 types?
Kivuli hutengenezwa wakati mwanga unapozuiwa na kitu kisichopitisha mwanga kinachoitwa kitu opaque. Sehemu iliyozuiwa haijapata mwanga hivyo inaonekana giza. Kwa kivuli kutengenezwa lazima kuwe na vitu 3: chanzo cha mwanga, kitu opaque, na uso wa kupokea kivuli. Aina ya kwanza ni kivuli kamili au umbra ambayo ni sehemu ya giza kabisa katikati ya kivuli ambapo mwanga wote umezuiwa. Aina ya pili ni kivuli sehemu au penumbra ambayo ni sehemu yenye giza hafifu kuzunguka umbra ambapo sehemu ya mwanga imezuiwa. Wakati wa kupatwa kwa jua, mwezi hutengeneza umbra na penumbra duniani.
A shadow is formed when light is blocked by an object that does not allow light to pass called opaque object. The blocked part receives no light so it appears dark. For a shadow to be formed there must be 3 things: source of light, opaque object, and surface to receive shadow. The first type is umbra or total shadow which is the completely dark part in center of shadow where all light is blocked. The second type is penumbra or partial shadow which is faint dark part surrounding umbra where part of light is blocked. During solar eclipse, moon forms umbra and penumbra on earth.

Sauti inatengenezwa vipi na eleza sifa 4 za sauti?
How is sound produced and explain 4 properties of sound?
Sauti inatengenezwa wakati kitu kinatetereka au vibrations haraka sana. Vibrations hizi husukuma chembe za hewa na kutengeneza mawimbi ya kubana na kupanuka ambayo masikio yetu husikia kama sauti. Sifa ya kwanza ni sauti inahitaji kitu cha kupitishia kama hewa, maji, au chuma. Haiwezi kusafiri kwenye utupu. Sifa ya pili ni sauti husafiri kwa kasi tofauti kwenye vitu tofauti. Kwa hewa ni mita 340 kwa sekunde, kwa maji ni mita 1500 kwa sekunde, kwa chuma ni mita 5000 kwa sekunde. Sifa ya tatu ni sauti inaweza kuakisiwa na kutoa echo. Ndio maana unasikia sauti yako milimani. Sifa ya nne ni sauti ina sifa mbili kuu: pitch au unene mwembamba na loudness au nguvu. Pitch inategemea frequency wakati loudness inategemea amplitude.
Sound is produced when an object vibrates or moves very fast back and forth. These vibrations push air particles and create compression and rarefaction waves which our ears hear as sound. The first property is sound needs a medium like air, water, or metal to travel. It cannot travel in vacuum. The second property is sound travels at different speeds in different materials. In air it is 340m per second, in water 1500m per second, in steel 5000m per second. The third property is sound can be reflected and produce echo. That is why you hear your voice in mountains. The fourth property is sound has two main qualities: pitch or sharpness and loudness or strength. Pitch depends on frequency while loudness depends on amplitude.

Sumaku ni nini na ueleze njia 3 za kutengeneza sumaku?
What is a magnet and explain 3 methods of making magnets?
Sumaku ni chuma maalum kama chuma cha pua au chuma cha aloi ambacho kina uwezo wa kuvuta vitu vingine vya chuma. Nguvu ya sumaku iko zaidi kwenye ncha zake kuliko katikati. Njia ya kwanza ni msuguano ambapo chuma cha pua kinasuguliwa kwa ncha moja ya sumaku mara nyingi kwa mwelekeo mmoja. Njia ya pili ni kupiga nyundo ambapo chuma cha pua kinapigwa nyundo wakati kimeelekezwa kaskazini kusini na dunia. Nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia hupanga chembe za chuma. Njia ya tatu ni umeme ambapo waya wenye mkondo wa umeme unazungushwa kwenye chuma cha pua. Mkondo wa umeme hutengeneza uwanja wa sumaku na kufanya chuma kuwa sumaku ya muda. Hii ndio kanuni ya sumaku-umeme.
A magnet is a special metal like steel or alloy that has ability to attract other iron materials. Magnet force is stronger at its poles than in the middle. The first method is stroking where steel bar is rubbed with one pole of magnet many times in one direction. The second method is hammering where steel bar is hammered while aligned north-south with earth. Earth magnetic force aligns particles of steel. The third method is electricity where wire carrying electric current is coiled around steel bar. Electric current creates magnetic field and makes steel become temporary magnet. This is the principle of electromagnetism.

Taja kanuni 8 za usalama katika maabara ya fizikia?
Name 8 safety rules in physics laboratory?
Kanuni ya kwanza ni vaa koti la maabara, miwani, na glavu wakati wote wa majaribio ili kujilinda dhidi ya kemikali na kioo kinachovunjika. Kanuni ya pili ni usikimbie au kusukuma wanafunzi wenzako ndani ya maabara kwa sababu unaweza kugonga meza na vyombo kuvunjika. Kanuni ya tatu ni soma na uelewe maelekezo yote ya mwalimu kabla ya kuanza jaribio ili kuepuka ajali. Kanuni ya nne ni usionje, usinuse moja kwa moja, wala usiguse kemikali kwa mikono bila ruhusa. Kanuni ya tano ni zima vifaa vyote vya umeme na gesi baada ya kutumia ili kuepuka moto na mshtuko wa umeme. Kanuni ya sita ni ripoti ajali yoyote ndogo ndogo kwa mwalimu mara moja ili apate matibabu ya haraka. Kanuni ya saba ni weka nywele zako ndefu ndani ya kofia na usivae nguo zisizo huru ili zisishike moto. Kanuni ya nane ni safisha meza yako na mikono yako kwa sabuni baada ya kumaliza majaribio yote.
The first rule is wear lab coat, goggles, and gloves all the time during experiments to protect yourself from chemicals and broken glass. The second rule is do not run or push fellow students inside laboratory because you may hit tables and apparatus may break. The third rule is read and understand all teacher instructions before starting experiment to avoid accidents. The fourth rule is do not taste, do not smell directly, or touch chemicals with hands without permission. The fifth rule is switch off all electrical and gas equipment after use to avoid fire and electric shock. The sixth rule is report any small accident to teacher immediately for quick treatment. The seventh rule is tie your long hair inside cap and do not wear loose clothes so they do not catch fire. The eighth rule is clean your table and wash your hands with soap after finishing all experiments.

Kwanini maabara inapaswa kuwa na vifaa vya kuzima moto na sanduku la huduma ya kwanza?
Why should laboratory have fire extinguishers and first aid box?
Maabara ina vifaa vingi hatari kama kemikali zinazowaka, gesi, umeme, na vyombo vya kioo vinavyoweza kusababisha moto au majeraha ghafla. Kifaa cha kuzima moto ni muhimu kwa sababu moto ukianza unaweza kuenea haraka na kuchoma maabara nzima na wanafunzi. Aina tofauti za moto zinahitaji vizima moto tofauti kama moto wa umeme na moto wa mafuta. Sanduku la huduma ya kwanza ni muhimu kwa sababu ajali ndogo ndogo kama kukatwa na kioo, kuchomwa na moto, au kumeza kemikali zinaweza kutokea wakati wowote. Vifaa ndani ya sanduku kama pamba, dawa ya kuua vijidudu, na bandage husaidia kumpa mwanafunzi msaada wa kwanza kabla ya kupelekwa hospitali. Kuwa na vifaa hivi ni utaratibu wa lazima katika shule zote.
Laboratory contains many dangerous things like flammable chemicals, gas, electricity, and glass apparatus that can cause fire or injuries suddenly. Fire extinguisher is important because fire can spread quickly and burn whole laboratory and students. Different types of fire need different extinguishers like electrical fire and oil fire. First aid box is important because small accidents like glass cuts, burns, or swallowing chemicals can happen anytime. Items inside box like cotton, antiseptic, and bandage help give student first aid before being taken to hospital. Having these equipment is a compulsory requirement in all schools.

What is measurement and why is it important in physics?
Upimaji ni mchakato wa kulinganisha kiasi fulani na kiasi kingine cha aina moja ambacho kimekubaliwa kama kiwango. Kwa mfano tunapopima urefu wa meza kwa mita, tunalinganisha urefu wa meza na urefu wa mita 1. Upimaji ni muhimu katika fizikia kwa sababu wanasayansi wanahitaji namba sahihi ili kuthibitisha sheria na nadharia. Bila upimaji sahihi hatuwezi kujua kama majaribio yetu yamefanikiwa au la. Upimaji hutumika pia katika maisha ya kila siku kama mpishi apimavyo unga, fundi seremala apimavyo mbao, na daktari apimavyo halijoto ya mgonjwa. Kwa hiyo upimaji ni uti wa mgongo wa sayansi na teknolojia yote.
Measurement is the process of comparing a certain quantity with another quantity of same kind which has been accepted as a standard. For example when we measure length of a table with a meter, we compare length of table with length of 1 meter. Measurement is important in physics because scientists need accurate numbers to prove laws and theories. Without correct measurement we cannot know whether our experiments succeeded or not. Measurement is also used in daily life like a cook measuring flour, a carpenter measuring wood, and a doctor measuring patient temperature. Therefore measurement is the backbone of all science and technology.

Taja viasi vya msingi 7 vya SI na vitengo vyake?
Name 7 base quantities of SI and their units?
Kiasi cha kwanza ni urefu ambacho hupima umbali kati ya pointi mbili. Kitengo chake cha SI ni mita na kinaashiriwa na m. Kiasi cha pili ni misa ambacho hupima kiasi cha vitu vilivyomo kwenye kitu. Kitengo chake ni kilogram na kinaashiriwa na kg. Kiasi cha tatu ni muda ambacho hupima kipindi kati ya matukio mawili. Kitengo chake ni sekunde na kinaashiriwa na s. Kiasi cha nne ni mkondo wa umeme ambacho hupima kasi ya elektroni kwenye waya. Kitengo chake ni ampere na kinaashiriwa na A. Kiasi cha tano ni halijoto ambacho hupima jinsi kitu kilivyo moto. Kitengo chake ni Kelvin na kinaashiriwa na K. Kiasi cha sita ni kiasi cha vitu ambacho hupima idadi ya chembe ndogo ndogo. Kitengo chake ni mole na kinaashiriwa na mol. Kiasi cha saba ni nguvu ya mwanga ambacho hupima mwangaza wa chanzo cha mwanga. Kitengo chake ni candela na kinaashiriwa na cd.
The first quantity is length which measures distance between two points. Its SI unit is meter denoted by m. The second quantity is mass which measures amount of matter in an object. Its unit is kilogram denoted by kg. The third quantity is time which measures duration between two events. Its unit is second denoted by s. The fourth quantity is electric current which measures rate of electron flow in a wire. Its unit is ampere denoted by A. The fifth quantity is temperature which measures how hot an object is. Its unit is Kelvin denoted by K. The sixth quantity is amount of substance which measures number of tiny particles. Its unit is mole denoted by mol. The seventh quantity is luminous intensity which measures brightness of light source. Its unit is candela denoted by cd.

Viasi vya derivative ni nini na taja mifano 5?
What are derived quantities and name 5 examples?
Viasi vya derivative ni viasi vinavyotokana na kuunganisha viasi vya msingi viwili au zaidi kwa kuzidisha au kugawanya. Viasi hivi havina vitengo vya msingi bali vitengo vinavyotokana na vitengo vya msingi. Mfano wa kwanza ni eneo ambalo linatokana na urefu x upana. Kitengo chake ni mita za mraba m². Mfano wa pili ni kiasi ambalo linatokana na urefu x upana x urefu. Kitengo chake ni mita za ujazo m³. Mfano wa tatu ni kasi ambayo ni umbali kugawanywa na muda. Kitengo chake ni mita kwa sekunde m/s. Mfano wa nne ni wiani ambayo ni misa kugawanywa na kiasi. Kitengo chake ni kilogram kwa mita za ujazo kg/m³. Mfano wa tano ni nguvu ambayo ni misa x kasi. Kitengo chake ni Newton N ambacho ni sawa na kg m/s².
Derived quantities are quantities that come from combining two or more base quantities by multiplying or dividing. These quantities do not have base units but units derived from base units. The first example is area which comes from length x width. Its unit is square meter m². The second example is volume which comes from length x width x height. Its unit is cubic meter m³. The third example is speed which is distance divided by time. Its unit is meter per second m/s. The fourth example is density which is mass divided by volume. Its unit is kilogram per cubic meter kg/m³. The fifth example is force which is mass x acceleration. Its unit is Newton N which is equal to kg m/s².

Kuna aina ngapi za makosa katika upimaji na eleza kila moja?
How many types of errors are there in measurement and explain each?
Kuna aina 3 kuu za makosa katika upimaji. Aina ya kwanza ni kosa la kibinadamu au human error ambalo husababishwa na mwanafunzi. Mfano ni kusoma kiwango cha mita akiwa pembeni badala ya moja kwa moja, au kusahau kurekebisha sifuri kabla ya kupima. Hili linazuiliwa kwa kuwa makini na kufuata utaratibu. Aina ya pili ni kosa la kifaa au instrument error ambalo husababishwa na kifaa chenyewe kuwa cha zamani au kisichosahihishwa vizuri. Mfano ni mita ambayo sifuri yake haipo sehemu sahihi. Hili linazuiliwa kwa kutumia vifaa bora na kuvi-calibrate kabla ya matumizi. Aina ya tatu ni kosa la bahati au random error ambalo husababishwa na mabadiliko ya mazingira kama joto, upepo, au unyevu. Hili haliwezi kuzuilika kabisa lakini linaweza kupunguzwa kwa kurudia jaribio mara nyingi na kuchukua wastani.
There are 3 main types of errors in measurement. The first type is human error caused by student. Example is reading meter scale from side instead of straight, or forgetting to adjust zero before measuring. This is prevented by being careful and following procedure. The second type is instrument error caused by old instrument or instrument not well calibrated. Example is a meter rule whose zero mark is not at correct position. This is prevented by using good instruments and calibrating them before use. The third type is random error caused by changes in environment like heat, wind, or humidity. This cannot be completely prevented but can be reduced by repeating experiment many times and taking average.

Usahihi na usahihivu ni nini na tofauti yake ni ipi?
What is accuracy and precision and what is their difference?
Usahihi accuracy ni jinsi matokeo ya kipimo yalivyo karibu na thamani halisi ya kitu. Kipimo kiko sahihi kama kimetoka karibu na jibu la kweli. Usahihivu precision ni jinsi vipimo vinavyorudiwa vingi vilivyo karibu na kila kimoja. Kipimo kiko sahihihi kama ukipima mara 5 unapata namba karibu sawa. Tofauti kuu ni kipimo kinaweza kuwa sahihihi lakini kiswe sahihi. Mfano ni mwanafunzi akipima urefu wa penseli mara 3 akapata 15.1cm, 15.2cm, 15.1cm. Hapa vipimo ni sahihihi kwa sababu viko karibu lakini kama urefu halisi ni 14.5cm basi vipimo si sahihi. Kwa hiyo mwanasayansi mzuri anahitaji vipimo viwe sahihi na sahihihi.
Accuracy is how close measurement results are to the true value of an object. A measurement is accurate if it is close to the real answer. Precision is how close repeated measurements are to each other. A measurement is precise if you measure 5 times and get almost same number. The main difference is a measurement can be precise but not accurate. Example is a student measuring length of pencil 3 times and getting 15.1cm, 15.2cm, 15.1cm. Here measurements are precise because they are close but if true length is 14.5cm then measurements are not accurate. Therefore a good scientist needs measurements to be accurate and precise.


Vifaa vya kupima urefu ni vipi na utumie wake ni upi?
What are instruments for measuring length and their uses?
Kifaa cha kwanza ni mita ya mita meter rule ambayo ni mstari wa mita 1 uliopigwa alama za sentimita na milimita. Hutumika kupima vitu vya wastani kama urefu wa meza, kitabu, na urefu wa mwanafunzi. Usahihi wake ni hadi milimita 1. Kifaa cha pili ni rula ya sentimita ambayo ni fupi zaidi sentimita 30. Hutumika kupima vitu vidogo kama urefu wa kalamu, kipenyo cha sarafu, na upana wa karatasi. Kifaa cha tatu ni tape measure ambayo ni utepe mrefu wa chuma au plastiki unaoweza kukunjwa. Hutumika kupima umbali mrefu kama urefu wa shamba, urefu wa chumba, na urefu wa mtu. Kifaa cha nne ni vernier calipers ambayo hupima kwa usahihi mkubwa hadi milimita 0.02. Hutumika kupima kipenyo cha ndani, kipenyo cha nje, na kina cha vitu vidogo. Kifaa cha tano ni micrometer screw gauge ambayo ni sahihi zaidi hadi milimita 0.01. Hutumika kupima unene wa waya, karatasi, na sahani nyembamba.
Instruments for measuring length and their uses are as follows. The first instrument is meter rule which is 1 meter long line marked in centimeters and millimeters. It is used to measure medium objects like length of table, book, and height of student. Its accuracy is up to 1 millimeter. The second instrument is centimeter ruler which is shorter 30cm long. It is used to measure small objects like length of pen, diameter of coin, and width of paper. The third instrument is tape measure which is long metal or plastic tape that can be folded. It is used to measure long distances like length of farm, length of room, and height of person. The fourth instrument is vernier calipers which measures with high accuracy up to 0.02mm. It is used to measure internal diameter, external diameter, and depth of small objects. The fifth instrument is micrometer screw gauge which is even more accurate up to 0.01mm. It is used to measure thickness of wire, paper, and thin plates.

Vernier calipers linafanyaje kazi na sehemu zake kuu ni zipi?
How does vernier calipers work and what are its main parts?
Vernier calipers lina sehemu kuu 5. Sehemu ya kwanza ni mstari mkuu main scale uliopigwa alama za sentimita na milimita. Sehemu ya pili ni mstari wa vernier vernier scale ambao umegawanywa sehemu 10 au 20. Sehemu ya tatu ni pua za nje outside jaws zinazotumika kupima kipenyo cha nje na urefu wa kitu. Sehemu ya nne ni pua za ndani inside jaws zinazotumika kupima kipenyo cha ndani ya bomba au pete. Sehemu ya tano ni fimbo ya kina depth bar inayopima kina cha chombo. Linafanya kazi kwa kusoma alama ya sifuri kwenye vernier scale dhidi ya main scale kupata sentimita na milimita, kisha unatafuta mstari wowote kwenye vernier unaolingana kabisa na mstari wa main scale. Namba ya mstari huo unazidisha na 0.02mm ili kupata sehemu ya desimali. Kwa mfano kama main scale inasoma 3.4cm na mstari wa 8 unalingana, basi urefu ni 3.4cm + 8 x 0.02mm = 3.416cm.
Vernier calipers has 5 main parts. The first part is main scale marked in centimeters and millimeters. The second part is vernier scale divided into 10 or 20 parts. The third part is outside jaws used to measure external diameter and length of object. The fourth part is inside jaws used to measure internal diameter of tube or ring. The fifth part is depth bar that measures depth of a container. It works by reading zero mark on vernier scale against main scale to get centimeters and millimeters, then you find any line on vernier that matches exactly with line on main scale. That line number you multiply by 0.02mm to get decimal part. For example if main scale reads 3.4cm and line 8 matches, then length is 3.4cm + 8 x 0.02mm = 3.416cm.

Micrometer screw gauge hupima nini na kwa nini ni sahihi zaidi?
What does micrometer screw gauge measure and why is it more accurate?
Micrometer screw gauge hutumika kupima vitu nyembamba sana ambavyo mita ya mita haiwezi kupima kwa usahihi. Mifano ni unene wa karatasi, kipenyo cha waya, unene wa sahani ya chuma, na kipenyo cha risasi. Ni sahihi zaidi kwa sababu ina screw yenye thread nyembamba sana. Kila mzunguko kamili wa ngoma thimble husogeza spindle milimita 0.5 tu. Ngoma imegawanywa sehemu 50 hivyo kila sehemu ni 0.5mm ÷ 50 = 0.01mm. Kwa hiyo micrometer inaweza kupima hadi sehemu ya mia ya milimita. Sehemu zake kuu ni fremu, anvil, spindle, sleeve, thimble, na ratchet. Unaposoma, unaangalia alama kwenye sleeve kwa milimita, kisha unasoma alama kwenye thimble kwa sehemu za 0.01mm na kuziunganisha.
Micrometer screw gauge is used to measure very thin objects that meter rule cannot measure accurately. Examples are thickness of paper, diameter of wire, thickness of metal sheet, and diameter of bullet. It is more accurate because it has a screw with very fine thread. One complete turn of thimble moves spindle only 0.5mm. The thimble is divided into 50 parts so each part is 0.5mm ÷ 50 = 0.01mm. Therefore micrometer can measure up to hundredth of a millimeter. Its main parts are frame, anvil, spindle, sleeve, thimble, and ratchet. When reading, you look at mark on sleeve for millimeters, then read mark on thimble for 0.01mm parts and add them together.

Kosa la parallax ni nini na jinsi ya kulizuia?
What is parallax error and how to avoid it?
Kosa la parallax ni kosa linalotokea wakati mwanafunzi anasoma kiwango cha mita akiwa pembeni badala ya moja kwa moja. Kwa sababu kiwango na mshale wa mita haviko kwenye ndege moja, mshale unaonekana kusogea kulia au kushoto kulingana na pembe ya macho yako. Matokeo yake unapata kipimo kisicho sahihi. Hili ni kosa la kawaida sana wakati wa kupima kwa mita ya mita, rula, na mizani ya sindano. Ili kulizuia lazima macho yako yawe sawa kabisa na mshale wa mita na kiwango. Wanasayansi huweka kioo nyuma ya mizani ili usione picha yako kama unatoka pembeni. Kanuni ni: "Soma kwa macho yako moja kwa moja na kiwango".
Parallax error is error that occurs when a student reads scale of a meter while standing at side instead of straight. Because scale and pointer of meter are not on same plane, pointer appears to move right or left depending on angle of your eyes. Result is you get incorrect measurement. This is very common error when measuring with meter rule, ruler, and needle balance. To avoid it your eyes must be exactly level with pointer and scale. Scientists put mirror behind scale so you cannot see your image if you are viewing from side. The rule is: "Read with your eyes straight and level with scale".

Zero error ni nini na aina zake ni zipi?
What is zero error and what are its types?
Zero error ni kosa linalotokea wakati kifaa cha kupima hakina sifuri yake sehemu sahihi wakati hakitumiki. Hii inafanya vipimo vyote viwe vikubwa zaidi au vidogo zaidi kwa kiasi fulani. Kuna aina 2 za zero error. Aina ya kwanza ni zero error chanya positive wakati mshale wa sifuri uko kulia kwa alama ya sifuri. Hii inafanya kipimo kiwe kikubwa zaidi kuliko thamani halisi. Mfano kama zero error ni +0.02cm, kipimo chako cha 5.00cm ni kubwa zaidi, thamani halisi ni 5.00cm - 0.02cm = 4.98cm. Aina ya pili ni zero error hasi negative wakati mshale wa sifuri uko kushoto kwa alama ya sifuri. Hii inafanya kipimo kiwe kidogo zaidi. Mfano kama zero error ni -0.03cm, kipimo chako cha 5.00cm ni kidogo, thamani halisi ni 5.00cm + 0.03cm = 5.03cm. Kabla ya kupima daima angalia zero error kwanza.
Zero error is error that occurs when measuring instrument does not have its zero at correct position when not in use. This makes all measurements bigger or smaller by some amount. There are 2 types of zero error. The first type is positive zero error when zero pointer is to right of zero mark. This makes measurement bigger than true value. Example if zero error is +0.02cm, your measurement of 5.00cm is bigger, true value is 5.00cm - 0.02cm = 4.98cm. The second type is negative zero error when zero pointer is to left of zero mark. This makes measurement smaller. Example if zero error is -0.03cm, your measurement of 5.00cm is smaller, true value is 5.00cm + 0.03cm = 5.03cm. Before measuring always check zero error first.

Kiasi ni nini na vitengo vyake vya SI na SIU ni vipi?
What is volume and what are its SI and SIU units?
Kiasi ni nafasi yote ambayo kitu kinachukua au inawezwa na chombo. Kiasi ni kiasi cha derivative kinachotokana na kuzidisha urefu x upana x urefu. Kitengo cha SI cha kiasi ni mita za ujazo na kinaashiriwa na m³. Kitengo hiki ni kikubwa sana kwa vitu vya kila siku. Kwa hiyo tunatumia kitengo cha SIU ambacho ni sentimita za ujazo cm³ au mililita ml. Uhusiano ni 1 m³ = 1,000,000 cm³ na 1 cm³ = 1 ml. Kwa hiyo kikombe cha maji 250ml ni sawa na 250cm³. Kiasi cha vitu imara husika, vitu vya umbo la kawaida hupimwa kwa formula wakati vitu vya umbo lisilo la kawaida hupimwa kwa mbinu ya kuhamisha maji.
Volume is all space occupied by an object or enclosed by a container. Volume is a derived quantity that comes from multiplying length x width x height. The SI unit of volume is cubic meter denoted by m³. This unit is too big for daily objects. So we use SIU unit which is cubic centimeter cm³ or milliliter ml. The relationship is 1 m³ = 1,000,000 cm³ and 1 cm³ = 1 ml. Therefore a cup of water 250ml is equal to 250cm³. Volume of solid objects with regular shape is measured using formula while irregular objects are measured using displacement method.

Eleza mbinu 3 za kupima kiasi cha vitu imara, kioevu na gesi?
Explain 3 methods of measuring volume of solids, liquids and gases?
Kwa vitu imara vya umbo la kawaida kama mchemraba na silinda tunatumia formula. Kiasi cha mchemraba ni urefu x upana x urefu. Kiasi cha silinda ni pi r² h. Kisha tunapima urefu kwa vernier calipers na kuhesabu. Kwa vitu imara vya umbo lisilo la kawaida kama jiwe tunatumia mbinu ya kuhamisha maji. Tunajaza maji kwenye silinda ya kupimia hadi kiwango fulani V1, kisha tunaweka jiwe ndani, maji yanapanda hadi V2. Kiasi cha jiwe ni V2 - V1. Kwa vitu kioevu tunatumia silinda ya kupimia measuring cylinder au pipette au burette. Tunasoma kiwango cha chini cha uso wa maji meniscus. Kwa gesi kiasi chake ni sawa na kiasi cha chombo kinachokaliwa kwa sababu gesi haina kiasi maalum. Tunatumia silinda ya gesi au bomba la kupimia gesi gas syringe.
For regular solid objects like cube and cylinder we use formula. Volume of cube is length x width x height. Volume of cylinder is pi r² h. Then we measure length with vernier calipers and calculate. For irregular solid objects like stone we use displacement method. We fill water in measuring cylinder to level V1, then we put stone inside, water rises to V2. Volume of stone is V2 - V1. For liquids we use measuring cylinder or pipette or burette. We read bottom of water surface meniscus. For gases its volume is equal to volume of container it occupies because gas has no definite volume. We use gas cylinder or gas syringe.

Wiani ni nini na andika fomula yake na vitengo?
What is density and write its formula and units?
Wiani ni misa ya kitu kwa kila kitengo cha kiasi chake. Inaeleza ni kiasi gani cha vitu vimebanwa ndani ya nafasi fulani. Chuma kina wiani juu kwa sababu kina misa kubwa kwenye kiasi kidogo, wakati pamba ina wiani chini. Fomula ya wiani ni Wiani ρ = Misa m ÷ Kiasi V. Kitengo cha SI cha wiani ni kilogram kwa mita za ujazo kg/m³. Kitengo cha SIU ni gram kwa sentimita za ujazo g/cm³. Uhusiano ni 1 g/cm³ = 1000 kg/m³. Maji safi yana wiani wa 1 g/cm³ au 1000 kg/m³ kwenye halijoto ya 4°C. Kitu kizito zaidi ya maji huzama, kitu chepesi zaidi ya maji huelea.
Density is mass of an object per unit volume of that object. It tells how much matter is packed inside a certain space. Iron has high density because it has big mass in small volume, while cotton has low density. The formula for density is Density ρ = Mass m ÷ Volume V. The SI unit of density is kilogram per cubic meter kg/m³. The SIU unit is gram per cubic centimeter g/cm³. The relationship is 1 g/cm³ = 1000 kg/m³. Pure water has density of 1 g/cm³ or 1000 kg/m³ at temperature 4°C. An object heavier than water sinks, object lighter than water floats.

Eleza jinsi ya kupima wiani wa kitu imara kisichoyeyuka majini?
Explain how to measure density of a solid that does not dissolve in water?
Kwanza tunapima misa ya jiwe kwa kutumia mizani ya mihimili beam balance na tunapata m, kwa mfano gramu 60. Pili tunapima kiasi cha jiwe kwa mbinu ya kuhamisha maji. Tunajaza silinda ya kupimia maji hadi alama ya cm³ 50. Kisha tunaweka jiwe ndani kwa kutumia uzi mwembamba, maji yanapanda hadi cm³ 74. Tofauti ya viwango ni 74 - 50 = cm³ 24. Hii ndio kiasi V cha jiwe. Tatu tunatumia fomula: Wiani ρ = m ÷ V = 60g ÷ 24cm³ = 2.5 g/cm³. Kwa hiyo wiani wa jiwe ni gramu 2.5 kwa kila sentimita za ujazo. Kanuni muhimu ni jiwe lisinywe maji na uzi usiwe mzito ili kutoathiri kipimo.
First we measure mass of stone using beam balance and get m, for example 60 grams. Second we measure volume of stone using displacement method. We fill measuring cylinder with water to 50cm³ mark. Then we put stone inside using thin thread, water rises to 74cm³. Difference of levels is 74 - 50 = 24cm³. This is volume V of stone. Third we use formula: Density ρ = m ÷ V = 60g ÷ 24cm³ = 2.5 g/cm³. Therefore density of stone is 2.5 grams per cubic centimeter. Important rule is stone should not absorb water and thread should not be heavy to affect measurement.

Kwa nini mafuta huelea juu ya maji na chuma huzama?
Why does oil float on water and iron sinks?
Hii inategemea wiani wa vitu hivyo. Maji yana wiani wa 1.0 g/cm³. Mafuta ya kupikia yana wiani wa takriban 0.9 g/cm³ ambayo ni chini ya wiani wa maji. Kwa hiyo nguvu ya upanuzi upthrust inayofukuzwa na mafuta ni kubwa zaidi kuliko uzito wa mafuta yenyewe, hivyo mafuta husukumwa juu na kuelea. Chuma kina wiani wa takriban 7.9 g/cm³ ambayo ni juu zaidi ya wiani wa maji. Kwa hiyo uzito wa chuma ni mkubwa zaidi kuliko nguvu ya upanuzi, hivyo chuma huzama chini. Kanuni ni: Kitu kikitaka kuelea lazima kiwe na wiani chini ya kioevu, kikitaka kuzama lazima kiwe na wiani juu ya kioevu. Ndio maana meli kubwa ya chuma huelea kwa sababu ina hewa nyingi ndani yake hivyo wiani wake wote unakuwa chini ya maji.
This depends on density of those substances. Water has density of 1.0 g/cm³. Cooking oil has density of about 0.9 g/cm³ which is less than density of water. So upthrust force displaced by oil is greater than weight of oil itself, so oil is pushed up and floats. Iron has density of about 7.9 g/cm³ which is greater than density of water. So weight of iron is greater than upthrust force, so iron sinks down. The rule is: An object that wants to float must have density less than liquid, that wants to sink must have density greater than liquid. That is why big iron ship floats because it has a lot of air inside so its overall density becomes less than water.

Misa ni nini na tofauti yake na uzito ni ipi?
What is mass and what is its difference with weight?
Misa ni kiasi cha vitu au matter kilichomo ndani ya kitu. Misa haijabadilika popote ulipo ulimwenguni. Kama una misa ya kilogram 50 duniani, utakuwa na misa ya kilogram 50 pia mwezini. Kitengo cha SI cha misa ni kilogram kg. Uzito ni nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia inayovuta misa kuelekea katikati ya dunia. Uzito hubadilika kulingana na nguvu ya mvuto wa sehemu ulipo. Mwezini uzito wako ni 1/6 ya uzito wako duniani kwa sababu mvuto wa mwezi ni mdogo. Kitengo cha uzito ni Newton N kwa sababu ni nguvu. Fomula ya uzito ni W = m x g ambapo g ni 9.8 m/s² duniani. Kwa hiyo misa ni kiasi cha vitu wakati uzito ni nguvu.
Mass is amount of matter contained in an object. Mass does not change anywhere you are in the universe. If you have mass of 50kg on earth, you will have mass of 50kg also on moon. The SI unit of mass is kilogram kg. Weight is gravitational force of earth pulling mass towards center of earth. Weight changes depending on gravitational force of place you are. On moon your weight is 1/6 of your weight on earth because moon gravity is smaller. The unit of weight is Newton N because it is force. Formula for weight is W = m x g where g is 9.8 m/s² on earth. Therefore mass is amount of matter while weight is force.

Vifaa vya kupima misa ni vipi na utumie wake?
What are instruments for measuring mass and their uses?
Kifaa cha kwanza ni mizani ya mihimili beam balance ambayo inalinganisha misa isiyojulikana na misa inayojulikana ya vipimo. Haitumii umeme na ni sahihi sana. Hutumika maabarani kupima misa ya kemikali na vitu vidogo. Kifaa cha pili ni mizani ya umeme electronic balance ambayo inaonyesha misa kwenye screen ya digitali. Ni haraka na sahihi hadi gramu 0.01. Hutumika maduka ya dhahabu na maabara. Kifaa cha tatu ni mizani ya spring ambayo hupima uzito badala ya misa. Inaonyesha kwa Newton. Hutumika kupima uzito wa matunda sokoni na mizigo. Kifaa cha nne ni mizani ya kuoga bathroom scale ambayo hupima uzito wa binadamu. Kifaa cha tano ni mizani ya lori weigh bridge ambayo hupima misa ya magari makubwa na mizigo ya tani.
Instruments for measuring mass and their uses are as follows. The first instrument is beam balance which compares unknown mass with known masses of weights. It does not use electricity and is very accurate. It is used in laboratory to measure mass of chemicals and small objects. The second instrument is electronic balance which shows mass on digital screen. It is fast and accurate up to 0.01 gram. It is used in gold shops and laboratories. The third instrument is spring balance which measures weight instead of mass. It shows in Newton. It is used to measure weight of fruits at market and luggage. The fourth instrument is bathroom scale which measures weight of humans. The fifth instrument is weigh bridge which measures mass of big trucks and cargo of tons.

Muda ni nini na vifaa vya kupima muda ni vipi?
What is time and what are instruments for measuring time?
Muda ni kipindi kati ya matukio mawili. Muda ni kiasi cha msingi na kitengo chake cha SI ni sekunde s. Sekunde moja ilifafanuliwa awali kama 1/86400 ya siku moja. Kifaa cha kwanza ni saa ya mkono wrist watch ambayo hutumika kupima muda wa kila siku kama saa ngapi, dakika ngapi. Kifaa cha pili ni saa ya kukokotoa stopwatch ambayo hutumika kupima muda mfupi sana katika mashindano ya riadha. Ina usahihi wa sekunde 0.01. Kifaa cha tatu ni pendulum clock ambayo hutumia swing ya pendulum kudhibiti muda. Swing moja kamili huchukua muda sawa hivyo saa husonga sawa. Kifaa cha nne ni saa ya jua sundial ambayo ilitumiwa zamani. Inatumia kivuli cha fimbo kujua saa kulingana na nafasi ya jua. Kifaa cha tano ni saa ya atomiki atomic clock ambayo ni sahihi zaidi ulimwenguni. Hutumia vibrations za atomu ya cesium.
Time is duration between two events. Time is a base quantity and its SI unit is second s. One second was originally defined as 1/86400 of one day. The first instrument is wrist watch used to measure daily time like what hour, what minute. The second instrument is stopwatch used to measure very short time in athletics races. It has accuracy of 0.01 second. The third instrument is pendulum clock which uses swing of pendulum to control time. One complete swing takes equal time so clock moves evenly. The fourth instrument is sundial used long time ago. It uses shadow of stick to tell time according to position of sun. The fifth instrument is atomic clock which is most accurate in world. It uses vibrations of cesium atom.

Jinsi ya kubadilisha vitengo vya urefu, misa na muda?
How to convert units of length, mass and time?
Kwa urefu kumbuka ngazi hii: km hm dam m dm cm mm. Kila ukishuka ngazi moja unazidisha na 10, ukipanda unagawanya na 10. Mfano 5m kwenda cm: m kwenda cm ni kushuka ngazi 2, hivyo 5 x 100 = 500cm. Mfano 300cm kwenda m: cm kwenda m ni kupanda ngazi 2, hivyo 300 ÷ 100 = 3m. Kwa misa ngazi ni: tani kg g mg. 1kg = 1000g, 1 tani = 1000kg. Mfano 2.5kg kwenda g: 2.5 x 1000 = 2500g. Kwa muda: 1 dakika = 60 sekunde, 1 saa = 60 dakika, 1 siku = 24 saa. Mfano 2.5 saa kwenda dakika: 2.5 x 60 = 150 dakika. Mfano 180 sekunde kwenda dakika: 180 ÷ 60 = 3 dakika. Kanuni: Andika ngazi, tambua unapanda au kushuka, kisha zidisha au gawanya.
For length remember this ladder: km hm dam m dm cm mm. Every step down you multiply by 10, every step up you divide by 10. Example 5m to cm: m to cm is going down 2 steps, so 5 x 100 = 500cm. Example 300cm to m: cm to m is going up 2 steps, so 300 ÷ 100 = 3m. For mass ladder is: tonne kg g mg. 1kg = 1000g, 1 tonne = 1000kg. Example 2.5kg to g: 2.5 x 1000 = 2500g. For time: 1 minute = 60 seconds, 1 hour = 60 minutes, 1 day = 24 hours. Example 2.5 hours to minutes: 2.5 x 60 = 150 minutes. Example 180 seconds to minutes: 180 ÷ 60 = 3 minutes. Rule: Draw ladder, identify if going up or down, then multiply or divide.

Kwanini ni muhimu kutumia vitengo vya SI katika sayansi?
Why is it important to use SI units in science?
Vitengo vya SI ni muhimu kwa sababu vinakubalika ulimwenguni kote. Mwanasayansi nchini Tanzania akisema misa ni kilogram 5, mwanasayansi nchini Japan atafahamu kiasi hicho hicho bila kuchanganyikiwa. Kama kila nchi ingekuwa na vitengo vyake, sayansi ingekuwa vigumu kushirikiana. Vitengo vya SI pia vina uhusiano rahisi wa 10, 100, 1000 hivyo kubadilisha vitengo ni rahisi. Mfano kutoka mita kwenda sentimita ni kuzidisha na 100 tu. Tofauti na vitengo vya zamani kama inchi, futi, pauni ambavyo havina uhusiano rahisi. Matumizi ya vitengo vya SI hupunguza makosa na hurahisisha mahesabu katika majaribio na utafiti. Ndio maana mitihani yote ya NECTA hutumia vitengo vya SI pekee.
SI units are important because they are accepted worldwide. A scientist in Tanzania saying mass is 5 kilograms, a scientist in Japan will understand same amount without confusion. If every country had its own units, science would be difficult to share. SI units also have simple relationship of 10, 100, 1000 so converting units is easy. Example from meter to centimeter is just multiplying by 100. Unlike old units like inch, foot, pound which have no simple relationship. Use of SI units reduces errors and simplifies calculations in experiments and research. That is why all NECTA exams use only SI units.

Taja na ueleze tofauti 4 kati ya misa na uzito kwa jedwali?
Name and explain 4 differences between mass and weight in a table?
Misa ni kiasi cha vitu vilivyomo ndani ya kitu wakati uzito ni nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia inayovuta misa. Tofauti ya kwanza: Misa ni kiasi cha scalar wakati uzito ni kiasi cha vector kwa sababu ina mwelekeo kuelekea katikati ya dunia. Tofauti ya pili: Misa hupimwa kwa kilogram kg wakati uzito hupimwa kwa Newton N. Tofauti ya tatu: Misa haibadilika popote ulipo ulimwenguni wakati uzito hubadilika. Mwezini uzito ni 1/6 ya duniani lakini misa ni sawa. Tofauti ya nne: Misa hupimwa kwa mizani ya mihimili beam balance wakati uzito hupimwa kwa mizani ya spring balance.
Mass is amount of matter in an object while weight is gravitational force of earth pulling the mass. First difference: Mass is scalar quantity while weight is vector quantity because it has direction towards center of earth. Second difference: Mass is measured in kilogram kg while weight is measured in Newton N. Third difference: Mass does not change anywhere in universe while weight changes. On moon weight is 1/6 of earth but mass is same. Fourth difference: Mass is measured using beam balance while weight is measured using spring balance.

Kikombe cha plastiki kina misa ya gramu 20. Kimejazwa maji hadi kiwango cha cm³ 150. Kama wiani wa maji ni 1g/cm³, tafuta misa yote ya kikombe na maji?
A plastic cup has mass of 20 grams. It is filled with water up to 150cm³. If density of water is 1g/cm³, find total mass of cup and water?
Kwanza tunapata misa ya maji peke yake kwa kutumia fomula ya wiani. Tunaelewa kuwa Wiani ρ = Misa m ÷ Kiasi V. Kwa hiyo Misa m = Wiani ρ x Kiasi V. Tunaweka namba: m = 1g/cm³ x 150cm³ = gramu 150. Hii ni misa ya maji peke yake. Pili tunaongeza misa ya kikombe: Misa yote = misa ya kikombe + misa ya maji = 20g + 150g = gramu 170. Kwa hiyo misa yote ya kikombe kilichojaa maji ni gramu 170. Jibu hili linaonyesha jinsi wiani, misa na kiasi vinavyohusiana.
First we find mass of water alone using density formula. We know that Density ρ = Mass m ÷ Volume V. So Mass m = Density ρ x Volume V. We put numbers: m = 1g/cm³ x 150cm³ = 150 grams. This is mass of water alone. Second we add mass of cup: Total mass = mass of cup + mass of water = 20g + 150g = 170 grams. Therefore total mass of cup filled with water is 170 grams. This answer shows how density, mass and volume are related.

Kosa la sifuri zero error linasomwaje na kurekebishwa vipi?
How is zero error read and how is it corrected?
Ili kusoma zero error, funga pua za vernier calipers au micrometer kabisa bila kuweka kitu. Angalia ambapo mstari wa sifuri wa vernier scale unasimama dhidi ya main scale. Kama mstari wa sifuri uko kulia kwa sifuri ya main scale, hiyo ni zero error chanya. Hesabu idadi ya mistari iliyopita sifuri na uzidishe na 0.02mm kwa vernier. Mfano kama mistari 3 imepita, zero error = +3 x 0.02mm = +0.06mm. Kama mstari wa sifuri uko kushoto, hiyo ni zero error hasi. Mfano kama mistari 2 imepita kushoto, zero error = -2 x 0.02mm = -0.04mm. Kurekebisha: Baada ya kupata kipimo, toa zero error chanya au ongeza zero error hasi. Fomula: Kipimo halisi = Kipimo kilichosomwa - Zero error.
To read zero error, close jaws of vernier calipers or micrometer completely without putting object. Look where zero line of vernier scale stops against main scale. If zero line is to right of main scale zero, that is positive zero error. Count number of lines passed zero and multiply by 0.02mm for vernier. Example if 3 lines passed, zero error = +3 x 0.02mm = +0.06mm. If zero line is to left, that is negative zero error. Example if 2 lines passed left, zero error = -2 x 0.02mm = -0.04mm. To correct: After getting reading, subtract positive zero error or add negative zero error. Formula: True reading = Observed reading - Zero error.

Nguvu ni nini na ni kiasi cha aina gani?
What is force and what type of quantity is it?
Nguvu ni msukumo au kivutio kinachotumika kwenye kitu na kusababisha mabadiliko katika hali yake ya mwendo au umbo. Nguvu ni kiasi cha vector kwa sababu ina kiasi magnitude na mwelekeo direction. Kwa mfano ukisukuma meza, lazima ueleze nguvu ni Newton ngapi na unasukuma upande gani. Kitengo cha SI cha nguvu ni Newton N. Newton 1 ni nguvu inayohitajika kutoa kasi ya mita 1 kwa sekunde kwa kila sekunde kwa misa ya kilogramu 1. Nguvu hupimwa kwa chombo kinachoitwa dynamometer au spring balance. Nguvu iligunduliwa na mwanasayansi Isaac Newton kupitia sheria zake 3 za mwendo.
Force is a push or pull applied on an object that causes change in its state of motion or shape. Force is a vector quantity because it has magnitude and direction. For example when you push a table, you must state force in how many Newtons and which direction you are pushing. The SI unit of force is Newton N. 1 Newton is force required to give acceleration of 1 meter per second per second to a mass of 1 kilogram. Force is measured using instrument called dynamometer or spring balance. Force was discovered by scientist Isaac Newton through his 3 laws of motion.

Taja na ueleze aina 6 za nguvu kwa mifano?
Name and explain 6 types of force with examples?
Aina ya kwanza ni nguvu ya misuli muscular force ambayo binadamu na wanyama hutumia kwa mikono au miguu. Mfano ni kubeba mfuko wa saruji na kuvuta gari la mikokoteni. Aina ya pili ni nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia gravitational force ambayo huvuta vitu vyote kuelekea katikati ya dunia. Mfano ni tufaha likianguka kutoka mti na mvua ikinyesha chini. Aina ya tatu ni msuguano friction ambayo ni nguvu inayopinga mwendo kati ya nyuso mbili zinazogusana. Mfano ni breki za gari zikishika tairi na nyayo zako zikwamisha sakafu. Aina ya nne ni nguvu ya sumaku magnetic force ambayo huvuta vitu vya chuma tu. Mfano ni sumaku ikivuta misumari na dira ikielekeza kaskazini. Aina ya tano ni nguvu ya mkazo tension force ambayo hufanyika kwenye kamba au mnyororo unaovutwa. Mfano ni kamba ya bendera ikishikwa na upepo na mnyororo ukibeba ndoo ya maji. Aina ya sita ni nguvu ya upanuzi upthrust force ambayo husukuma kitu kuelekea juu kwenye kioevu au gesi. Mfano ni baluni ikipepea angani na meli ikiweza kuelea majini.
The first type is muscular force which humans and animals use with hands or legs. Example is carrying bag of cement and pulling handcart. The second type is gravitational force which pulls all objects towards center of earth. Example is apple falling from tree and rain falling down. The third type is friction which is force opposing motion between two surfaces in contact. Example is car brakes gripping tyre and your shoes gripping floor. The fourth type is magnetic force which attracts only iron objects. Example is magnet attracting nails and compass pointing north. The fifth type is tension force that occurs in rope or chain being pulled. Example is flag rope being pulled by wind and chain carrying bucket of water. The sixth type is upthrust force that pushes object upwards in liquid or gas. Example is balloon floating in air and ship floating on water.

Nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia ni nini na ni ipi nguvu yake duniani?
What is gravitational force and what is its strength on earth?
Nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia ni nguvu ambayo dunia huvuta vitu vyote vilivyo juu yake kuelekea katikati yake. Nguvu hii ndio inayosababisha vitu vianguke chini badala ya kuelea angani. Nguvu ya mvuto hubadilika kulingana na misa ya dunia na umbali kutoka katikati ya dunia. Duniani nguvu ya mvuto ni takriban Newton 9.8 kwa kila kilogramu ya misa. Hii inaandikwa g = 9.8 m/s² au N/kg. Kwa hiyo mtu mwenye misa ya kg 50 ana uzito wa 50 x 9.8 = Newton 490 duniani. Mwezini g = 1.6 m/s² hivyo uzito wa mtu huyo unakuwa 50 x 1.6 = Newton 80 tu. Ndio maana wanamwezi huruka juu kwa urahisi.
Gravitational force is force that earth pulls all objects on it towards its center. This force is what causes objects to fall down instead of floating in air. Gravitational force changes depending on mass of earth and distance from center of earth. On earth gravitational force is about 9.8 Newton for every kilogram of mass. This is written g = 9.8 m/s² or N/kg. So a person with mass 50kg has weight of 50 x 9.8 = 490 Newton on earth. On moon g = 1.6 m/s² so weight of that person becomes 50 x 1.6 = 80 Newton only. That is why astronauts jump high easily.

Sheria ya Hooke inasema nini na andika fomula yake?
What does Hooke's Law state and write its formula?
Sheria ya Hooke inasema upanuzi extension wa chemchemi ni sawia moja kwa moja na nguvu iliyotumika, mradi kikomo cha elasticity hakijavukwa. Kwa maneno mengine chemchemi ikiunganishwa uzito, itapanuka zaidi kadri uzito unavyoongezeka. Fomula ya sheria ya Hooke ni F = k x e ambapo F ni nguvu kwa Newton, e ni upanuzi kwa mita, na k ni uthabiti wa chemchemi spring constant kwa Newton kwa mita N/m. Kama chemchemi ina k = 20 N/m na ukiunganisha uzito wa Newton 10, upanuzi utakuwa e = F ÷ k = 10 ÷ 20 = mita 0.5. Sheria hii hutumika kutengeneza mizani ya spring na mattresses.
Hooke's Law states that extension of a spring is directly proportional to force applied, provided elastic limit is not exceeded. In other words when you hang weight on spring, it will extend more as weight increases. Formula for Hooke's Law is F = k x e where F is force in Newton, e is extension in meter, and k is spring constant in Newton per meter N/m. If spring has k = 20 N/m and you hang weight of 10N, extension will be e = F ÷ k = 10 ÷ 20 = 0.5 meter. This law is used to make spring balances and mattresses.

Nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia inatofautianaje mwezini na duniani?
How does gravitational force differ on moon and earth?
Nguvu ya mvuto wa dunia duniani ni g = 9.8 m/s² au N/kg. Hii inamaanisha kila kilogramu 1 ya misa huvutwa na nguvu ya Newton 9.8 kuelekea katikati ya dunia. Mwezini nguvu ya mvuto ni ndogo sana g = 1.6 m/s² tu kwa sababu mwezi una misa ndogo na radius ndogo kuliko dunia. Kwa hiyo uzito wa kitu mwezini ni 1/6 ya uzito wake duniani. Mfano mwanaanga mwenye misa ya kg 60 ana uzito wa 60 x 9.8 = 588N duniani. Mwezini uzito wake ni 60 x 1.6 = 96N tu. Ndio maana wanamwezi huruka juu na kuchukua hatua kubwa. Lakini misa yao bado ni kg 60 popote kwa sababu misa haitegemei mvuto.
Gravitational force on earth is g = 9.8 m/s² or N/kg. This means every 1 kilogram of mass is pulled by 9.8 Newton force towards center of earth. On moon gravitational force is very small g = 1.6 m/s² only because moon has smaller mass and radius than earth. So weight of object on moon is 1/6 of its weight on earth. Example astronaut with mass 60kg has weight 60 x 9.8 = 588N on earth. On moon his weight is 60 x 1.6 = 96N only. That is why astronauts jump high and take big steps. But their mass is still 60kg anywhere because mass does not depend on gravity.

Kwa nini ni vigumu kuanzisha mwendo wa sanduku zito kuliko kusogeza likishasonga?
Why is it harder to start motion of heavy box than to keep it moving?
Hii inatokea kwa sababu ya aina 2 za msuguano: msuguano tuli static friction na msuguano kinetiki kinetic friction. Msuguano tuli ni nguvu kubwa inayopinga kuanza kwa mwendo wakati kitu kimesimama. Lazima utumie nguvu kubwa kushinda msuguano huu kwanza. Msuguano kinetiki ni nguvu ndogo inayopinga mwendo wakati kitu kisha kinazama. Kwa hiyo ukishaanza kusogeza sanduku, msuguano unapungua na inakuwa rahisi kuusogeza. Mfano ni kubeba meza nzito chumbani. Unasukuma kwa nguvu sana kwanza ili ianze kusonga, lakini ikishasonga unaweza kusukuma kwa nguvu ndogo tu. Kanuni: Static friction > Kinetic friction.
This happens because of 2 types of friction: static friction and kinetic friction. Static friction is big force that opposes starting of motion when object is stationary. You must use big force to overcome this friction first. Kinetic friction is smaller force that opposes motion when object is already moving. So once you start moving box, friction reduces and it becomes easier to push. Example is moving heavy table in room. You push with very big force first to start moving, but once moving you can push with small force only. Rule: Static friction > Kinetic friction.

Sheria ya Hooke inatumikaje kwenye maisha ya kila siku?
How is Hooke's Law applied in daily life?
Sheria ya Hooke F = k x e hutumika sana. Matumizi ya kwanza ni kwenye mizani ya spring balance. Uzito ukiongezwa, chemchemi inapanuka zaidi hivyo sindano inaonyesha uzito mkubwa. Matumizi ya pili ni kwenye mattress na sofa za spring. Unapokaa, spring zinabanwa ili kukupa raha. Matumizi ya tatu ni kwenye magari kama suspension system. Spring za gari zinakandamizwa wakati gari linapita kwenye matuta ili kutoa mshtuko. Matumizi ya nne ni kwenye kalamu ya kubonyeza ballpoint pen. Spring ndogo ndani yake inakandamizwa na kurudi nyuma. Muhimu: Sheria hii inafanya kazi mradi uzito usizidi elastic limit, vinginevyo chemchemi haitarudi umbo lake.
Hooke's Law F = k x e is used a lot. First use is in spring balance. When weight increases, spring extends more so needle shows bigger weight. Second use is in mattresses and spring sofas. When you sit, springs are compressed to give you comfort. Third use is in cars as suspension system. Car springs are compressed when car passes bumps to reduce shock. Fourth use is in ballpoint pen. Small spring inside is compressed and returns back. Important: This law works provided weight does not exceed elastic limit, otherwise spring will not return to shape.

Eleza Sheria ya Kwanza ya Newton na mfano wake?
Explain Newton's First Law and its example?
Sheria ya Kwanza ya Newton inasema kitu kimesimama kitaendelea kusimama na kitu kinachosonga kwa kasi sawa kwenye mstari wa moja kwa moja kitaendelea kusonga hivyo, isipokuwa nguvu ya nje itumike. Hii inaitwa inertia. Inertia ni tabia ya kitu kupinga mabadiliko ya hali yake ya mwendo. Misa kubwa ina inertia kubwa. Mfano 1: Unapovuta nguo ya meza kwa haraka, vyombo haviumi kwa sababu inertia inataka vyombo vibaki pale. Mfano 2: Gari likisimama ghafla abiria husukumwa mbele kwa sababu miili yao inataka kuendelea kusonga. Mfano 3: Matunda yanaanguka kutoka mti wakati mti unatikiswa kwa sababu matunda yanataka kubaki pale.
Newton's First Law states an object at rest will remain at rest and an object moving at constant speed in straight line will continue moving, unless external force acts. This is called inertia. Inertia is tendency of object to resist change in its state of motion. Big mass has big inertia. Example 1: When you pull tablecloth quickly, dishes do not fall because inertia wants dishes to stay there. Example 2: When car stops suddenly passengers are pushed forward because their bodies want to continue moving. Example 3: Fruits fall from tree when tree is shaken because fruits want to remain there.

Sheria ya Pili ya Newton ni ipi na andika fomula yake?
What is Newton's Second Law and write its formula?
Sheria ya Pili ya Newton inasema kasi ya mabadiliko ya mwendo wa kitu ni sawia moja kwa moja na nguvu iliyotumika na inaelekea upande ule ule wa nguvu. Kwa maneno rahisi: Nguvu kubwa inatoa kasi kubwa, na misa ndogo inatoa kasi kubwa. Fomula yake ni F = m x a ambapo F ni nguvu kwa Newton, m ni misa kwa kilogramu, a ni kasi ya mabadiliko acceleration kwa m/s². Mfano: Kama unasukuma sanduku la kg 10 kwa nguvu ya N 50, kasi ya mabadiliko ni a = F ÷ m = 50 ÷ 10 = 5 m/s². Kama misa ingekuwa kg 20 na nguvu ileile N 50, kasi ingekuwa 50 ÷ 20 = 2.5 m/s² tu. Hii inaonyesha misa kubwa inahitaji nguvu kubwa.
Newton's Second Law states rate of change of motion of object is directly proportional to force applied and acts in same direction of force. In simple words: Bigger force gives bigger acceleration, and smaller mass gives bigger acceleration. Its formula is F = m x a where F is force in Newton, m is mass in kilogram, a is acceleration in m/s². Example: If you push box of 10kg with force of 50N, acceleration is a = F ÷ m = 50 ÷ 10 = 5 m/s². If mass was 20kg and same force 50N, acceleration would be 50 ÷ 20 = 2.5 m/s² only. This shows big mass needs big force.

Sheria ya Tatu ya Newton inasema nini na mfano wake?
What does Newton's Third Law state and its example?
Sheria ya Tatu ya Newton inasema kwa kila kitendo kuna mwelekeo sawa na kinyume. Kwa maneno mengine, nguvu zote huja kwa jozi action na reaction. Kitu A kikisukuma kitu B, kitu B pia kinasukuma kitu A kwa nguvu sawa lakini mwelekeo kinyume. Mfano 1: Unapotembea, mguu wako unasukuma ardhi nyuma, ardhi inasukuma mguu wako mbele. Mfano 2: Roketi ikirusha gesi chini kwa nguvu kubwa, gesi inasukuma roketi juu. Mfano 3: Ukiukanda ukuta kwa nguvu, ukuta pia unakusukuma kwa nguvu ileile. Muhimu: Nguvu za action na reaction hazifutani kwa sababu zinafanya kazi kwenye vitu tofauti.
Newton's Third Law states for every action there is equal and opposite reaction. In other words, all forces come in pairs action and reaction. If object A pushes object B, object B also pushes object A with equal force but opposite direction. Example 1: When you walk, your foot pushes ground backwards, ground pushes your foot forward. Example 2: Rocket pushes gas down with big force, gas pushes rocket up. Example 3: When you punch wall with force, wall also pushes you with same force. Important: Action and reaction forces do not cancel because they act on different objects.

Hesabu 1: Gari la misa kg 1000 linasukumwa kwa nguvu ya N 2000. Tafuta kasi ya mabadiliko?
Calculation 1: A car of mass 1000kg is pushed with force of 2000N. Find acceleration?
Tumia Sheria ya Pili ya Newton: F = m x a. Tunataka kupata a, kwa hiyo a = F ÷ m. Weka namba: a = 2000N ÷ 1000kg = 2 m/s². Kwa hiyo gari linaongeza kasi kwa mita 2 kwa kila sekunde kwa kila sekunde. Kama gari lilianza kutoka 0, baada ya sekunde 5 kasi yake itakuwa v = u + at = 0 + 2 x 5 = 10 m/s. Hii ni matumizi ya fomula mbili za mwendo.
Use Newton's Second Law: F = m x a. We want a, so a = F ÷ m. Put numbers: a = 2000N ÷ 1000kg = 2 m/s². So car increases speed by 2 meters per second every second. If car started from 0, after 5 seconds its speed will be v = u + at = 0 + 2 x 5 = 10 m/s. This is use of two motion formulas.

Hesabu 2: Uzito wa mfuko wa saruji ni N 490 duniani. Tafuta misa yake?
Calculation 2: Weight of cement bag is 490N on earth. Find its mass?
Tumia fomula ya uzito: W = m x g. Duniani g = 9.8 m/s². Tunataka m, kwa hiyo m = W ÷ g. Weka namba: m = 490N ÷ 9.8 m/s² = 50 kg. Kwa hiyo mfuko una misa ya kilogramu 50. Kumbuka misa hii itakuwa kg 50 pia mwezini, lakini uzito wake mwezini utakuwa W = 50 x 1.6 = 80N tu kwa sababu g ya mwezi ni 1.6 m/s².
Use weight formula: W = m x g. On earth g = 9.8 m/s². We want m, so m = W ÷ g. Put numbers: m = 490N ÷ 9.8 m/s² = 50 kg. So bag has mass of 50 kilograms. Remember this mass will be 50kg also on moon, but its weight on moon will be W = 50 x 1.6 = 80N only because moon g is 1.6 m/s².

Taja matumizi 5 ya nguvu za sumaku na msuguano katika teknolojia?
Name 5 uses of magnetic and frictional forces in technology?
Matumizi ya nguvu ya sumaku: 1. Katika dynamo na motor za umeme, sumaku hutengeneza umeme na kusogeza mashine. 2. Katika dira compass, sindano ya sumaku inaelekeza kaskazini ili wasafiri wasipotee. 3. Katika crane za uwanja wa vyuma, sumaku kubwa huvuta na kubeba vipande vya chuma. 4. Katika simu na radio, sumaku ndogo ndani ya spika hutoa sauti. 5. Katika kadi za benki ATM cards, data huhifadhiwa kwenye kipande cha sumaku. Matumizi ya msuguano: 1. Breki za magari na baisikeli hutumia msuguano kusimamisha gurudumu. 2. Nyayo za viatu na matairi ya gari huundwa mbaya ili yafute vizuri barabarani. 3. Wapiga kiberiti hutumia msuguano kuwasha moto. 4. Wachezaji wa mpira hutumia msuguano kudhibiti mpira wasiteleze. 5. Vilevile vya chupa hutumiwa msuguano ili ufuniko usiteleze.
Uses of magnetic force: 1. In dynamo and electric motor, magnet makes electricity and moves machines. 2. In compass, magnetic needle points north so travelers do not get lost. 3. In scrap yard cranes, big magnet lifts and carries iron pieces. 4. In phones and radio, small magnet in speaker produces sound. 5. In ATM bank cards, data is stored on magnetic strip. Uses of friction: 1. Car and bicycle brakes use friction to stop wheel. 2. Shoe soles and car tyres are made rough to grip road well. 3. Match strikers use friction to light fire. 4. Football players use friction to control ball without slipping. 5. Bottle lids use friction so cover does not slip.

Eleza umuhimu wa elasticity na kikomo cha elasticity?
Explain importance of elasticity and elastic limit?
Elasticity ni uwezo wa kitu kurudi umbo lake la awali baada ya nguvu kuondolewa. Mfano ni bendi ya mpira na chemchemi. Kikomo cha elasticity elastic limit ni nguvu kubwa zaidi ambayo kitu kinaweza kubeba bila kuharibika umbo lake. Kama ukivuta bendi zaidi ya kikomo chake, itakata na haitarudi tena. Umuhimu wa elasticity: 1. Inatumika kwenye mattress na sofa ili zitoe raha. 2. Inatumika kwenye magari kama suspension springs ili zipunguza mshtuko. 3. Inatumika kwenye nguo za spandex ili ziweze kunyoshoka na kurudi. 4. Inatumika kwenye trampoline ili mpiga mbizi arudi juu. 5. Inatumika kwenye kalamu za kubonyeza. Kama kitu hakina elasticity, hakitarudi umbo lake na kitavunjika.
Elasticity is ability of object to return to original shape after force is removed. Example is rubber band and spring. Elastic limit is maximum force that object can take without permanent damage to shape. If you stretch rubber band beyond its limit, it will break and not return. Importance of elasticity: 1. Used in mattresses and sofas to give comfort. 2. Used in cars as suspension springs to reduce shock. 3. Used in spandex clothes to stretch and return. 4. Used in trampoline so diver bounces up. 5. Used in push pens. If object has no elasticity, it will not return to shape and will break.

Mazoezi ya NECTA: Sanduku la kg 5 linasukumwa kwa nguvu ya N 20 kwenye sakafu isiyo na msuguano. Tafuta kasi ya mabadiliko?
NECTA Exercise: Box of 5kg is pushed with force of 20N on floor with no friction. Find acceleration?
Kwa sababu hakuna msuguano, nguvu yote N 20 inatumika kusukuma sanduku. Tumia Sheria ya Pili ya Newton F = m x a. Tunataka a, kwa hiyo a = F ÷ m = 20N ÷ 5kg = 4 m/s². Jibu: Sanduku linaongeza kasi kwa mita 4 kwa kila sekunde kwa kila sekunde. Kama lilianza kutoka 0, baada ya sekunde 3 kasi yake itakuwa v = 0 + 4 x 3 = 12 m/s. Hii ni swali la kawaida NECTA linachanganya Sheria ya Newton na mwendo.
Because there is no friction, all 20N force is used to push box. Use Newton's Second Law F = m x a. We want a, so a = F ÷ m = 20N ÷ 5kg = 4 m/s². Answer: Box increases speed by 4 meters per second every second. If it started from 0, after 3 seconds its speed will be v = 0 + 4 x 3 = 12 m/s. This is common NECTA question mixing Newton's Law with motion.

Muhtasari wa Mada 3: Nguvu
Summary of Topic 3: Force
Nguvu ni msukumo au kivutio vector quantity kitengo Newton N. Athari za nguvu ni: kuanzisha mwendo, kusimamisha mwendo, kubadilisha kasi, kubadilisha umbo, kubadilisha mwelekeo. Aina za nguvu: misuli, mvuto, msuguano, sumaku, mkazo, upanuzi. Nguvu za mawasiliano zinahitaji kugusa, zisizo za mawasiliano hazihitaji. Msuguano ni nguvu inayopinga mwendo. Una faida kama kutusaidia kutembea na hasara kama kuharisha mashine. Sheria 3 za Newton: 1. Inertia 2. F = m x a 3. Action = Reaction. Force is push or pull vector quantity unit Newton N. Effects of force are: start motion, stop motion, change speed, change shape, change direction. Types of force: muscular, gravity, friction, magnetic, tension, upthrust. Contact forces need touching, non-contact forces don’t. Friction is force opposing motion. It has advantages like helping us walk and disadvantages like wearing machines. 3 Newton's Laws: 1. Inertia 2. F = m x a 3. Action = Reaction. Hooke's Law: F = k x e provided elastic limit not exceeded. Rules: Force measured by dynamometer, weight = m x g, on earth g = 9.8 m/s².

Shinikizo ni nini na fomula yake ni ipi?
What is pressure and what is its formula?
Shinikizo ni nguvu inayofanya kazi kwa kila kitengo cha eneo. Kwa maneno rahisi ni "nguvu iliyokamana". Fomula ya shinikizo ni P = F ÷ A ambapo P ni shinikizo kwa Pascal Pa au N/m², F ni nguvu kwa Newton N, A ni eneo kwa mita za mraba m². Kitengo cha SI cha shinikizo ni Pascal Pa. 1 Pascal = 1 Newton kwa mita ya mraba 1. Kwa hiyo ukisukuma ukuta kwa nguvu ya N 100 kwa eneo la m² 0.5, shinikizo ni 100 ÷ 0.5 = Pascal 200. Shinikizo kubwa linatokea nguvu ikawa kubwa au eneo likawa dogo.
Pressure is force acting per unit area. In simple words it is "concentrated force". Formula for pressure is P = F ÷ A where P is pressure in Pascal Pa or N/m², F is force in Newton N, A is area in square meter m². The SI unit of pressure is Pascal Pa. 1 Pascal = 1 Newton per 1 square meter. So if you push wall with 100N force on area 0.5m², pressure is 100 ÷ 0.5 = 200 Pascal. Big pressure happens when force is big or area is small.

Tofauti 4 kati ya nguvu na shinikizo ni zipi?
What are 4 differences between force and pressure?
Tofauti ya kwanza: Nguvu hupima msukumo au kivutio kwa ujumla wakati shinikizo hupima nguvu kwa kila kitengo cha eneo. Tofauti ya pili: Kitengo cha nguvu ni Newton N wakati kitengo cha shinikizo ni Pascal Pa au N/m². Tofauti ya tatu: Nguvu ni kiasi cha vector wakati shinikizo ni kiasi cha scalar kwa sababu haina mwelekeo maalum. Tofauti ya nne: Nguvu inaweza kubadilisha mwendo au umbo wakati shinikizo linaeleza jinsi nguvu ilivyosambazwa. Mfano: Msumari mkali una eneo dogo hivyo hata nguvu ndogo hutoa shinikizo kubwa na kupenya mbao, lakini msumari butu una eneo kubwa hivyo nguvu ileile inatoa shinikizo ndogo.
First difference: Force measures push or pull in general while pressure measures force per unit area. Second difference: Unit of force is Newton N while unit of pressure is Pascal Pa or N/m². Third difference: Force is vector quantity while pressure is scalar quantity because it has no specific direction. Fourth difference: Force can change motion or shape while pressure explains how force is distributed. Example: Sharp nail has small area so even small force gives big pressure and penetrates wood, but blunt nail has big area so same force gives small pressure.

Kwa nini kisu chenye makali hukata vizuri kuliko kisichokuwa na makali?
Why does sharp knife cut better than blunt knife?
Hii inatokea kwa sababu ya eneo. Kisu chenye makali kina ncha nyembamba sana hivyo eneo A ni dogo sana. Kwa fomula P = F ÷ A, eneo dogo linatoa shinikizo kubwa hata kwa nguvu ndogo F. Kwa hiyo shinikizo kubwa linaweza kukata nyama na mboga kwa urahisi. Kisu kisichokuwa na makali kina ncha pana hivyo eneo A ni kubwa. Nguvu ileile itatoa shinikizo ndogo hivyo haiwezi kukata. Ndio maana mama anapopanga mboga ananoa kisu kwanza ili eneo lipungue na shinikizo liongezeke. Kanuni: Eneo dogo = shinikizo kubwa, Eneo kubwa = shinikizo ndogo.
This happens because of area. Sharp knife has very thin edge so area A is very small. From formula P = F ÷ A, small area gives big pressure even with small force F. So big pressure can cut meat and vegetables easily. Blunt knife has wide edge so area A is big. Same force will give small pressure so it cannot cut. That is why mother sharpens knife first before cutting vegetables so area reduces and pressure increases. Rule: Small area = big pressure, Big area = small pressure.

Kwa nini mtu akivaa viatu vya kisigino nyembamba anaacha alama kwenye sakafu?
Why does person wearing thin heel shoes leave marks on floor?
Viatu vya kisigino nyembamba vina eneo dogo sana chini ya kisigino. Misa ya mtu inatoa uzito W = m x g ambao ni nguvu F. Hii nguvu inatenda kwenye eneo dogo la kisigino. Kwa fomula P = F ÷ A, eneo dogo linazidisha shinikizo sana. Shinikizo kubwa linaharibu au kuacha alama kwenye sakafu laini kama mbao au tiles. Viatu vya mpira wa michezo vina eneo kubwa chini, hivyo nguvu ileile inasambazwa kwenye eneo kubwa na shinikizo linapungua. Ndio maana viatu vya michezo haviharibu sakafu. Ndio maana tembo miguu yake inaenea pana ili isizame mchanga.
Thin heel shoes have very small area under heel. Mass of person gives weight W = m x g which is force F. This force acts on small area of heel. From formula P = F ÷ A, small area multiplies pressure a lot. Big pressure damages or leaves marks on soft floor like wood or tiles. Sports shoes have big area underneath, so same force is spread on big area and pressure reduces. That is why sports shoes do not damage floor. That is why elephant feet spread wide so they do not sink in sand.

Vifaa vya kupima shinikizo ni vipi?
What are instruments for measuring pressure?
Kifaa cha kwanza ni barometer ambayo hupima shinikizo la anga atmospheric pressure. Inatumia safu ya zebaki mercury. Shinikizo la kawaida la anga ni 760mmHg au Pascal 101,300. Kifaa cha pili ni manometer ambayo hupima tofauti ya shinikizo kati ya gesi na anga. Hutumika kwenye bomba la gesi. Kifaa cha tatu ni aneroid barometer ambayo haina zebaki. Ina sanduku la chuma linalopanuka au kupungua na shinikizo. Kifaa cha nne ni tire pressure gauge ambayo hupima shinikizo la hewa kwenye tairi la gari. Shinikizo la kawaida la tairi ni 220-250 kPa. Kifaa cha tano ni sphygmomanometer ambayo hupima shinikizo la damu ya binadamu kwa mmHg.
Instruments for measuring pressure are: The first instrument is barometer which measures atmospheric pressure. It uses column of mercury. Normal atmospheric pressure is 760mmHg or 101,300 Pascal. The second instrument is manometer which measures difference between gas pressure and atmosphere. It is used in gas pipe. The third instrument is aneroid barometer which has no mercury. It has metal box that expands or contracts with pressure. The fourth instrument is tire pressure gauge which measures air pressure in car tyre. Normal tyre pressure is 220-250 kPa. The fifth instrument is sphygmomanometer which measures human blood pressure in mmHg.

Shinikizo la kioevu linategemea nini na fomula yake ni ipi?
On what does liquid pressure depend and what is its formula?
Shinikizo la kioevu ni nguvu inayofanya kazi kwa kila kitengo cha eneo ndani ya kioevu. Linategemea vitu 3: 1. Kina depth h - kioevu kikinzani zaidi shinikizo huongezeka. 2. Wiani density ρ wa kioevu - kioevu kizito kama zebaki kina shinikizo kubwa kuliko maji. 3. Nguvu ya mvuto g - duniani g = 9.8 m/s². Fomula yake ni P = h x ρ x g ambapo P ni shinikizo kwa Pascal, h ni kina kwa mita, ρ ni wiani kwa kg/m³, g ni 9.8 m/s². Mfano: Bwawa la maji lenye kina mita 10, wiani wa maji 1000kg/m³, shinikizo chini ni P = 10 x 1000 x 9.8 = Pascal 98,000. Ndio maana kuta za bwawa chini huwa nene kuliko juu.
Liquid pressure depends on 3 things: 1. Depth h - deeper liquid has higher pressure. 2. Density ρ of liquid - heavy liquid like mercury has bigger pressure than water. 3. Gravitational force g - on earth g = 9.8 m/s². Its formula is P = h x ρ x g where P is pressure in Pascal, h is depth in meter, ρ is density in kg/m³, g is 9.8 m/s². Example: Water dam with depth 10m, water density 1000kg/m³, pressure at bottom is P = 10 x 1000 x 9.8 = 98,000 Pascal. That is why dam walls are thicker at bottom than top.

Sheria ya Pascal inasema nini na mifano 3?
What does Pascal's Law state and 3 examples?
Sheria ya Pascal inasema shinikizo lililotumika kwenye kioevu kilichofungwa husambazwa sawa sawa katika pande zote. Kioevu hakiwezi kukandamizwa compress, hivyo shinikizo linasafirishwa haraka kila mahali. Mfano 1: Ukikanyaga bomba la maji lililofungwa, maji husukumwa kwa nguvu sawa kwenye pua zote za bomba. Mfano 2: Brakes za maji hydraulic brakes za gari. Unapokanyaga pedal, shinikizo husafirishwa kwa mafuta hadi breki zote 4 na gari lisimame. Mfano 3: Jack ya maji hydraulic jack inayotumika kuinua magari gereji. Nguvu ndogo kwenye pistoni ndogo inatoa nguvu kubwa kwenye pistoni kubwa.
Pascal's Law states pressure applied to enclosed liquid is transmitted equally in all directions. Liquid cannot be compressed, so pressure is transmitted quickly everywhere. Example 1: When you step on closed water pipe, water pushes with equal force at all pipe openings. Example 2: Hydraulic brakes of car. When you press pedal, pressure is transmitted by oil to all 4 brakes and car stops. Example 3: Hydraulic jack used to lift cars in garage. Small force on small piston gives big force on big piston.

Eleza jinsi mashine ya maji hydraulic machine inavyofanya kazi?
Explain how hydraulic machine works?
Mashine ya maji ina pistoni 2 za saizi tofauti zilizounganishwa na bomba lililojaa mafuta. Pistoni ndogo ina eneo A1, pistoni kubwa ina eneo A2. Ukisukuma pistoni ndogo kwa nguvu F1, shinikizo P = F1 ÷ A1 linatengenezwa. Kwa Sheria ya Pascal, shinikizo hili P husafirishwa hadi pistoni kubwa. Kwa hiyo nguvu F2 kwenye pistoni kubwa ni F2 = P x A2 = F1 x A2 ÷ A1. Kwa sababu A2 ni kubwa kuliko A1, F2 inakuwa kubwa sana kuliko F1. Kwa hiyo nguvu ndogo inaweza kuinua gari zito. Kanuni: Faida ya nguvu = Eneo la pistoni kubwa ÷ Eneo la pistoni ndogo.
Hydraulic machine has 2 pistons of different sizes connected by pipe filled with oil. Small piston has area A1, big piston has area A2. When you push small piston with force F1, pressure P = F1 ÷ A1 is created. By Pascal's Law, this pressure P is transmitted to big piston. So force F2 on big piston is F2 = P x A2 = F1 x A2 ÷ A1. Because A2 is bigger than A1, F2 becomes much bigger than F1. So small force can lift heavy car. Rule: Force advantage = Area of big piston ÷ Area of small piston.

Kwa nini mabwawa na mabakuli ya maji yana kuta nene chini?
Why are dam and water tank walls thicker at bottom?
Hii ni kwa sababu shinikizo la maji huongezeka kadri kina kinavyozidi. Kwa fomula P = h x ρ x g, kina h kikizidi, shinikizo P linaongezeka. Chini ya bwawa maji yana shinikizo kubwa sana dhidi ya ukuta, hivyo ukuta lazima uwe nene na imara ili usivunjike. Juu ya bwawa maji yana kina kidogo hivyo shinikizo ni ndogo, ukuta unaweza kuwa mwembamba. Ndio maana ukiona bwawa la Mtera au Nyerere, chini yake ukuta ni mzito sana. Kanuni ileile inatumika kwa mabakuli ya maji na meli za mafuta.
This is because water pressure increases as depth increases. From formula P = h x ρ x g, as depth h increases, pressure P increases. At bottom of dam water has very big pressure against wall, so wall must be thick and strong to avoid breaking. At top of dam water has small depth so pressure is small, wall can be thin. That is why when you see Mtera or Nyerere dam, bottom wall is very heavy. Same rule applies to water tanks and oil tankers.

Barometer ya zebaki inafanyaje kazi na urefu wa kawaida ni upi?
How does mercury barometer work and what is normal height?
Barometer ya zebaki iligunduliwa na Evangelista Torricelli. Ina bomba la glasi lenye urefu mita 1 lililofungwa upande mmoja na kujazwa zebaki. Bomba linageuzwa na kuwekwa kwenye bakuli la zebaki. Zebaki hushuka na kusimama kwenye urefu fulani. Nafasi juu ya zebaki ni utupu vacuum. Urefu wa safu ya zebaki unasawazisha shinikizo la anga. Urefu wa kawaida baharini ni milimita 760 mmHg. Hii ni sawa na Pascal 101,300. Kama urefu unashuka hadi 740mm, maana yake shinikizo la anga limepungua na mvua inaweza kunyesha. Kama unapanda mlima, urefu unapungua kwa sababu anga juu ni nyepesi.
Mercury barometer was discovered by Evangelista Torricelli. It has 1 meter glass tube closed at one end and filled with mercury. Tube is inverted and placed in bowl of mercury. Mercury falls and stops at certain height. Space above mercury is vacuum. Height of mercury column balances atmospheric pressure. Normal height at sea level is 760mmHg. This equals 101,300 Pascal. If height falls to 740mm, it means atmospheric pressure has reduced and rain may fall. If you climb mountain, height reduces because air up is lighter.

Shinikizo la gesi linasababishwa na nini?
What causes gas pressure?
Shinikizo la gesi linasababishwa na mgongano wa chembe za gesi na kuta za chombo. Gesi ina chembe ndogo ndogo zinazosogea kwa haraka kila upande. Chembe hizi zinapogonga ukuta wa chombo, hutoa msukumo mdogo. Idadi kubwa ya migongano kwa sekunde moja inatengeneza shinikizo. Kwa hiyo shinikizo la gesi linategemea vitu 3: 1. Idadi ya chembe - chembe nyingi zinagonga zaidi hivyo shinikizo kubwa. 2. Kasi ya chembe - joto likiongezeka chembe husogea haraka hivyo shinikizo kubwa. 3. Kiasi cha chombo - chombo kidogo chembe zinagonga ukuta mara nyingi zaidi hivyo shinikizo kubwa.
Gas pressure is caused by collision of gas particles with walls of container. Gas has very small particles moving fast in all directions. When these particles hit wall of container, they give small push. Large number of collisions per second creates pressure. So gas pressure depends on 3 things: 1. Number of particles - many particles hit more so pressure is big. 2. Speed of particles - when temperature increases particles move faster so pressure is big. 3. Volume of container - small container particles hit wall more times so pressure is big.

Sheria ya Boyle inasema nini na fomula yake?
What does Boyle's Law state and its formula?
Sheria ya Boyle inasema kwa joto lisilobadilika, kiasi cha gesi kinyume sawia na shinikizo lake. Kwa maneno rahisi: shinikizo likiongezeka, kiasi kinapungua na kinyume chake. Fomula yake ni P1 x V1 = P2 x V2 ambapo P1 na V1 ni shinikizo na kiasi cha mwanzo, P2 na V2 ni shinikizo na kiasi cha mwisho. Mfano: Sindano ya mpira ikiwa na hewa kiasi cm³ 100 na shinikizo kPa 100. Ukikandamiza kiasi hadi cm³ 50, shinikizo litakuwa P2 = P1 x V1 ÷ V2 = 100 x 100 ÷ 50 = kPa 200. Hii ndio maana pikipiki ukipiga pampu, hewa inakandamizwa na shinikizo kuongezeka.
Boyle's Law states at constant temperature, volume of gas is inversely proportional to its pressure. In simple words: if pressure increases, volume decreases and vice versa. Its formula is P1 x V1 = P2 x V2 where P1 and V1 are initial pressure and volume, P2 and V2 are final pressure and volume. Example: Bicycle pump with air volume 100cm³ and pressure 100kPa. If you compress volume to 50cm³, pressure will be P2 = P1 x V1 ÷ V2 = 100 x 100 ÷ 50 = 200kPa. That is why when you pump bicycle, air is compressed and pressure increases.

Sheria ya Charles inasema nini?
What does Charles' Law state?
Sheria ya Charles inasema kwa shinikizo lisilobadilika, kiasi cha gesi sawia moja kwa moja na joto lake kwa Kelvin. Kwa maneno rahisi: joto likiongezeka, kiasi cha gesi kinaongezeka na kinyume chake. Fomula yake ni V1 ÷ T1 = V2 ÷ T2 ambapo T lazima iwe kwa Kelvin K. Kelvin = Celsius + 273. Mfano: Puto iliyojaa hewa baridi ina kiasi kidogo. Ukiweka kwenye jua, joto linaongezeka hivyo hewa ndani inapanuka na puto inavimba. Hii ndio kanuni ya puto ya hewa moto hot air balloon kupaa. Joto likipungua usiku, puto inashuka.
Charles' Law states at constant pressure, volume of gas is directly proportional to its temperature in Kelvin. In simple words: if temperature increases, gas volume increases and vice versa. Its formula is V1 ÷ T1 = V2 ÷ T2 where T must be in Kelvin K. Kelvin = Celsius + 273. Example: Balloon filled with cold air has small volume. If you put in sun, temperature increases so air inside expands and balloon inflates. This is principle of hot air balloon to fly. When temperature drops at night, balloon goes down.

Kwa nini tairi la gari hupata moto likitumiwa muda mrefu?
Why does car tyre get hot when used for long?
Hii inatokea kwa sababu ya Sheria ya Boyle na msuguano. Tairi likizunguka barabarani, linabanwa na kunyoshoka mara kwa mara. Hewa ndani ya tairi inakandamizwa na kupanuka mara kwa mara. Kama hewa inakandamizwa, kiasi kinapungua hivyo shinikizo na joto vinaongezeka kwa Sheria ya Boyle. Msuguano kati ya tairi na barabara pia hutoa joto. Kwa hiyo tairi linapata moto. Ndio maana madereva hupima shinikizo la tairi likiwa baridi asubuhi. Kama shinikizo ni kubwa sana likiwa moto, tairi linaweza kupasuka.
This happens because of Boyle's Law and friction. When tyre rotates on road, it is compressed and expands repeatedly. Air inside tyre is compressed and expands repeatedly. When air is compressed, volume reduces so pressure and temperature increase by Boyle's Law. Friction between tyre and road also produces heat. So tyre gets hot. That is why drivers measure tyre pressure when cold in morning. If pressure is too high when hot, tyre can burst.

Hesabu: Gesi ina shinikizo kPa 200 na kiasi cm³ 500 kwa joto lisilobadilika. Ikiwa shinikizo likawa kPa 400, tafuta kiasi kipya?
Calculation: Gas has pressure 200kPa and volume 500cm³ at constant temperature. If pressure becomes 400kPa, find new volume?
Tumia Sheria ya Boyle: P1 x V1 = P2 x V2. Tunataka V2, kwa hiyo V2 = P1 x V1 ÷ P2. Weka namba: V2 = 200 x 500 ÷ 400 = 250 cm³. Kwa hiyo kiasi kimepungua kutoka 500 hadi 250 cm³ kwa sababu shinikizo limeongezeka mara 2. Hii inaonyesha uhusiano kinyume kati ya P na V. NECTA hupenda swali kama hili.
Use Boyle's Law: P1 x V1 = P2 x V2. We want V2, so V2 = P1 x V1 ÷ V2. Put numbers: V2 = 200 x 500 ÷ 400 = 250 cm³. So volume has reduced from 500 to 250 cm³ because pressure doubled. This shows inverse relationship between P and V. NECTA likes this type of question.

Taja matumizi 5 ya shinikizo la kioevu katika maisha ya kila siku?
Name 5 uses of liquid pressure in daily life?
Matumizi ya kwanza ni kwenye brakes za maji hydraulic brakes za magari. Unapokanyaga pedal, shinikizo la mafuta husafirishwa kwa breki zote 4 na gari lisimame haraka. Matumizi ya pili ni kwenye jack ya maji hydraulic jack inayotumika kuinua magari gereji. Nguvu ndogo inatoa nguvu kubwa ya kuinua gari zito. Matumizi ya tatu ni kwenye mashine ya kukamua takataka garbage compactor. Pistoni kubwa inatumia shinikizo kubwa kubana takataka. Matumizi ya nne ni kwenye viti vya nywele barber chair. Shinikizo la maji husaidia kiti kupanda na kushuka. Matumizi ya tano ni kwenye mashine za ujenzi kama bulldozer na excavator. Shinikizo la mafuta husogeza mikono mizito ya mashine.
First use is in hydraulic brakes of cars. When you press pedal, oil pressure is transmitted to all 4 brakes and car stops fast. Second use is in hydraulic jack used to lift cars in garage. Small force gives big force to lift heavy car. Third use is in garbage compactor machine. Big piston uses big pressure to compress garbage. Fourth use is in barber chair. Liquid pressure helps chair go up and down. Fifth use is in construction machines like bulldozer and excavator. Oil pressure moves heavy arms of machine.

Shinikizo la anga atmospheric pressure ni nini na athari zake 3?
What is atmospheric pressure and 3 effects?
Shinikizo la anga ni uzito wa safu ya hewa iliyoko juu yetu kutoka angani hadi ardhini. Kwa kawaida baharini ni Pascal 101,300 au 760mmHg. Athari ya kwanza: Inatusaidia kunywa kwa bomba straw. Unapovuta hewa kwenye bomba, shinikizo ndani ya bomba linapungua hivyo shinikizo la anga nje linasukuma kinywaji juu. Athari ya pili: Inashikilia karatasi kwenye dirisha. Kwenye treni ya mwendo kasi, shinikizo nje ya dirisha ni kubwa kuliko ndani hivyo karatasi inashikamana. Athari ya tatu: Inasababisha kupata pumzi wakati wa kupanda mlima mrefu. Juu ya mlima shinikizo la anga ni ndogo hivyo oksijeni ni ndogo na binadamu hupata shida kupumua.
Atmospheric pressure is weight of air column above us from sky to ground. Normally at sea level it is 101,300 Pascal or 760mmHg. First effect: It helps us drink using straw. When you suck air in straw, pressure inside straw reduces so atmospheric pressure outside pushes drink up. Second effect: It holds paper on window. In fast train, pressure outside window is bigger than inside so paper sticks. Third effect: It causes breathing difficulty when climbing high mountain. On top of mountain atmospheric pressure is small so oxygen is small and people get breathing problems.

Kwa nini bomba la maji linawekwa chini ya tanki la maji?
Why is water pipe placed at bottom of water tank?
Hii ni kwa sababu ya shinikizo la maji. Kwa fomula P = h x ρ x g, shinikizo huongezeka kadri kina kinavyozidi. Bomba likiwekwa chini, maji yana kina kikubwa zaidi juu yake hivyo shinikizo ni kubwa na maji yanatoka kwa kasi kubwa. Kama bomba ingewekwa juu ya tanki, kina kingekuwa kidogo na shinikizo ndogo hivyo maji yangetoka polepole. Ndio maana tanki la maji ya nyumbani huwekwa juu ya paa na bomba la maji linatoka chini ya tanki ili shinikizo lilete maji mpaka bafuni.
This is because of water pressure. From formula P = h x ρ x g, pressure increases as depth increases. If pipe is placed at bottom, water has bigger depth above it so pressure is big and water comes out with high speed. If pipe was placed at top of tank, depth would be small and pressure small so water would come out slowly. That is why home water tank is placed on roof and water pipe comes from bottom of tank so pressure brings water to bathroom.

Eleza jinsi sindano na pampu inavyofanya kazi kwa kutumia shinikizo?
Explain how syringe and pump work using pressure?
Sindano ya dawa inafanya kazi kwa kupunguza shinikizo. Unapovuta pistoni nyuma, hewa ndani ya sindano inapanuka hivyo shinikizo linapungua. Shinikizo la anga nje ya sindano ni kubwa, hivyo linasukuma dawa kuingia ndani ya sindano. Unapobonyeza pistoni, shinikizo ndani linaongezeka na dawa inasukumwa mwilini. Pampu ya mkono inafanya kazi vilevile. Unapovuta mpini juu, shinikizo ndani ya pampu linapungua hivyo hewa au maji yanasukumwa ndani na shinikizo la anga. Unapobaniza mpini chini, shinikizo linaongezeka na maji yanatupwa nje.
Syringe works by reducing pressure. When you pull piston back, air inside syringe expands so pressure reduces. Atmospheric pressure outside syringe is big, so it pushes medicine into syringe. When you press piston, pressure inside increases and medicine is pushed into body. Hand pump works same way. When you pull handle up, pressure inside pump reduces so air or water is pushed in by atmospheric pressure. When you push handle down, pressure increases and water is thrown out.

Kwa nini ndege ya abiria ina mashimo madogo kwenye dirisha?
Why does passenger aeroplane have small holes in window?
Ndege ya abiria ikiruka juu sana, shinikizo la anga nje ni ndogo sana. Ndani ya ndege shinikizo huwekwa sawa na baharini ili abiria wapumue vizuri. Tofauti kubwa ya shinikizo kati ya ndani na nje inaweza kuvunja dirisha. Mashimo madogo madogo yanayoitwa bleed holes huwekwa kwenye dirisha la ndani. Mashimo haya husaidia kusawazisha shinikizo kati ya tabaka 2 za kioo. Shinikizo la nje linasafirishwa kupitia shimo hadi kati ya vioo, hivyo kioo cha nje ndio kinabeba shinikizo kubwa. Hii inalinda abiria na dirisha lisipasuke.
Passenger aeroplane flying very high has very small atmospheric pressure outside. Inside aeroplane pressure is kept same as sea level so passengers breathe well. Big difference in pressure between inside and outside can break window. Small holes called bleed holes are placed in inner window. These holes help equalize pressure between 2 glass layers. Outside pressure is transmitted through hole to space between glasses, so outer glass carries big pressure. This protects passengers and prevents window from bursting.

Hesabu 1: Msumari una ncha ya eneo cm² 0.01 unasukumwa kwa nguvu ya N 50. Tafuta shinikizo?
Calculation 1: Nail with tip area 0.01cm² is pushed with force of 50N. Find pressure?
Kwanza badili cm² kwenda m²: 0.01 cm² = 0.01 ÷ 10000 = 0.000001 m². Tumia fomula P = F ÷ A. Weka namba: P = 50N ÷ 0.000001m² = Pascal 50,000,000. Kwa hiyo shinikizo ni kubwa sana Pa 50 milioni. Ndio maana msumari mkali unaweza kupenya mbao kwa nguvu ndogo tu. Kama eneo lingekuwa cm² 1, shinikizo lingekuwa 50 ÷ 0.0001 = Pa 500,000 tu. Hii inaonyesha eneo dogo linazidisha shinikizo mara 100.
First convert cm² to m²: 0.01 cm² = 0.01 ÷ 10000 = 0.000001 m². Use formula P = F ÷ A. Put numbers: P = 50N ÷ 0.000001m² = 50,000,000 Pascal. So pressure is very big 50 million Pa. That is why sharp nail can penetrate wood with small force only. If area was 1cm², pressure would be 50 ÷ 0.0001 = 500,000 Pa only. This shows small area multiplies pressure 100 times.

Hesabu 2: Bwawa la maji lina kina mita 15. Wiani wa maji 1000kg/m³, g = 9.8 m/s². Tafuta shinikizo chini?
Calculation 2: Water dam has depth 15m. Water density 1000kg/m³, g = 9.8 m/s². Find pressure at bottom?
Tumia fomula ya shinikizo la kioevu: P = h x ρ x g. Weka namba: P = 15 x 1000 x 9.8 = Pascal 147,000. Kwa hiyo shinikizo chini ya bwawa ni Pa 147,000. Kama wiani wa zebaki 13600kg/m³ ungetumiwa, shinikizo lingekuwa 15 x 13600 x 9.8 = Pa 1,999,200 kubwa zaidi. Hii inaeleza kwa nini barometer hutumia zebaki badala ya maji.
Use liquid pressure formula: P = h x ρ x g. Put numbers: P = 15 x 1000 x 9.8 = 147,000 Pascal. So pressure at bottom of dam is 147,000 Pa. If mercury density 13600kg/m³ was used, pressure would be 15 x 13600 x 9.8 = 1,999,200 Pa bigger. This explains why barometer uses mercury instead of water.

Hesabu 3: Mashine ya maji ina pistoni ndogo eneo cm² 5 na pistoni kubwa eneo cm² 500. Nguvu N 20 inatumika kwenye ndogo. Tafuta nguvu kwenye kubwa?
Calculation 3: Hydraulic machine has small piston area 5cm² and big piston area 500cm². Force 20N applied on small. Find force on big?
Kwanza tafuta shinikizo: P = F1 ÷ A1 = 20N ÷ 5cm² = 4 N/cm². Kwa Sheria ya Pascal, shinikizo hili husafirishwa. Tafuta F2: F2 = P x A2 = 4 x 500 = N 8000. Kwa hiyo nguvu ndogo N 20 imetoa nguvu kubwa N 8000. Faida ya nguvu = 500 ÷ 5 = mara 100. Hii ndio kanuni ya jack ya gari.
First find pressure: P = F1 ÷ A1 = 20N ÷ 5cm² = 4 N/cm². By Pascal's Law, this pressure is transmitted. Find F2: F2 = P x A2 = 4 x 500 = 8000 N. So small force 20N has produced big force 8000N. Force advantage = 500 ÷ 5 = 100 times. This is principle of car jack.

Muhtasari wa Mada 4: Shinikizo
Summary of Topic 4: Pressure
Shinikizo ni nguvu kwa kila kitengo cha eneo. Fomula P = F ÷ A, kitengo Pascal Pa au N/m². Shinikizo kubwa = nguvu kubwa au eneo dogo. Shinikizo la kioevu P = h x ρ x g. Linaongezeka na kina na wiani. Sheria ya Pascal: shinikizo kwenye kioevu kilichofungwa husambazwa sawa. Matumizi: brakes za maji, jack ya maji, bulldozer. Shinikizo la anga ni uzito wa hewa = 101,300 Pa. Linasababisha kunywa kwa bomba na kupumua mlima. Vipimo: barometer, manometer, tire gauge. Kanuni: Eneo dogo = shinikizo kubwa, Eneo kubwa = shinikizo ndogo.
Pressure is force per unit area. Formula P = F ÷ A, unit Pascal Pa or N/m². Big pressure = big force or small area. Liquid pressure P = h x ρ x g. It increases with depth and density. Pascal's Law: pressure in enclosed liquid is transmitted equally. Uses: hydraulic brakes, hydraulic jack, bulldozer. Atmospheric pressure is weight of air = 101,300 Pa. It causes drinking with straw and breathing on mountain. Instruments: barometer, manometer, tire gauge. Rule: Small area = big pressure, Big area = small pressure.

Mazoezi ya NECTA: Eleza kwa nini wanawake wanaoanza kutembea kwa visigino nyembamba huacha mashimo kwenye sakafu laini?
NECTA Exercise: Explain why ladies walking with thin heels leave holes on soft floor?
Visigino nyembamba vina eneo dogo sana chini yake. Uzito wa mwanamke unatoa nguvu F. Kwa fomula P = F ÷ A, eneo A dogo linatoa shinikizo P kubwa sana. Shinikizo kubwa linazidi nguvu ya sakafu laini kama mbao au tiles hivyo linaacha mashimo. Viatu vya mpira vina eneo kubwa, shinikizo linapungua hivyo haviharibu sakafu. Jibu la NECTA lazima litaje fomula P = F/A na uhusiano kati ya A na P.
Thin heels have very small area underneath. Weight of lady gives force F. From formula P = F ÷ A, small area A gives very big pressure P. Big pressure exceeds strength of soft floor like wood or tiles so it leaves holes. Rubber shoes have big area, pressure reduces so they do not damage floor. NECTA answer must mention formula P = F/A and relationship between A and P.

Nishati ni nini na kitengo chake ni kipi?
What is energy and what is its unit?
Nishati ni uwezo wa kufanya kazi au kusababisha mabadiliko. Kitu chenye nishati kinaweza kusukuma, kuinua, kupasha joto, kutoa mwanga au sauti. Kama hakuna nishati, hakuna kazi inayofanyika. Kitengo cha SI cha nishati ni Joule J kwa heshima ya James Joule. Vitengo vingine ni kilojoule kJ = 1000J, kalori cal, kilowatt-hour kWh ambayo hutumika kwenye bili za umeme. 1 kWh = 3,600,000J. Fomula ya kazi na nishati ni ileile: Kazi = Nguvu x Umbali = Joule. Kwa hiyo nishati iliyotumika = kazi iliyofanyika.
Energy is ability to do work or cause change. Object with energy can push, lift, heat, give light or sound. If there is no energy, no work is done. SI unit of energy is Joule J named after James Joule. Other units are kilojoule kJ = 1000J, calorie cal, kilowatt-hour kWh used in electricity bills. 1 kWh = 3,600,000J. Formula for work and energy is same: Work = Force x Distance = Joule. So energy used = work done.

Taja aina 8 za nishati na mfano wa kila moja?
Name 8 types of energy and example of each?
Aina 8 za nishati ni: 1. Nishati ya mwendo kinetic energy - gari linalokimbia, mpira unaozunguka. 2. Nishati inayoweza kutumika potential energy - jiwe juu ya paa, bendi iliyonyoshwa. 3. Nishati ya joto heat energy - moto, jua, maji ya moto. 4. Nishati ya mwanga light energy - jua, balbu, umeme wa radi. 5. Nishati ya sauti sound energy - redio, kengele, mtu anapopiga kelele. 6. Nishati ya umeme electrical energy - betri, stima, simu. 7. Nishati ya kemikali chemical energy - chakula, betri, mafuta ya petroli. 8. Nishati ya nyuklia nuclear energy - bomu la atomiki, mtambo wa umeme wa nyuklia.
8 types of energy are: 1. Kinetic energy - moving car, rolling ball. 2. Potential energy - stone on roof, stretched rubber band. 3. Heat energy - fire, sun, hot water. 4. Light energy - sun, bulb, lightning. 5. Sound energy - radio, bell, person shouting. 6. Electrical energy - battery, electricity, phone. 7. Chemical energy - food, battery, petrol fuel. 8. Nuclear energy - atomic bomb, nuclear power plant.

Tofauti kati ya nishati ya mwendo na inayoweza kutumika?
Difference between kinetic and potential energy?
Nishati ya mwendo kinetic energy ni nishati ambayo kitu kina kwa sababu kinazosonga. Kitu kikitulia kinetic energy yake ni sifuri. Inategemea misa na kasi. Fomula: KE = ½ x m x v². Mfano: gari, mpira, mtu anayekimbia. Nishati inayoweza kutumika potential energy ni nishati iliyohifadhiwa kwenye kitu kwa sababu ya nafasi au umbo lake. Inasubiri kutumika. Fomula ya juu: PE = m x g x h. Mfano: maji juu ya mlima, jiwe lililoinuliwa, mshale kwenye upinde. Kanuni: Kitu kikianguka, PE inabadilika kuwa KE.
Kinetic energy is energy an object has because it is moving. Stationary object has zero kinetic energy. It depends on mass and speed. Formula: KE = ½ x m x v². Example: car, ball, person running. Potential energy is energy stored in object due to position or shape. It waits to be used. Formula for height: PE = m x g x h. Example: water on mountain, lifted stone, arrow on bow. Rule: When object falls, PE changes to KE.

Kwa nini chakula kinachukuliwa kuwa nishati ya kemikali?
Why is food considered chemical energy?
Chakula kama wali, maharage, nyama kina molekuli za sukari, mafuta na protini. Ndani ya molekuli hizi kuna vifungo vya kemikali chemical bonds vilivyohifadhi nishati. Tunapokula, mwili wetu huvunja vifungo hivyo kupitia mmeng'enyo digestion. Nishati iliyofungwa inaachiliwa na kutumika kwa kazi: kupumua, kutembea, kufikiria, kupasha mwili joto. Nishati hii haionekani lakini athari zake tunaona. Ndio maana mtu asiyekula hana nguvu. Betri pia ina nishati ya kemikali ndani yake ambayo inabadilika kuwa nishati ya umeme.
Food like rice, beans, meat has molecules of sugar, fats and proteins. Inside these molecules there are chemical bonds that store energy. When we eat, our body breaks these bonds through digestion. Locked energy is released and used for work: breathing, walking, thinking, keeping body warm. This energy is not seen but we see its effects. That is why person who does not eat has no strength. Battery also has chemical energy inside that changes to electrical energy.

Sheria ya uhifadhi wa nishati inasema nini?
What does Law of Conservation of Energy state?
Sheria ya uhifadhi wa nishati inasema nishati haiwezi kutengenezwa wala kuharibiwa, inaweza tu kubadilika kutoka aina moja kwenda nyingine. Jumla ya nishati katika mfumo uliofungwa hubaki ileile. Mfano 1: Pendo likirushwa juu, PE yake inaongezeka na KE inapungua, likishuka PE inabadilika kuwa KE. Mfano 2: Balbu inachukua nishati ya umeme na kuibadilisha kuwa mwanga + joto. Mfano 3: Gari linachoma petroli chemical energy → nishati ya joto → nishati ya mwendo. Hakuna nishati inayopotea, baadhi hubadilika kuwa joto ambalo ni vigumu kutumia.
Law of Conservation of Energy states energy cannot be created or destroyed, it can only change from one form to another. Total energy in closed system remains same. Example 1: Ball thrown up, its PE increases and KE decreases, when falling PE changes to KE. Example 2: Bulb takes electrical energy and changes it to light + heat. Example 3: Car burns petrol chemical energy → heat energy → kinetic energy. No energy is lost, some changes to heat which is hard to use.


Kanuni ya Archimedes inasema nini?
What does Archimedes Principle state?
Kanuni ya Archimedes inasema: Kitu kikizama kwa kiasi au kwa yote kwenye kioevu, husukumwa juu kwa nguvu sawa na uzito wa kioevu kilichohamishwa. Nguvu hiyo inaitwa msukumo upthrust. Fomula: Msukumo = Uzito wa kioevu kilichohamishwa = misa ya kioevu x 10. Kama uzito wa kitu > msukumo, kitu kitasama. Kama uzito wa kitu < msukumo, kitu kitaelea. Kama ni sawa, kitasimama katikati floating neutrally. Kanuni: Sababu ya vitu kuelea ni msukumo.
Archimedes Principle states: An object wholly or partly immersed in fluid is pushed up by force equal to weight of fluid displaced. That force is called upthrust. Formula: Upthrust = Weight of fluid displaced = mass of fluid x 10. If weight of object > upthrust, object will sink. If weight of object < upthrust, object will float. If equal, it will stay in middle floating neutrally. Rule: Reason things float is upthrust.

Hesabu 2: Jiwe la uzito 20N lilizamishwa majini. Uzito wake majini ukawa 15N. Tafuta msukumo?
Calculation 2: Stone of weight 20N was immersed in water. Its weight in water became 15N. Find upthrust?
Msukumo = Uzito hewani - Uzito majini. Msukumo = 20N - 15N = 5N. Hii maana yake maji yalisukuma jiwe juu kwa nguvu 5N. Kwa hiyo uzito wa maji yaliyohamishwa na jiwe ni 5N. NECTA hupenda swali hili sana: "Pata hasara ya uzito". Hasara = msukumo.
Upthrust = Weight in air - Weight in water. Upthrust = 20N - 15N = 5N. This means water pushed stone up by force 5N. So weight of water displaced by stone is 5N. NECTA likes this question a lot: "Find loss in weight". Loss = upthrust.

Maombi 3 ya wiani na kanuni ya Archimedes?
3 applications of density and Archimedes Principle?
Maombi 1: Hydromita - kifaa cha kupima wiani wa vinywaji kama maziwa na asidi ya betri. Inaelea zaidi kwenye kinywaji kizito. Maombi 2: Boti ya anga hot air balloon - inajazwa hewa ya moto ambayo wiani wake ni mdogo kuliko hewa ya baridi. Msukumo wa hewa baridi inainua baluni. Maombi 3: Meli na manowari submarine. Meli ina umbo la mashimo ili wastani wa wiani upungue. Manowari hubadilisha wiani kwa kuingiza au kutoa maji kwenye matangi yake ili izame au ielee. Kanuni: Badili wiani, badili kuelea.
Application 1: Hydrometer - device for measuring density of liquids like milk and battery acid. It floats higher in heavier liquid. Application 2: Hot air balloon - filled with hot air whose density is less than cold air. Upthrust of cold air lifts balloon. Application 3: Ships and submarines. Ship has hollow shape so average density reduces. Submarine changes density by putting in or removing water in its tanks to sink or float. Rule: Change density, change floating.

Tulinganishe kuelea floating na kuzama sinking?
Compare floating and sinking?
Kuelea hutokea wakati wiani wa kitu ni mdogo kuliko wiani wa kioevu, RD < 1. Sehemu ya kitu inabaki juu ya kioevu. Mfano: Mbao, plastiki, meli. Msukumo = uzito wa kitu. Kuzama hutokea wakati wiani wa kitu ni mkubwa kuliko wiani wa kioevu, RD > 1. Kitu chote kinaingia ndani ya kioevu. Mfano: Chuma, mawe, sarafu. Msukumo < uzito wa kitu. Kuelea kwa sehemu partial floating hutokea wakati RD = 1. Kanuni: Pima RD kwanza ujue kuelea au kuzama.
Floating happens when density of object is less than density of liquid, RD < 1. Part of object remains above liquid. Example: Wood, plastic, ship. Upthrust = weight of object. Sinking happens when density of object is bigger than density of liquid, RD > 1. Whole object goes inside liquid. Example: Iron, stones, coin. Upthrust < weight of object. Partial floating happens when RD = 1. Rule: Check RD first to know float or sink.

Muhtasari wa Mada 9: Densela
Summary of Topic 9: Density
Wiani ni misa kwa kila ujazo, ρ = m/V, kitengo kg/m³. Uzito maalum RD = wiani wa kitu ÷ wiani wa maji, haina kitengo. Kama RD > 1 kitasama, RD < 1 kitaelea. Wiani wa vitu vikali unapatikana kwa njia ya kuhamisha maji. Kanuni ya Archimedes: kitu kizama kinapata msukumo sawa na uzito wa kioevu kilichohamishwa. Maombi: hydromita, meli, baluni. NECTA hupenda hesabu za ρ=m/V na kupata msukumo kwa kutumia hasara ya uzito.
Density is mass per volume, ρ = m/V, unit kg/m³. Relative density RD = density of object ÷ density of water, no unit. If RD > 1 it sinks, RD < 1 it floats. Density of solids found by displacement method. Archimedes Principle: immersed object gets upthrust equal to weight of displaced fluid. Applications: hydrometer, ship, balloon. NECTA likes calculations of ρ=m/V and finding upthrust using loss in weight.

Umeme ni nini na aina zake 2?
What is electricity and its 2 types?
Umeme electricity ni nishati inayotokana na elektroni zinazosogea. Ni nishati inayoweza kubadilika kuwa mwanga, joto, mwendo, sauti. Aina 2 za umeme: 1. Umeme tulivu static electricity - elektroni husimama sehemu moja na kujikusanya. Mfano: Unapopiga mswaki kwenye nywele kavu kisha unavuta, nywele zinasimama. Mabao ya nguo yanashikana. 2. Umeme mtiriko current electricity - elektroni husogea kwa mfululizo kwenye waya. Hii ndio tunayotumia nyumbani na shuleni kuwasha taa, radio, TV. Kanuni: Static = simama, Current = tiri.
Electricity is energy from moving electrons. It is energy that can change to light, heat, motion, sound. 2 types of electricity: 1. Static electricity - electrons stay in one place and collect. Example: When you rub comb on dry hair then pull, hair stands. Clothes stick together. 2. Current electricity - electrons move in a flow in wire. This is what we use at home and school to switch on light, radio, TV. Rule: Static = stay, Current = flow.

Eleza tofauti kati ya viendeshaji na visivyopitisha umeme?
Explain difference between conductors and insulators?
Viendeshaji conductors ni vitu vinavyopitisha umeme kwa urahisi kwa sababu vina elektroni huru. Mfano: Shaba, alumini, chuma, maji ya chumvi. Hutumika kutengeneza nyaya za umeme. Visivyopitisha umeme insulators ni vitu visivyopitisha umeme kwa sababu havina elektroni huru. Mfano: Plastiki, mpira, glasi, mbao kavu. Hutumika kufunika nyaya na kutengeneza vipini vya vitu vya umeme ili tusipate mshtuko. Kanuni: Gusa chuma + umeme = hatari. Gusa plastiki = salama.
Conductors are materials that pass electricity easily because they have free electrons. Example: Copper, aluminium, iron, salt water. Used to make electric wires. Insulators are materials that do not pass electricity because they have no free electrons. Example: Plastic, rubber, glass, dry wood. Used to cover wires and make handles of electric things so we do not get shock. Rule: Touch metal + electricity = danger. Touch plastic = safe.

Sehemu 4 kuu za mzunguko wa umeme simple circuit?
4 main parts of simple electric circuit?
Mzunguko wa umeme ni njia iliyofungwa ambayo elektroni hupita. Sehemu 1: Chanzo cha umeme source - kama betri cell au betri kavu. Inatoa nishati ya kusukuma elektroni. Sehemu 2: Waya wa kuunganishia connecting wires - hufanywa na shaba au alumini kwa sababu ni viendeshaji. Sehemu 3: Kizima/washa switch - hufungua au kufunga mzunguko. Ukifungua, taa inazima. Ukifunga, taa inawaka. Sehemu 4: Kipokea nishati load - kama balbu, kengele, motori. Hubadilisha umeme kuwa aina nyingine ya nishati. Kanuni: Mzunguko lazima ufungwe ili umeme utirike.
Electric circuit is closed path that electrons pass. Part 1: Source - like cell or dry battery. It gives energy to push electrons. Part 2: Connecting wires - made of copper or aluminium because they are conductors. Part 3: Switch - opens or closes circuit. When open, bulb goes off. When closed, bulb lights. Part 4: Load - like bulb, bell, motor. Changes electricity to other energy. Rule: Circuit must be closed for current to flow.

Tufafanue tofauti kati ya mzunguko wazi na mzunguko uliofungwa?
Explain difference between open and closed circuit?
Mzunguko uliofungwa closed circuit ni mzunguko ambao njia ya umeme haina kukatika. Elektroni zinaweza kutirika kutoka betri kwenda balbu na kurudi betri. Taa itawaka. Mzunguko wazi open circuit ni mzunguko ambao kuna sehemu ilikatika, mara nyingi kwa sababu swichi imefunguliwa au waya umekatika. Elektroni hazipiti. Taa inazima. Kwenye nyumba zetu, swichi inapotuzima taa inafanya mzunguko wazi. Kanuni: Kama kuna pengo, hakuna umeme.
Closed circuit is circuit where path of electricity has no break. Electrons can flow from battery to bulb and back to battery. Bulb will light. Open circuit is circuit where there is a break, usually because switch is open or wire is cut. Electrons do not pass. Bulb goes off. In our houses, when switch turns off light it makes open circuit. Rule: If there is gap, no electricity.

Kwa nini ndege ya nanga hazishiki umeme wakati wa radi?
Why does aeroplane not get electric shock during lightning?
Ndege imetengenezwa kwa chuma ambayo ni kiendeshaji kizuri. Wakati radi ipiga ndege, umeme unapita juu ya uso wa ndege badala ya kupita ndani kwa abiria. Hii inaitwa kanuni ya Faraday cage. Umeme unapita kwenye mwili wa chuma na kutoka kwa mkia wa ndege kwenda angani. Abiria wako salama ndani kwa sababu umeme haufuati njia ya ndani. Ndio maana pia gari la chuma ni salama wakati wa mvua ya radi. Kanuni: Kaa ndani ya chuma, umeme upite nje.
Aeroplane is made of metal which is good conductor. When lightning hits plane, electricity passes on surface of plane instead of passing inside to passengers. This is called Faraday cage principle. Electricity passes on metal body and exits at tail of plane to sky. Passengers are safe inside because electricity does not follow inside path. That is why also metal car is safe during thunderstorm. Rule: Stay inside metal, electricity passes outside.

Tulinganishe mzunguko wa mfululizo na mzunguko sambamba?
Compare series and parallel circuit?
Mzunguko wa mfululizo series circuit: Vipengele vinaunganishwa mstari mmoja. Umeme unapita sehemu moja tu. Hasara: Kama balbu moja ikiharibika, zote zinazima. Voltage ya betri inagawanywa. Hutumika kwenye taa za sherehe Christmas lights za zamani. Mzunguko sambamba parallel circuit: Vipengele vinaunganishwa njia tofauti tofauti. Umeme unagawanyika. Faida: Kama balbu moja ikiharibika, nyingine zinawaka. Kila balbu inapata voltage kamili. Nyumba zetu zote zimeunganishwa sambamba ili TV na friji zifanye kazi pamoja. Kanuni: Series = mstari 1, Parallel = njia nyingi.
Series circuit: Components connected in one line. Current passes one path only. Disadvantage: If one bulb spoils, all go off. Battery voltage is shared. Used in old Christmas lights. Parallel circuit: Components connected in different separate paths. Current divides. Advantage: If one bulb spoils, others stay on. Each bulb gets full voltage. All our houses are connected parallel so TV and fridge work together. Rule: Series = 1 line, Parallel = many paths.

Hatari 3 za umeme na kinga yake?
3 dangers of electricity and protection?
Hatari 1: Mshtuko wa umeme electric shock - unatokea mtu akiugusa waya uchi au kifaa chenye hitilafu. Unaweza kuua. Kinga: Vaa viatu vya mpira, usiguse umeme kwa mikono iliyo mvua, zima swichi kabla ya kurekebisha. Hatari 2: Moto fire - unatokea waya zikizidishwa current nyingi au kufungwa short circuit. Kinga: Usizibie soketi nyingi, tumia fuse. Hatari 3: Kufa ghafla - umeme wa voltage juu. Kinga: Weka ishara "HATARI UMEME" kwenye nguzo. Kanuni: Maji + umeme = kifo.
Danger 1: Electric shock - happens when person touches bare wire or faulty device. Can kill. Protection: Wear rubber shoes, do not touch electricity with wet hands, switch off before repairing. Danger 2: Fire - happens when wires carry too much current or short circuit. Protection: Do not overload sockets, use fuse. Danger 3: Sudden death - high voltage electricity. Protection: Put sign "DANGER ELECTRICITY" on poles. Rule: Water + electricity = death.

4 uses of electricity in daily life?
Matumizi 1: Taa na mwanga - balbu, fluorescent, LED. Umeme unabadilika kuwa mwanga. Matumizi 2: Joto - pasi, hita, jiko la umeme, maji ya kuogea. Umeme unabadilika kuwa joto. Use 1: Light - bulb, fluorescent, LED. Electricity changes to light. Use 2: Heat - iron, heater, electric cooker, bathing water. Electricity changes to heat. Use 3: Motion - motors of fan, washing machine, blender, electric car. Electricity changes to motion. Use 4: Communication and entertainment - phone, radio, TV, computer. Electricity changes to sound and picture. Rule: Electricity is good servant but bad master.

Shinikizo la anga atmospheric pressure ni nini?
What is atmospheric pressure?
Shinikizo la anga ni nguvu inayofanywa na uzito wa hewa iliyo juu yetu kwenye kila kitengo cha eneo. Hewa ina misa hivyo ina uzito. Uzito huo unasukuma chini vitu vyote duniani. Kwa wastani bahari, shinikizo la anga = 101,300 N/m² au 101.3 kPa au 760 mmHg. Kifaa cha kupima shinikizo la anga kinaitwa baromita barometer. Kanuni: Tunapoishi ndani ya "bahari ya hewa" kubwa. Ndio maana masikio yetu husikia "pup" tukipanda mlima.
Atmospheric pressure is force due to weight of air above us per unit area. Air has mass so it has weight. That weight pushes down all things on earth. At average sea level, atmospheric pressure = 101,300 N/m² or 101.3 kPa or 760 mmHg. Device for measuring atmospheric pressure is called barometer. Rule: We live inside big "sea of air". That is why our ears go "pop" when we climb mountain.

Eleza majaribio ya Torricelli ya kupima shinikizo la anga?
Explain Torricelli experiment to measure atmospheric pressure?
Evangelista Torricelli mwaka 1643 alitumia bomba la glasi urefu 1m lililofungwa mwisho mmoja. Hatua 1: Alijaza bomba zima na zebaki mercury. Hatua 2: Akaifunika ncha kwa kidole kisha akaibadilisha kwenye beseni la zebaki. Hatua 3: Alipotoa kidole, zebaki ikashuka na kusimama urefu 760mm juu ya zebaki ya beseni. Nafasi iliyobaki juu inaitwa utupu wa Torricelli vacuum. Urefu 760mm ndio shinikizo la anga. Zebaki imeshikwa juu na shinikizo la anga likisukuma zebaki kwenye beseni. Kanuni: Shinikizo la anga = uzito wa safu ya zebaki.
Evangelista Torricelli in 1643 used 1m glass tube closed at one end. Step 1: He filled tube full with mercury. Step 2: He covered end with finger then inverted it in basin of mercury. Step 3: When he removed finger, mercury fell and stopped at height 760mm above basin mercury. Space left on top is called Torricelli vacuum. Height 760mm is atmospheric pressure. Mercury is held up by atmospheric pressure pushing mercury in basin. Rule: Atmospheric pressure = weight of mercury column.

Kwa nini maji huwezi kuyavuta kwa bomba zaidi ya mita 10?
Why can you not suck water with pipe more than 10m?
Ukivuta maji kwa bomba/nyasi straw, wewe huondoa hewa mdomoni. Shinikizo ndani ya bomba linapungua. Shinikizo la anga nje linasukuma maji kwenye bomba kwenda mdomoni. Lakini shinikizo la anga linaweza kusukuma safu ya maji hadi urefu 10.3m tu. Kama bomba ni urefu mita 15, shinikizo la anga halitoshi kusukuma maji yote hadi juu. Ndio maana pampu za maji za "kuvuta" haziwezi zaidi ya mita 10. Kanuni: Pampu inasukuma, si kuvuta.
When you suck water with pipe/straw, you remove air in mouth. Pressure inside pipe reduces. Atmospheric pressure outside pushes water in pipe to mouth. But atmospheric pressure can only push water column up to 10.3m only. If pipe is 15m long, atmospheric pressure is not enough to push all water up. That is why "suction" water pumps cannot go more than 10m. Rule: Pump pushes, does not suck.

Kifaa cha kupima shinikizo la gesi kinaitwaje?
What is device for measuring gas pressure called?
Kifaa kinaitwa manomita manometer. Aina rahisi: Bomba la glasi lenye umbo la U lililojaa kioevu kama maji au zebaki. Upande mmoja unaunganishwa na gesi, upande mwingine wazi kwa anga. Kama shinikizo la gesi > shinikizo la anga, kioevu hushuka upande wa gesi na kupanda upande wa anga. Tofauti ya urefu h x wiani wa kioevu x 10 inatoa shinikizo la gesi. Kanuni: Tofauti ya urefu = tofauti ya shinikizo.
Device is called manometer. Simple type: U-shaped glass tube filled with liquid like water or mercury. One side connects to gas, other side open to air. If gas pressure > atmospheric pressure, liquid falls on gas side and rises on air side. Height difference h x density of liquid x 10 gives gas pressure. Rule: Height difference = pressure difference.

Sheria ya Boyle inasema nini kuhusu gesi?
What does Boyle's Law say about gas?
Sheria ya Boyle: Kwa joto lisilobadilika constant temperature, ujazo wa kiasi fulani cha gesi hubadilika kinyume na shinikizo lake. Fomula: P1V1 = P2V2. Maana: Ukiongeza shinikizo mara 2, ujazo unapungua nusu. Ukipunguza shinikizo nusu, ujazo unapanda mara 2. Mfano: Sindano ya mpira ukiziba ncha na kubonyeza, hewa ndani inabanwa - shinikizo juu, ujazo mdogo. Kanuni: Shinikizo juu = ujazo chini, kama joto halibadiliki.
Boyle's Law: At constant temperature, volume of fixed mass of gas changes inversely with its pressure. Formula: P1V1 = P2V2. Meaning: If you double pressure, volume halves. If you halve pressure, volume doubles. Example: Syringe with tip blocked and you press, air inside is compressed - high pressure, small volume. Rule: High pressure = low volume, if temperature constant.

Sheria ya Charles inasema nini kuhusu gesi?
What does Charles Law say about gas?
Sheria ya Charles: Kwa shinikizo lisilobadilika constant pressure, ujazo wa kiasi fulani cha gesi hubadilika sawia na joto lake la Kelvin. Fomula: V1/T1 = V2/T2. Joto lazima liwe Kelvin K = °C + 273. Maana: Ukipasha joto gesi, inapanuka ujazo unapanda. Ukipooza gesi, inabanana ujazo unapungua. Mfano: Baluni ya hewa ukiweka kwenye maji ya moto inavimba, ukiweka kwenye maji ya barafu inabanana. Kanuni: Joto juu = ujazo juu, kama shinikizo halibadiliki.
Charles Law: At constant pressure, volume of fixed mass of gas changes directly with its Kelvin temperature. Formula: V1/T1 = V2/T2. Temperature must be in Kelvin K = °C + 273. Meaning: If you heat gas, it expands volume increases. If you cool gas, it contracts volume decreases. Example: Air balloon in hot water inflates, in ice water it shrinks. Rule: High temperature = high volume, if pressure constant.

Hesabu 1: Gesi ina ujazo 200cm³ kwa joto 27°C. Kama joto likapanda hadi 127°C na shinikizo lisibadili, pata ujazo mpya?
Calculation 1: Gas has volume 200cm³ at temperature 27°C. If temperature rises to 127°C and pressure does not change, find new volume?
Kwanza geuza °C kuwa Kelvin: T1 = 27 + 273 = 300K. T2 = 127 + 273 = 400K. Tumia V1/T1 = V2/T2. 200/300 = V2/400. V2 = 200 x 400 ÷ 300 = 266.67cm³. Kwa hiyo ujazo mpya ni 266.7cm³. NECTA hupenda swali la kusahau kugeuza Kelvin - hapo ndio wanapoteza alama.
First convert °C to Kelvin: T1 = 27 + 273 = 300K. T2 = 127 + 273 = 400K. Use V1/T1 = V2/T2. 200/300 = V2/400. V2 = 200 x 400 ÷ 300 = 266.67cm³. So new volume is 266.7cm³. NECTA likes question of forgetting to convert Kelvin - that is where students lose marks.

Maombi 3 ya shinikizo la anga na sheria za gesi?
3 applications of atmospheric pressure and gas laws?
Maombi 1: Sindano ya matibabu syringe - ukivuta bastola, unapunguza shinikizo ndani, shinikizo la anga linasukuma dawa kuingia. Maombi 2: Tairi la gari - hewa inabanwa ndani kwa shinikizo kubwa kwa Sheria ya Boyle. Ndio maana tairi linakuwa gumu. Maombi 3: Baluni ya anga na ndege - gesi ya heliamu ina wiani mdogo kuliko hewa, kwa Sheria ya Archimedes inainua baluni. Pia ndege inapopanda, cabin inabanwa ili abiria wasikose hewa. Kanuni: Sheria za gesi zinatawala anga na mashine.
Application 1: Medical syringe - when you pull plunger, you reduce pressure inside, atmospheric pressure pushes medicine in. Application 2: Car tyre - air is compressed inside at high pressure by Boyle's Law. That is why tyre becomes hard. Application 3: Hot air balloon and plane - helium gas has lower density than air, by Archimedes Principle it lifts balloon. Also when plane climbs, cabin is pressurized so passengers do not lack air. Rule: Gas laws control sky and machines.

Kwa nini masikio husikia "pop" tukipanda mlima mrefu?
Why do ears go "pop" when we climb high mountain?
Ukipanda mlima, shinikizo la anga nje ya mwili linapungua kwa sababu hewa juu ni nyepesi. Lakini shinikizo la hewa ndani ya sikio lako bado ni kubwa la chini. Tofauti hii ya shinikizo inasukuma ngoma ya sikio eardrum ndani au nje. Ngoma ikisukumwa unahisi maumivu au "pop". Ukimeza mate au kupiga miayo, unaruhusu hewa iingie au itoke ili shinikizo ndani na nje lisawazike. Kanuni: Mwili unapenda shinikizo lisawazike.
When you climb mountain, atmospheric pressure outside body reduces because air up is light. But air pressure inside your ear is still high from below. This pressure difference pushes eardrum in or out. When drum is pushed you feel pain or "pop". When you swallow or yawn, you allow air to enter or leave so pressure inside and outside equalizes. Rule: Body likes balanced pressure.

Summary of Topic 1: Atmospheric Pressure and Gases
Shinikizo la anga ni uzito wa hewa, hupimwa na baromita, wastani 760mmHg. Majaribio ya Torricelli yalionyesha kuwepo kwake. Shinikizo la anga linaweza kusukuma maji hadi 10.3m. Atmospheric pressure is weight of air, measured by barometer, average 760mmHg. Torricelli experiment showed its existence. Atmospheric pressure can push water up to 10.3m. Kazi work katika fizikia inamaanisha nini?
What does work mean in physics?
Katika fizikia kazi inafanyika wakati nguvu inaposukuma au kuvuta kitu na kitu kinasogea kwa mwelekeo wa nguvu ile. Kama hakuna mwendo, hakuna kazi hata kama umechoka. Fomula: Kazi W = Nguvu F x Umbali s. Kitengo cha SI ni Joule J. 1 Joule = 1 Newton x 1 mita. Mfano 1: Ukisukuma ukuta kwa nguvu zote lakini hausogei, kazi = 0J. Mfano 2: Ukibeba mkoba kg 5 umbali mita 2 juu, umefanya kazi. Kanuni: Nguvu + Mwendo = Kazi.
In physics work is done when force pushes or pulls object and object moves in direction of that force. If there is no movement, no work even if you are tired. Formula: Work W = Force F x Distance s. SI unit is Joule J. 1 Joule = 1 Newton x 1 meter. Example 1: If you push wall with all force but it does not move, work = 0J. Example 2: If you carry bag 5kg distance 2m up, you have done work. Rule: Force + Movement = Work.

Hesabu 2: Mwanafunzi anasukuma meza kwa nguvu 40N umbali mita 3. Tafuta kazi aliyofanya?
Calculation 2: Student pushes table with force 40N distance 3m. Find work done?
Tumia fomula W = F x s. Weka namba: F = 40N, s = 3m. W = 40 x 3 = 120 Joules. Kwa hiyo mwanafunzi amefanya kazi Joules 120. Kumbuka: Kama angeisukuma meza lakini isisogee, kazi ingekuwa 0J. NECTA hupenda mitego kama hii ya "kusukuma lakini hakuna mwendo".
Use formula W = F x s. Put numbers: F = 40N, s = 3m. W = 40 x 3 = 120 Joules. So student has done work 120 Joules. Remember: If he pushed table but it did not move, work would be 0J. NECTA likes traps like this of "pushing but no movement".

Nguvu power ni nini na tofauti yake na kazi?
What is power and its difference from work?
Nguvu power ni kiwango cha kufanya kazi kwa kila kitengo cha muda. Inaonyesha "haraka kiasi gani" kazi inafanyika. Fomula: Nguvu P = Kazi W ÷ Muda t. Kitengo cha SI ni Watt W. 1 Watt = 1 Joule kwa sekunde 1. Tofauti: Kazi inaonyesha kiasi cha jumla cha nishati iliyotumika. Nguvu inaonyesha haraka ya kutumia hiyo nishati. Mfano: Mtu A na B wote wabebe mifuko juu ghorofa. Wote wamefanya kazi sawa. Lakini A akapanda kwa dakika 1, B kwa dakika 5. Nguvu ya A ni kubwa mara 5. Kanuni: Nguvu = Kazi/ Muda.
Power is rate of doing work per unit time. It shows "how fast" work is done. Formula: Power P = Work W ÷ Time t. SI unit is Watt W. 1 Watt = 1 Joule per 1 second. Difference: Work shows total amount of energy used. Power shows speed of using that energy. Example: Person A and B both carry bags upstairs. Both did same work. But A climbed in 1 minute, B in 5 minutes. Power of A is 5 times bigger. Rule: Power = Work/Time.

Kanuni ya uhifadhi wa nishati inasema nini?
What does Law of Conservation of Energy state?
Kanuni inasema: Nishati haiwezi kutengenezwa wala kuharibiwa, inaweza tu kubadilika kutoka aina moja kwenda nyingine. Kiasi cha jumla cha nishati ulimwenguni ni sawa daima. Mifano: 1. Balbu - umeme unabadilika kuwa mwanga + joto. 2. Gari - nishati ya kemikali ya petroli inabadilika kuwa mwendo + joto + sauti. 3. Pendulum - nishati ya uwezo potential inapobadilika kuwa nishati ya mwendo kinetic na kurudi. Jumla yake inabaki sawa. Kanuni: Nishati = badilika tu, haiharibiki.
Law states: Energy cannot be created nor destroyed, it can only change from one form to another. Total amount of energy in universe is always same. Examples: 1. Bulb - electricity changes to light + heat. 2. Car - chemical energy of petrol changes to motion + heat + sound. 3. Pendulum - potential energy changes to kinetic energy and back. Total remains same. Rule: Energy = only changes, not destroyed.

Aina 4 kuu za nishati na mabadiliko yake?
4 main types of energy and their changes?
Aina 1: Nishati ya Uwezo Potential - nishati iliyohifadhiwa kwa sababu ya nafasi au urefu. Mfano: Jiwe juu ya paa, mkuki uliovutwa. Aina 2: Nishati ya Mwendo Kinetic - nishati ya kitu kinachosogea. Fomula: KE = ½mv². Aina 3: Nishati ya Joto Heat - nishati ya chembe kusogea haraka. Aina 4: Nishati ya Kemikali Chemical - iliyohifadhiwa kwenye chakula, betri, petroli. Mabadiliko: Chakula kemikali → mwendo kinetic unapokimbia. Betri kemikali → umeme → mwanga balbu. Kanuni: Kila kitu ni nishati inayobadilika.
Type 1: Potential Energy - energy stored due to position or height. Example: Stone on roof, pulled arrow. Type 2: Kinetic Energy - energy of moving object. Formula: KE = ½mv². Type 3: Heat Energy - energy of particles moving fast. Type 4: Chemical Energy - stored in food, battery, petrol. Changes: Food chemical → kinetic motion when you run. Battery chemical → electricity → light bulb. Rule: Everything is energy changing.

Mashine rahisi simple machine ni nini na faida yake?
What is simple machine and its advantage?
Mashine rahisi ni kifaa kinachofanya kazi iwe rahisi kwa kubadilisha ukubwa au mwelekeo wa nguvu. Haipunguzi kazi, lakini inakufanya utumie nguvu ndogo kwa umbali mrefu. Faida 3: 1. Kubadilisha mwelekeo wa nguvu - mfano kapi, unavuta chini mzigo unapanda juu. 2. Kupunguza nguvu inayohitajika - mfano koleo, lever. 3. Kuongeza kasi au umbali - mfano fimbo ya uvuvi. Aina kuu 6: Koleo lever, Kapi pulley, Mpangilio inclined plane, Kabari wedge, Skrubu screw, Gurudumu na ekseli wheel and axle. Kanuni: Mashine = kazi rahisi, si kazi ndogo.
Simple machine is device that makes work easier by changing size or direction of force. It does not reduce work, but makes you use small force over longer distance. 3 advantages: 1. Change direction of force - example pulley, you pull down load goes up. 2. Reduce force needed - example lever. 3. Increase speed or distance - example fishing rod. 6 main types: Lever, Pulley, Inclined plane, Wedge, Screw, Wheel and axle. Rule: Machine = easier work, not less work.

Faida ya kimitambo MA na ufanisi efficiency ni nini?
What is Mechanical Advantage MA and efficiency?
Faida ya kimitambo MA ni idadi inayoonyesha mara ngapi mashine inakuzidishia nguvu. Fomula 2: MA = Nguvu ya mzigo Load ÷ Nguvu ya juhudi Effort. Au MA = Umbali wa juhudi ÷ Umbali wa mzigo. Kama MA = 4, maana yake nguvu ndogo mara 4 inaweza kuinua mzigo. Ufanisi efficiency ni uwiano wa kazi yenye faida kwa kazi uliyoweka, kwa asilimia. Fomula: Ufanisi = Kazi yenye faida ÷ Kazi uliyoweka x 100%. Hakuna mashine yenye ufanisi 100% kwa sababu ya msuguano. Kanuni: MA kubwa = nguvu ndogo, lakini umbali mrefu.
Mechanical Advantage MA is number showing how many times machine multiplies force. 2 formulas: MA = Load ÷ Effort. Or MA = Distance of effort ÷ Distance of load. If MA = 4, means small force 4 times can lift load. Efficiency is ratio of useful work to work input, in percent. Formula: Efficiency = Useful work ÷ Work input x 100%. No machine has 100% efficiency because of friction. Rule: Big MA = small force, but long distance.

Hesabu 3: Koleo urefu mita 2 linatumika kuinua jiwe uzito 500N. Nguvu ya juhudi 125N. Tafuta MA na ufanisi kama mzigo umepanda 0.4m na juhudi imesogea 1.6m?
Calculation 3: Lever length 2m is used to lift stone weight 500N. Effort force 125N. Find MA and efficiency if load moved 0.4m and effort moved 1.6m?
MA = Load ÷ Effort = 500 ÷ 125 = 4. Au MA = Umbali juhudi ÷ Umbali mzigo = 1.6 ÷ 0.4 = 4. Kazi yenye faida = 500N x 0.4m = 200J. Kazi uliyoweka = 125N x 1.6m = 200J. Ufanisi = 200 ÷ 200 x 100% = 100%. Hii ni mashine bora isiyo na msuguano. Kwa kweli ufanisi huwa chini ya 100%. NECTA hupenda kulinganisha MA kwa njia 2.
MA = Load ÷ Effort = 500 ÷ 125 = 4. Or MA = Distance effort ÷ Distance load = 1.6 ÷ 0.4 = 4. Useful work = 500N x 0.4m = 200J. Work input = 125N x 1.6m = 200J. Efficiency = 200 ÷ 200 x 100% = 100%. This is ideal machine with no friction. In real life efficiency is less than 100%. NECTA likes comparing MA by 2 methods.

Tofauti kati ya koleo la aina ya 1, 2 na 3?
Difference between class 1, 2 and 3 lever?
Koleo la Aina ya 1: Fulcrum kati ya mzigo na juhudi. Mfano: Makasi, koleo la kubomoa misumari. Linaweza kubadilisha mwelekeo wa nguvu. MA inaweza kuwa >1, =1, au <1. Koleo la Aina ya 2: Mzigo kati ya fulcrum na juhudi. Mfano: Gari la mkokoteni wheelbarrow, kikwaju. MA daima >1 kwa sababu juhudi iko mbali zaidi. Koleo la Aina ya 3: Juhudi kati ya fulcrum na mzigo. Mfano: Fimbo ya uvuvi, mkono wa binadamu. MA daima <1 lakini linaongeza kasi na umbali. Kanuni: Aina 2 = nguvu, Aina 3 = kasi.
Class 1 Lever: Fulcrum between load and effort. Example: Scissors, claw hammer. Can change direction of force. MA can be >1, =1, or <1. Class 2 Lever: Load between fulcrum and effort. Example: Wheelbarrow, nutcracker. MA always >1 because effort is farther. Class 3 Lever: Effort between fulcrum and load. Example: Fishing rod, human arm. MA always <1 but it increases speed and distance. Rule: Class 2 = force, Class 3 = speed.

Work W = F x s, unit Joule. Power P = W ÷ t, unit Watt. Conservation law: Energy is not destroyed, only changes. Simple machines ease work by changing force or direction. MA = Load/Effort. Tafakari ya mwanga reflection ni nini na sheria zake?
What is reflection of light and its laws?
Tafakari ni kurudi kwa mwanga ukigonga uso wa kitu. Mwanga haupiti bali unarudi kwenye kati uleule. Sheria 2 za tafakari: 1. Pembe ya kuingia i = pembe ya kutafakari r. Hupimwa kutoka mstari wa kawaida normal. 2. Miale ya kuingia, miale ya kutafakari na mstari wa kawaida ziko kwenye ndege moja. Mfano: Unapojiona kwenye kioo, miale ya mwanga kutoka kwako inagonga kioo na kurudi machoni mwako. Ndio maana unaona picha yako. Kanuni: i = r daima.
Reflection is bouncing back of light when it hits surface of object. Light does not pass but returns in same medium. 2 laws of reflection: 1. Angle of incidence i = angle of reflection r. Measured from normal line. 2. Incident ray, reflected ray and normal are in same plane. Example: When you see yourself in mirror, light rays from you hit mirror and return to your eyes. That is why you see your image. Rule: i = r always.

Tufautishe tafakari ya kawaida na tafakari isiyo ya kawaida?
Differentiate regular and irregular reflection?
Tafakari ya kawaida regular reflection hutokea mwanga ukigonga uso laini na kung'aa kama kioo au chuma kilichopoliwa. Miale sambamba ikiingia inatoka sambamba. Inatengeneza picha nzuri wazi. Mfano: Kioo, maji tulivu, CD. Tafakari isiyo ya kawaida diffuse reflection hutokea mwanga ukigonga uso mbaya mbavu kama ukuta au karatasi. Miale sambamba ikiingia inatoka pande zote. Haileti picha wazi lakini ndio maana tunaweza kuona vitu vyote. Kanuni: Uso laini = picha, uso mbavu = tunaona vitu.
Regular reflection happens when light hits smooth shiny surface like mirror or polished metal. Parallel rays entering leave parallel. It forms clear image. Example: Mirror, still water, CD. Irregular reflection happens when light hits rough surface like wall or paper. Parallel rays entering scatter all directions. It does not give clear image but that is why we can see all objects. Rule: Smooth surface = image, rough surface = we see objects.

Picha ya kioo gorofa plane mirror ina sifa gani 6?
What are 6 properties of plane mirror image?
Picha ya kioo gorofa ina sifa 6: 1. Umbali wa kitu kutoka kioo = umbali wa picha kutoka kioo. 2. Ukubwa wa picha = ukubwa wa kitu. 3. Picha ni wima upright, haina kugeuzwa chini. 4. Picha ni ya nyuma laterally inverted - mkono wa kuume wa kitu unaonekana mkono wa kushoto kwenye picha. 5. Picha ni ya kuoneka virtual, haiwezi kukamatwa kwenye skrini. 6. Picha iko nyuma ya kioo. Mfano: Ukiinua mkono wa kuume, picha yako inaonekana kuinua mkono wa kushoto. Kanuni: Kioo gorofa = picha sawa na kitu.
Plane mirror image has 6 properties: 1. Distance of object from mirror = distance of image from mirror. 2. Size of image = size of object. 3. Image is upright, not inverted. 4. Image is laterally inverted - right hand of object appears left hand in image. 5. Image is virtual, cannot be caught on screen. 6. Image is behind mirror. Example: When you raise right hand, your image appears raising left hand. Rule: Plane mirror = image same as object.

Kioo kipapa concave mirror ni nini na sehemu zake?
What is concave mirror and its parts?
Kioo kipapa ni kioo ambacho uso unaotafakari umepinda ndani kama kijiko. Sehemu zake: 1. Kituo cha upindo C - kituo cha duara ambalo kioo ni sehemu yake. 2. Kilele cha kioo P - katikati ya uso wa kioo. 3. Mhimili mkuu principal axis - mstari unaopita C na P. 4. Urefu wa kitovu f - umbali kutoka P hadi F. 5. Kitovu F - sehemu ambayo miale sambamba na mhimili hukutania baada ya kutafakari. Uhusiano: f = r/2, yaani urefu wa kitovu ni nusu ya upindo. Kanuni: Kipapa = umbo la kijiko.
Concave mirror is mirror whose reflecting surface curves inwards like spoon. Its parts: 1. Center of curvature C - center of circle of which mirror is part. 2. Pole of mirror P - center of mirror surface. 3. Principal axis - line passing through C and P. 4. Focal length f - distance from P to F. 5. Focus F - point where rays parallel to axis meet after reflection. Relation: f = r/2, focal length is half radius of curvature. Rule: Concave = spoon shape.

Tumia sheria 3 za kuchora miale kwa kioo kipapa?
Use 3 rules for drawing rays in concave mirror?
Sheria 1: Miale sambamba na mhimili mkuu ikigonga kioo kipapa, baada ya kutafakari hupita kwenye kitovu F. Sheria 2: Miale inayopita kwenye kitovu F ikigonga kioo, baada ya kutafakari hutoka sambamba na mhimili mkuu. Sheria 3: Miale inayopita kwenye kituo cha upindo C ikigonga kioo, baada ya kutafakari hurudi kwenye njia ileile C. Kwa kuchora miale 2 kati ya hizi 3, mahali zinapokutania ndio mahali pa picha. Kanuni: Chora miale 2, pata picha.
Rule 1: Ray parallel to principal axis when hits concave mirror, after reflection passes through focus F. Rule 2: Ray passing through focus F when hits mirror, after reflection leaves parallel to principal axis. Rule 3: Ray passing through center of curvature C when hits mirror, after reflection returns along same path C. By drawing 2 of these 3 rays, where they meet is position of image. Rule: Draw 2 rays, get image.

Kioo dondoo convex mirror ni nini na picha yake?
What is convex mirror and its image?
Kioo dondoo ni kioo ambacho uso wa kutafakari unadondoka nje kama mgongo wa kijiko. Daima kileta picha ya kuoneka virtual, iliyonyooka upright na iliyopunguzwa diminished. Picha iko nyuma ya kioo kati ya nguzo na mhimili. Haijalishi kitu kiko wapi, picha daima itakuwa ndogo na karibu na kioo. Ndio maana magari hutumia kioo dondoo kama kioo cha pembeni side mirror - kinaandikwa "Objects are closer than they appear". Maana yake kinaonyesha eneo kubwa lakini vitu vinaonekana vidogo na mbali. Kanuni: Dondoo = picha ndogo daima.
Convex mirror is mirror whose reflecting surface bulges outward like back of spoon. It always gives virtual, upright and diminished image. Image is behind mirror between pole and focus. No matter where object is, image will always be small and near mirror. That is why cars use convex mirror as side mirror - it is written "Objects are closer than they appear". Means it shows wide area but objects look small and far. Rule: Convex = small image always.

Fomula ya kioo na ukuzaji magnification?
Mirror formula and magnification?
Kwa kioo chochote kipapa au dondoo, tunatumia fomula: 1/f = 1/u + 1/v. Ambapo f = umbali wa mhimili focal length, u = umbali wa kitu object distance, v = umbali wa picha image distance. Ishara: Umbali mbele ya kioo ni chanya, nyuma ya kioo ni hasi. Ukuzaji m = Ukubwa wa picha ÷ Ukubwa wa kitu = -v/u. Kama m ni chanya picha ni wima, kama ni hasi picha ni kugeuzwa chini. Mfano: Kitu kikiwa kwenye mhimili wa kioo kipapa, picha itatengenezwa kwenye mhimili pia na ukubwa sawa. Kanuni: Fomula moja inatawala vioo vyote.
For any concave or convex mirror, we use formula: 1/f = 1/u + 1/v. Where f = focal length, u = object distance, v = image distance. Sign: Distance in front of mirror is positive, behind mirror is negative. Magnification m = Size of image ÷ Size of object = -v/u. If m positive image is upright, if negative image is inverted. Example: When object is at center of concave mirror, image forms at center also and same size. Rule: One formula rules all mirrors.

Matumizi 4 ya kioo kipapa na dondoo?
4 uses of concave and convex mirror?
Kioo kipapa concave: 1. Taa za magari headlight - balbu huwekwa kwenye mhimili ili mwanga utoke kama miale sambamba. 2. Vioo vya kujipodoa vya kukuza - ukiweka uso karibu na mhimili unajiona umekuzwa. 3. Vioo vya madaktari wa meno - vinaonyesha meno ukiwa umekuzwa. 4. Vipokezi vya satelaiti satellite dishes. Kioo dondoo convex: 1. Kioo cha pembeni cha gari - kinaonyesha eneo kubwa barabarani. 2. Kioo cha pembe za maduka - mlinzi anaona wateja wote. 3. Kioo cha kona za barabara. Kanuni: Kipapa = kuzidisha mwanga, Dondoo = kupanua eneo.
Concave mirror: 1. Car headlight - bulb placed at focus so light comes out as parallel rays. 2. Shaving/makeup mirrors - when face is near focus you see enlarged. 3. Dentist mirrors - show teeth enlarged. 4. Satellite dishes. Convex mirror: 1. Car side mirror - shows wide area of road. 2. Corner mirrors in shops - guard sees all customers. 3. Road corner mirrors. Rule: Concave = concentrate light, Convex = expand area.

Kwa nini kioo cha gari cha pembeni kimeandikwa "Vitu viko karibu zaidi"?
Why is car side mirror written "Objects are closer"?
Kioo cha pembeni ni kioo dondoo convex mirror. Kioo dondoo huleta picha iliyopunguzwa diminished. Picha ndogo inafanya ubongo wako udhanie kitu kiko mbali zaidi kuliko ilivyo kweli. Kwa kweli gari lililo nyuma yako liko karibu zaidi. Hii ni hatari kwa dereva asiyejua. Ndio maana imeandikwa onyo ili dereva ajue kukadiria umbali kwa uangalifu. Faida: Kioo dondoo kinaonyesha eneo kubwa la barabara kwa kioo kidogo. Kanuni: Picha ndogo = udhani mbali.
Car side mirror is convex mirror. Convex mirror gives diminished image. Small image makes your brain think object is farther than it really is. In reality car behind you is closer. This is dangerous for driver who does not know. That is why warning is written so driver knows to judge distance carefully. Advantage: Convex mirror shows large area of road with small mirror. Rule: Small image = you think far.

Aina 3 kuu za mishipa ya damu na kazi zake?
3 main types of blood vessels and their functions?
Mishipa 3 kuu ni: 1. Arteria Artery - hupeleka damu kutoka moyoni kwenda sehemu zote za mwili. Ina ukuta mnono wenye misuli na nyuzinyuzi nyororo elastic ili isistahimili shinikizo kubwa la damu kutoka moyoni. Ina kipenyo kidogo na hakuna vali. Damu ndani yake ina oksijeni nyingi isipokuwa arteria ya mapafu. 2. Vena Vein - hurudisha damu kutoka sehemu zote za mwili kwenda moyoni. Ukuta ni mwembamba na nyororo kuliko arteria. Ina kipenyo kikubwa na ina vali kuzuia damu isirudi nyuma. Damu yake ina kaboni dioksidi nyingi isipokuwa vena ya mapafu. 3. Kapilari Capillary - ni mishipa nyembamba sana yenye ukuta wa seli moja tu. Hapa ndipo kubadilishana kwa gesi na virutubishi hufanyika kati ya damu na seli. Kanuni: Arteria = kutoka moyo, Vena = kwenda moyo, Kapilari = kubadilishana.
3 main types are: 1. Artery - carries blood from heart to all parts of body. Has thick wall with muscle and elastic fibers to withstand high pressure from heart. Has small diameter and no valves. Blood has lots of oxygen except pulmonary artery. 2. Vein - returns blood from all body parts to heart. Wall is thinner and less elastic than artery. Has large diameter and has valves to stop blood going back. Blood has lots of carbon dioxide except pulmonary vein. 3. Capillary - very thin vessels with wall of only one cell. Here gas and nutrient exchange happens between blood and cells. Rule: Artery = from heart, Vein = to heart, Capillary = exchange.

Tofauti 6 kati ya Arteria na Vena?
6 differences between Artery and Vein?
Tofauti 1: Mwelekeo - Arteria hupeleka damu kutoka moyo, Vena hurudisha damu kwenda moyo. 2: Ukuta - Arteria ukuta mnono na wenye misuli, Vena ukuta mwembamba na dhaifu. 3: Kipenyo - Arteria kipenyo kidogo, Vena kipenyo kikubwa. 4: Vali - Arteria haina vali, Vena ina vali kuzuia damu kurudi nyuma. 5: Shinikizo - Arteria damu ina shinikizo kubwa, Vena damu ina shinikizo dogo. 6: Oksijeni - Arteria nyingi zina damu yenye oksijeni nyingi isipokuwa ya mapafu, Vena nyingi zina damu ya CO2 nyingi isipokuwa ya mapafu. Kanuni: Tofauti kuu ni ukuta na mwelekeo.
6 differences: 1: Direction - Artery carries blood from heart, Vein returns blood to heart. 2: Wall - Artery wall thick with muscle, Vein wall thin and weak. 3: Diameter - Artery small diameter, Vein large diameter. 4: Valves - Artery has no valves, Vein has valves to stop backflow. 5: Pressure - Artery blood has high pressure, Vein blood has low pressure. 6: Oxygen - Most arteries have oxygenated blood except pulmonary, most veins have CO2 blood except pulmonary. Rule: Main difference is wall and direction.

Muundo wa moyo wa binadamu na vyumba 4?
Structure of human heart and 4 chambers?
Moyo wa binadamu una vyumba 4: 2 vya juu aitwayo Atria auricle na 2 vya chini aitwayo Ventrikali ventricles. Atria ya kulia hupokea damu chafu yenye CO2 kutoka mwili mzima kupitia vena cava. Ventrikali ya kulia huipumpa damu hii kwenda mapafu kupitia arteria ya mapafu. Atria ya kushoto hupokea damu safi yenye oksijeni kutoka mapafu kupitia vena ya mapafu. Ventrikali ya kushoto huipumpa damu hii kwenda mwili mzima kupitia aorta. Ukuta wa ventrikali ya kushoto ni mnono zaidi kwa sababu inafanya kazi kubwa ya kusukuma damu mwili mzima. Kanuni: Kulia = damu chafu, Kushoto = damu safi.
Human heart has 4 chambers: 2 top called Atria auricle and 2 bottom called Ventricles. Right atrium receives dirty CO2 blood from whole body through vena cava. Right ventricle pumps this blood to lungs through pulmonary artery. Left atrium receives clean oxygen blood from lungs through pulmonary vein. Left ventricle pumps this blood to whole body through aorta. Left ventricle wall is thicker because it does big job of pumping blood to whole body. Rule: Right = dirty blood, Left = clean blood.

Vali 4 za moyo na kazi yake?
4 valves of heart and their function?
Moyo una vali 4 kuzuia damu isirudi nyuma. 1. Vali ya Atrio-ventrikali ya kulia Tricuspid valve - kati ya atria ya kulia na ventrikali ya kulia. Ina "milango" 3. 2. Vali ya Mapafu Pulmonary valve - kati ya ventrikali ya kulia na arteria ya mapafu. 3. Vali ya Atrio-ventrikali ya kushoto Bicuspid/Mitral valve - kati ya atria ya kushoto na ventrikali ya kushoto. Ina "milango" 2. 4. Vali ya Aorta Aortic valve - kati ya ventrikali ya kushoto na aorta. Wakati ventrikali zinapopumzika vali hufunguka damu iingie. Wakati zinapopumpa vali hufunga "lub-dub" ili damu isirudi atria. Kanuni: Vali = mlango wa njia moja.
Heart has 4 valves to stop blood flowing back. 1. Right Atrio-ventricular Tricuspid valve - between right atrium and right ventricle. Has 3 "doors". 2. Pulmonary valve - between right ventricle and pulmonary artery. 3. Left Atrio-ventricular Bicuspid/Mitral valve - between left atrium and left ventricle. Has 2 "doors". 4. Aortic valve - between left ventricle and aorta. When ventricles relax valves open for blood to enter. When they pump valves close "lub-dub" so blood does not return to atria. Rule: Valve = one-way door.

Kwa nini upande wa kushoto wa moyo ni mnono zaidi?
Why is left side of heart thicker?
Upande wa kushoto hasa ventrikali ya kushoto una ukuta mnono zaidi wa misuli kuliko upande wa kulia. Sababu: Ventrikali ya kushoto ina kazi ngumu ya kusukuma damu kwa shinikizo kubwa kwenda mwili mzima wote - kichwa, mikono, miguu. Ni kama pampu kubwa. Lakini ventrikali ya kulia inasukuma damu tu kwenda mapafu ambayo yako karibu na moyo, hivyo inahitaji nguvu ndogo. Ndio maana misuli yake ni nyembamba. Kama ventrikali ya kushoto ikifaulu, mtu anapata shinikizo la damu. Kanuni: Kazi kubwa = misuli mnono.
Left side especially left ventricle has thicker muscle wall than right side. Reason: Left ventricle has hard job of pumping blood with high pressure to whole body - head, arms, legs. It is like big pump. But right ventricle only pumps blood to lungs which are near heart, so needs less force. That is why its muscle is thin. If left ventricle fails, person gets high blood pressure. Rule: Big job = thick muscle.

Eleza mzunguko wa damu wa mara mbili double circulation?
Explain double circulation of blood?
Binadamu ana mzunguko wa mara mbili. Mzunguko 1: Mzunguko wa Mapafu Pulmonary - Damu chafu yenye CO2 kutoka ventrikali ya kulia inasukumwa kupitia arteria ya mapafu kwenda mapafu. Mapafuni oksijeni inaingia CO2 inatoka. Damu safi yenye O2 inarudi atria ya kushoto kupitia vena ya mapafu. Mzunguko 2: Mzunguko wa Mfumo Systemic - Damu safi kutoka ventrikali ya kushoto inasukumwa kupitia aorta kwenda mwili mzima. Seli zinachukua O2 na kutoa CO2. Damu chafu inarudi atria ya kulia kupitia vena cava. Faida: Damu ya shinikizo kubwa haichanganyiki na damu ya shinikizo dogo. Kanuni: Kupitia moyo mara 2 = mzunguko 1 kamili.
Humans have double circulation. Cycle 1: Pulmonary - Dirty CO2 blood from right ventricle is pumped through pulmonary artery to lungs. In lungs oxygen enters CO2 leaves. Clean O2 blood returns to left atrium through pulmonary vein. Cycle 2: Systemic - Clean blood from left ventricle is pumped through aorta to whole body. Cells take O2 and give CO2. Dirty blood returns to right atrium through vena cava. Advantage: High pressure blood does not mix with low pressure blood. Rule: Through heart twice = 1 complete cycle.

Mapigo ya moyo heartbeat na presha ya damu blood pressure ni nini?
What is heartbeat and blood pressure?
Mapigo ya moyo ni sauti "lub-dub" inayosikika wakati vali za moyo zinapofunga. "Lub" ni kufunga vali kati ya atria na ventrikali, "dub" ni kufunga vali ya aorta na mapafu. Kwa kawaida mapigo ni 60-80 kwa dakika mtu mzima aliyepumzika. Presha ya damu ni nguvu ambayo damu inasukuma kuta za arteria. Ina vipimo 2: Systolic - shinikizo wakati ventrikali zinapopumpa, kawaida 120mmHg. Diastolic - shinikizo wakati ventrikali zinapopumzika, kawaida 80mmHg. Tunasema 120/80. Kanuni: Mapigo = vali kufunga, Presha = nguvu ya damu.
Heartbeat is "lub-dub" sound heard when heart valves close. "Lub" is closing of valves between atria and ventricles, "dub" is closing of aorta and pulmonary valves. Normally heartbeat is 60-80 per minute for adult at rest. Blood pressure is force blood pushes artery walls. It has 2 readings: Systolic - pressure when ventricles pump, normally 120mmHg. Diastolic - pressure when ventricles relax, normally 80mmHg. We say 120/80. Rule: Heartbeat = valves closing, Pressure = force of blood.

3 heart and blood vessel diseases and causes?

Ni hatari kwa sababu inaweza kupasua mishipa ya ubongo. Ugonjwa 2: Kiharusi Stroke - mtiriko wa damu kwenda ubongo ukikatika. Sababu: Mishipa iliyoziba au kupasuka. Ugonjwa 3: Mshtuko wa Moyo Heart Attack - mishipa ya moyo coronary arteries ikiziba, misuli ya moyo inakosa oksijeni na kufa. Sababu: Mafuta na cholesterol kujikusanya kwenye ukuta wa arteria. Kanuni: Kinga = kula mboga, fanya mazoezi.
3 diseases: 1. High Blood Pressure/Hypertension - blood pressure rises above 140/90. Causes: Too much salt, obesity, stress, no exercise. Dangerous because it can burst brain vessels. 2. Stroke - blood flow to brain is cut off. Cause: Blocked or burst vessels. 3. Heart Attack - coronary arteries blocked, heart muscle lacks oxygen and dies. Cause: Fat and cholesterol build up on artery wall. Rule: Prevention = eat vegetables, exercise.

Kwa nini miguu huvimba wakati mtu ana shida ya moyo?
Why do legs swell when person has heart problem?
Moyo ukishindwa kupumpa damu vizuri, damu inakwama kwenye vena za miguu. Shinikizo ndani ya kapilari linapanda na maji ya damu plasma yanatoka nje kuingia kwenye tishu za miguu. Hii inaitwa edema - kuvimba. Pia figo hazipati damu ya kutosha kuchuja maji, hivyo mwili unabaki na maji mengi. Ndio maana wagonjwa wa moyo miguu na tumbo huvimba. Tiba ni dawa za kutoa maji diuretics na kupunguza chumvi. Kanuni: Moyo dhaifu = maji yanakwama miguuni.
When heart fails to pump blood well, blood gets stuck in leg veins. Pressure inside capillaries rises and water plasma leaks out into leg tissues. This is called edema - swelling. Also kidneys do not get enough blood to filter water, so body keeps extra water. That is why heart patients' legs and stomach swell. Treatment is water pills diuretics and reducing salt. Rule: Weak heart = water gets stuck in legs.

Tofauti kati ya Pumaji Breathing na Pumaji la Seli Cellular Respiration?
Difference between Breathing and Cellular Respiration?
Pumaji breathing ni mchakato wa kimwili physical wa kuvuta hewa ndani inhalation na kutoa hewa nje exhalation. Hufanyika kwenye pua, kikohozi, mapafu. Haitoi nishati moja kwa moja. Pumaji la seli cellular respiration ni mchakato wa kikemia chemical unaofanyika ndani ya seli, hasa mitochondria. Hapa glukosi + oksijeni → kaboni dioksidi + maji + nishati ATP. Hii ndio hutoa nishati ya kufanya kazi mwili. Fomu: C6H12O6 + 6O2 → 6CO2 + 6H2O + Nishati. Kanuni: Pumaji = hewa kuingia/toka, Pumaji la seli = nishati kutengenezwa.
Breathing is physical process of taking air in inhalation and out exhalation. Happens in nose, trachea, lungs. Does not release energy directly. Cellular respiration is chemical process happening inside cells, especially mitochondria. Here glucose + oxygen → carbon dioxide + water + energy ATP. This is what releases energy for body work. Formula: C6H12O6 + 6O2 → 6CO2 + 6H2O + Energy. Rule: Breathing = air in/out, Cellular respiration = energy made.

Aina 2 za pumaji la seli na tofauti yake?
2 types of cellular respiration and difference?
Aina 1: Pumaji la hewa Aerobic respiration - hutumia oksijeni. Hutokea mitochondria. Hutoa nishati nyingi ATP 38. Mlingano: Glukosi + O2 → CO2 + H2O + Nishati nyingi. Ni bora kwa wanyama. Aina 2: Pumaji lisilo la hewa Anaerobic respiration - halitumi oksijeni. Hutokea cytoplasm. Hutoa nishati ndogo ATP 2 tu. Kwa wanyama: Glukosi → Asidi ya laktiki + Nishati ndogo. Ndio maana misuli inauma baada ya kukimbia sana. Kwa mimea/yeast: Glukosi → Pombe + CO2 + Nishati ndogo. Kanuni: Oksijeni ipo = nishati nyingi, Oksijeni haipo = nishati ndogo + uchafu.
2 types: 1. Aerobic respiration - uses oxygen. Happens in mitochondria. Releases lots of energy ATP 38. Equation: Glucose + O2 → CO2 + H2O + Lots of energy. Best for animals. 2. Anaerobic respiration - no oxygen used. Happens in cytoplasm. Releases small energy ATP 2 only. In animals: Glucose → Lactic acid + Small energy. That is why muscles ache after running hard. In plants/yeast: Glucose → Alcohol + CO2 + Small energy. Rule: Oxygen present = lots energy, No oxygen = little energy + waste.

Muundo wa mfumo wa pumaji wa binadamu?
Structure of human breathing system?
Mfumo una sehemu 6: 1. Pua na mdomo Nose & mouth - hewa inaingia, nywele na kamasi huchuja vumbi. 2. Kikohozi Trachea - bomba lenye pete za katili cartilage kuzuia lisianguke. 3. Mfereji wa hewa Bronchi - kikohozi hugawanyika kuwa 2, kwenda kila pafu. 4. Matawi ya mfereji Bronchioles - matawi madogo madogo. 5. Mfuko wa hewa Alveoli - mifuko midogo milioni 300 kwa kila pafu. Hapa kubadilishana kwa O2 na CO2 hufanyika. Ukuta wa alveoli ni seli moja tu na umezungukwa na kapilari. 6. Diaphragm - misuli kubwa chini ya mapafu. Kanuni: Alveoli = kiwanda cha kubadilishana gesi.
System has 6 parts: 1. Nose & mouth - air enters, hair and mucus filter dust. 2. Trachea - tube with cartilage rings to stop collapse. 3. Bronchi - trachea splits into 2, to each lung. 4. Bronchioles - small branches. 5. Alveoli - 300 million tiny sacs per lung. Here O2 and CO2 exchange happens. Alveoli wall is one cell thick and surrounded by capillaries. 6. Diaphragm - big muscle below lungs. Rule: Alveoli = gas exchange factory.

Sifa 5 za alveoli zilizofaa kubadilishana gesi?
5 features of alveoli suitable for gas exchange?
Alveoli zina sifa 5: 1. Ukuta mwembamba sana - seli moja tu, gesi zinapita haraka kwa usambazaji diffusion. 2. Unyevu wet - gesi huyeyuka ndani ya maji kabla ya kuingia damu. 3. Eneo kubwa sana - mifuko milioni 300 = eneo la uwanja wa mpira. Gesi nyingi zinabadilishwa. 4. Zimezungukwa na kapilari nyingi - damu husafirisha gesi haraka. 5. Tofauti kubwa ya mkusanyiko concentration gradient - O2 nyingi alveoli, kidogo damu, hivyo O2 inaingia. CO2 nyingi damu, kidogo alveoli, hivyo CO2 inatoka. Kanuni: Nyembamba + Eneo kubwa + Unyevu = kubadilishana bora.
Alveoli have 5 features: 1. Very thin wall - only one cell, gases pass fast by diffusion. 2. Moist - gas dissolves in water before entering blood. 3. Very large area - 300 million sacs = area of football field. Lots gas exchanged. 4. Surrounded by many capillaries - blood transports gas fast. 5. Big concentration gradient - lots O2 in alveoli, little in blood, so O2 enters. Lots CO2 in blood, little in alveoli, so CO2 leaves. Rule: Thin + Big area + Moist = good exchange.

Kazi ya diaphragm na mbavu wakati wa kuvuta hewa?
Function of diaphragm and ribs during inhalation?
Wakati wa kuvuta hewa inhalation: 1. Misuli ya diaphragm inakaza na kushuka chini kama bakuli. 2. Misuli ya kati ya mbavu intercostal muscles inavuta mbavu juu na nje. Matokeo: Kasha la kifua chest cavity linapanuka kwa pande zote. Shinikizo ndani ya mapafu linapungua kuliko shinikizo la anga nje. Hewa ya nje yenye shinikizo kubwa inasukuma kuingia mapafuni. Wakati wa kutoa hewa exhalation: Diaphragm inatulia na kupanda juu, mbavu zinashuka ndani. Kasha linabanana, shinikizo ndani linapanda, hewa inasukuma kutoka. Kanuni: Panuka = hewa ingia, Banana = hewa toka.
During inhalation: 1. Diaphragm muscle contracts and moves down like bowl. 2. Intercostal muscles pull ribs up and out. Result: Chest cavity expands all sides. Pressure inside lungs drops below atmospheric pressure outside. Outside air with high pressure pushes into lungs. During exhalation: Diaphragm relaxes and moves up, ribs move down and in. Chest shrinks, pressure inside rises, air is pushed out. Rule: Expand = air in, Shrink = air out.

Magonjwa 3 ya mfumo wa pumaji na sababu zake?
3 diseases of breathing system and causes?
Ugonjwa 1: Pumu Asthma - mirija ya hewa bronchioles inabanana na kujaa kamasi. Mgonjwa hupumua kwa shida na kupiga mizizo. Sababu: Mizio ya vumbi, moshi, hewa baridi. Ugonjwa 3: Saratani ya Mapafu Lung Cancer - seli za mapafu hukua kiholela. Sababu kubwa 90% ni sigara. Dalili: Kukohoa sana, kupumua kwa shida, maumivu ya kifua. Kanuni: Kuzuia = epuka moshi na vumbi.
3 diseases: 1. Asthma - bronchioles narrow and fill with mucus. Patient breathes with difficulty and wheezes. Causes: Dust, smoke, cold air allergies. 2. Tuberculosis TB - bacteria called Mycobacterium tuberculosis attacks lungs. Signs: Coughing blood, weight loss, night sweat. Spreads through saliva droplets. 3. Lung Cancer - lung cells grow abnormally. Main cause 90% is smoking. Signs: Persistent cough, breathing difficulty, chest pain. Rule: Prevention = avoid smoke and dust.

Sigara ina vitu gani 3 hatari na madhara yake?
What are 3 dangerous substances in cigarette and effects?
Kitu 1: Nikotini Nicotine - ni dawa ya kulevya drug. Inafanya moyo upige haraka na presha ya damu kupanda. Inasababisha kulevya. Kitu 2: Lami Tar - ni maji meusi ya nata. Inashikamana na nywele za cilia kwenye kikohozi na kuziharibu. Kamasi na vumbi hazitolewi, hivyo mapafu yanaharibika. Pia husababisha saratani. Kitu 3: Kaboni Monoksidi Carbon monoxide CO - gesi yenye sumu. Inashindana na oksijeni kushikamana na hemoglobin. Damu inabeba CO badala ya O2, hivyo seli zinakosa oksijeni. Kanuni: Sigara = sumu 3 ndani.
Cigarette has 3 dangerous substances: 1. Nicotine - it is addictive drug. Makes heart beat fast and blood pressure rise. Causes addiction. 2. Tar - is black sticky liquid. Sticks to cilia hairs in trachea and destroys them. Mucus and dust are not removed, so lungs get damaged. Also causes cancer. 3. Carbon monoxide CO - poisonous gas. Competes with oxygen to bind hemoglobin. Blood carries CO instead of O2, so cells lack oxygen. Rule: Smoking = 3 poisons inside.

Kwa nini kuvuta sigara husababisha kukohoa kila asubuhi?
Why does smoking cause coughing every morning?
Ndani ya kikohozi na mfereji wa hewa kuna nywele ndogo ndogo aitwayo cilia. Kazi yake ni kusukuma kamasi, vumbi na bakteria kutoka mapafuni kwenda kooni ili tumeze au tukohoe. Lami ya sigara inaharibu na kuzuia nywele hizi kufanya kazi. Kamasi na uchafu hukwama mapafuni usiku mzima. Asubuhi mwili unajaribu kuzitoa kwa nguvu - ndio unapata "kikohozi cha mvutaji sigara smoker's cough". Kwa muda mrefu mapafu yanajaa kamasi na kupata maambukizi. Kanuni: Cilia zikifa = kamasi hukwama.
Inside trachea and bronchi there are tiny hairs called cilia. Their job is to push mucus, dust and bacteria from lungs to throat so we swallow or cough. Tar in cigarette destroys and stops these hairs from working. Mucus and dirt get stuck in lungs all night. In morning body tries to remove them forcefully - that is "smoker's cough". Over time lungs fill with mucus and get infections. Rule: Dead cilia = mucus stuck.

Tofauti kati ya moshi wa moja kwa moja na usiokwisha?
Difference between mainstream and sidestream smoke?
Moshi wa moja kwa moja mainstream smoke ni moshi unaovutwa na kuvutwa ndani na mvutaji sigara mwenyewe. Moshi usiokwisha sidestream smoke ni moshi unaotoka ncha ya sigara inayowaka na moshi unaotolewa na mvutaji. Utafiti unaonyesha moshi usiokwisha una vitu vya sumu mara 2-3 zaidi kuliko wa moja kwa moja. Hii inaathiri watu wasiovuta lakini wako karibu - uvutaji sigara wa kupita passsive smoking. Watoto na wanawake wajawazito wako hatarini zaidi. Kanuni: Moshi wa jirani ni hatari zaidi.
Mainstream smoke is smoke inhaled by smoker himself. Sidestream smoke is smoke from burning tip of cigarette and smoke exhaled by smoker. Research shows sidestream has 2-3 times more poison than mainstream. This affects non-smokers nearby - passive smoking. Children and pregnant women are at higher risk. Rule: Neighbor's smoke is more dangerous.

Eleza mmomonyoko wa chakula kutoka kinywani hadi utumbo mdogo?
Explain digestion of food from mouth to small intestine?
Mchakato una hatua 4: 1. Kinywani Mouth - Meno yanaponda chakula mechanical digestion. Mate yana kimeng'enya amilasi salival amylase kinachovunja wanga starch → maltose sugar. Mate pia yanalainisha chakula. 2. Koromeo na Mirija ya chakula Esophagus - misuli inasukuma chakula kwenda tumboni kwa mwendo wa mawimbi peristalsis. Hakuna mmomonyoko hapa. 3. Tumbo Stomach - Asidi ya hidrokloriki HCl inaua bakteria na kuyeyusha protini. Kimeng'enya pepsini kinavunja protini → peptidi. Chakula kinakuwa kitu cha kimiminika aitwaye chymi. 4. Utumbo mdogo Small intestine - Hapa ndio mmomonyoko mkuu. Kongosho hutoa vimeng'enya: amilasi wanga→glukosi, lipasi mafuta→asidi ya mafuta + gliserol, tripsini protini→asidi ya amino. Ini hutoa nyongo bile kuyeyusha mafuta. Mwishowe virutubishi husagwa kabisa na kunyonya kwenye damu kupitia vinza villi. Kanuni: Kinywa = wanga, Tumbo = protini, Utumbo mdogo = vyote.
Process has 4 steps: 1. Mouth - Teeth grind food mechanical digestion. Saliva has enzyme amylase that breaks starch → maltose sugar. Saliva also softens food. 2. Throat and Esophagus - muscles push food to stomach by wave motion peristalsis. No digestion here. 3. Stomach - Hydrochloric acid HCl kills bacteria and dissolves protein. Enzyme pepsin breaks protein → peptides. Food becomes liquid called chyme. 4. Small intestine - This is main digestion. Pancreas gives enzymes: amylase starch→glucose, lipase fat→fatty acid + glycerol, trypsin protein→amino acid. Liver gives bile to emulsify fat. Finally nutrients are fully digested and absorbed into blood through villi. Rule: Mouth = carbs, Stomach = protein, Small intestine = all.

Vilinza villi ni nini na vimejipanga vipi kunyonya?
What are villi and how are they adapted for absorption?
Vilinza villi ni mikunjo midogo midogo kama vidole iliyoko kwenye ukuta wa ndani wa utumbo mdogo. Kila kilinza kimefunikwa na nywele ndogo zaidi aitwayo microvilli. Sifa zake 4: 1. Eneo kubwa sana - mikunjo + nywele huongeza eneo la kunyonya mara 600. 2. Ukuta mwembamba sana - seli moja tu, virutubishi vinapita haraka. 3. Zimezungukwa na kapilari nyingi za damu na mishipa ya limfu - husafirisha glukosi na asidi ya amino kwenda ini, asidi ya mafuta kwenda moyo. 4. Zina misuli ndogo - husaidia kusukuma virutubishi. Bila vilinza, kunyonya kungekua polepole sana. Kanuni: Villi = kiwanda cha kunyonya.
Villi are tiny finger-like folds on inner wall of small intestine. Each villus is covered with even tinier hairs called microvilli. 4 adaptations: 1. Very large area - folds + hairs increase absorption area 600 times. 2. Very thin wall - only one cell, nutrients pass fast. 3. Surrounded by many blood capillaries and lymph vessels - transport glucose and amino acids to liver, fatty acids to heart. 4. Have small muscles - help push nutrients. Without villi, absorption would be very slow. Rule: Villi = absorption factory.

Magonjwa 3 ya lishe na sababu zake?
3 nutrition diseases and causes?
Ugonjwa 1: Marasmus - utapiamlo wa nishati na protini kwa watoto. Sababu: Kunyonyesha kukoma mapema na chakula kibovu. Dalili: Mwili kufifia, ngozi kubanana na mfupa, anaonekana mzee. Ugonjwa 2: Kwashiorkor - upungufu wa protini lakini nishati ipo. Sababu: Mtoto kuachishwa maziwa na kupewa uji wa wanga tu. Dalili: Tumbo kuvimba kwa maji edema, miguu kuvimba, nywele kubadilika rangi, vidonda ngozi. Ugonjwa 3: Anemia - upungufu wa chuma. Sababu: Kula chakula kisicho na chuma au minyoo kunyonya damu. Dalili: Kuchoka, kichwa kuuma, ngozi kufa. Kanuni: Protini = kujenga, kukosa = kuvimba au kufifia.
3 nutrition diseases: 1. Marasmus - energy and protein malnutrition in children. Cause: Early stopping of breastfeeding and poor food. Signs: Body wasting, skin sticks to bone, looks old. 2. Kwashiorkor - protein deficiency but energy present. Cause: Child weaned and given only starchy porridge. Signs: Swollen stomach from water edema, swollen legs, hair color change, skin sores. 3. Anemia - iron deficiency. Cause: Eating no iron food or worms sucking blood. Signs: Tiredness, headache, pale skin. Rule: Protein = building, lack = swelling or wasting.

Kwa nini wanga na protini husagwa tofauti?
Why are carbs and proteins digested differently?
Kwa sababu muundo wao wa kemikali ni tofauti na mwili unahitaji vipande vidogo tofauti. Wanga kama wanga starch ni molekuli kubwa ya sukari nyingi. Mwili unahitaji glukosi molecule moja ili iweze kuingia damuni. Ndio maana kimeng'enya amilasi kinavunja wanga → maltose → glukosi. Protini ni minyororo mirefu ya asidi ya amino. Mwili unahitaji asidi ya amino moja ili ijenge misuli mpya. Ndio maana kimeng'enya pepsini na tripsini kinavunja protini → peptidi → asidi ya amino. Kila aina ya chakula ina "ufunguo" wake wa kimeng'enya. Kanuni: Kimeng'enya kimoja hakisagii chakula kingine.
Because their chemical structure is different and body needs different small pieces. Carbs like starch are big molecules of many sugars. Body needs glucose single molecules to enter blood. That is why enzyme amylase breaks starch → maltose → glucose. Protein is long chains of amino acids. Body needs single amino acids to build new muscles. That is why enzymes pepsin and trypsin break protein → peptides → amino acids. Each food type has its own enzyme "key". Rule: One enzyme cannot digest other food.

What is statistics and 3 uses in life?

How is frequency table made?

Wastani mean, mode, median ni nini na tofauti yake?
What is mean, mode, median and difference?
Wastani mean "average": Jumuisha data zote ÷ idadi ya data. Mfano: 2,4,6 → 2+4+6=12 ÷3 =4. Mode: Nambari iliyojirudia zaidi. Mfano: 2,4,4,5,6 → mode=4. Kama hakuna nambari inayorudia, hakuna mode. Median: Nambari ya kati baada ya kupanga data kwa mpangilio. Mfano: 2,4,5,6,7 → median=5. Kama idadi ni sawa: 2,4,5,6 → median=(4+5)÷2=4.5. Tofauti: Mean huathiriwa na nambari kubwa sana, mode haathiriwi, median iko kati. Kanuni: Mean=jumla÷idadi, Mode=rudia zaidi, Median=kati.
Mean "average": Add all data ÷ number of data. Example: 2,4,6 → 2+4+6=12 ÷3 =4. Mode: Number that repeats most. Example: 2,4,4,5,6 → mode=4. If no number repeats, no mode. Median: Middle number after arranging data in order. Example: 2,4,5,6,7 → median=5. If number is even: 2,4,5,6 → median=(4+5)÷2=4.5. Difference: Mean affected by very big number, mode not affected, median is middle. Rule: Mean=sum÷count, Mode=most repeat, Median=middle.
 
Grafu ya nguzo bar graph na picha picha graph zinatofautiana vipi?
How do bar graph and pictograph differ?
Grafu ya nguzo bar graph: Ina nguzo za mstatili. Urefu wa nguzo unaonyesha idadi/marudio. Nguzo zinaweza kuwa wima au mlalo. Inafaa kulinganisha vitu tofauti kama alama za masomo. Axe wima=idadi, Axe mlalo=vitu. Picha picha pictograph: Inatumia picha badala ya nguzo. Kila picha inaweza kuwakilisha idadi fulani. Mfano: 1 picha ya mti = miti 10. Inafaa kwa watoto au data rahisi. Hasara: Haiwezi kuonyesha nambari sahihi kama 23. Kanuni: Nguzo=usahihi, Picha=rahisi kuelewa.
Bar graph: Has rectangular bars. Height of bar shows quantity/frequency. Bars can be vertical or horizontal. Good for comparing different things like subject marks. Vertical axis=quantity, Horizontal axis=things. Pictograph: Uses pictures instead of bars. Each picture can represent certain number. Example: 1 tree picture = 10 trees. Good for children or simple data. Weakness: Cannot show exact numbers like 23. Rule: Bar=accuracy, Picture=easy to understand.
 
Rangi ya pie chart na jinsi ya kupata pembe?
What is pie chart and how to find angle?
Pie chart "chati ya pai" ni duara lilogawanywa vipande kama keki. Kila kipande kinaonyesha sehemu ya jumla. Jumla ya duara=360°. Ili kupata pembe ya kipande: Pembe = 360° × Sehemu ya kipande/Jumla. Mfano: Wanafunzi 60, 15 wanapenda Hisabati. Pembe ya Hisabati = 360° × 15/60 = 360° × 1/4 = 90°. Kwa hiyo unachora kipande cha 90° kwa protractor. Pie chart inafaa kuonyesha asilimia % ya jumla. Kanuni: Jumla=360°, Kipande=360°×sehemu/jumla.
Pie chart is circle divided into slices like cake. Each slice shows part of total. Total of circle=360°. To find angle of slice: Angle = 360° × Part of slice/Total. Example: 60 students, 15 like Mathematics. Angle of Maths = 360° × 15/60 = 360° × 1/4 = 90°. So you draw 90° slice with protractor. Pie chart is good for showing percentage % of total. Rule: Total=360°, Slice=360°×part/total.
